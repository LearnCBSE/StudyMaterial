Scholarship for 12th Passed Students 2024 | Get List of Scholarships for Class 12 Students

October 31, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Scholarship Scheme for 12th Passed Students 2024: There are many scholarships after 12th pass 2023 students introduced by private and government organization. Students who have qualified class 12th exam and wants to continue for further studies can apply for the scholarships as per the eligibility conditions mentioned by the individual organization. These scholarship rewards will help them to pursue higher studies so that they can look forward to a bright future. In this article, we will provide you the top 10 listed scholarships in 2024, including PM Narendra Modi Scholarship (Scholarship for 12th Passed Students by Narendra Modi). For more information keep reading the article of Scholarship after 12th pass 2023.

You can also find more Scholarship Articles for 12th passed, 10th passed Students and many more

List of Scholarships for Class 12th Students

Sooner, a student completes class 12th education, the stress of taking admission in the right course and institute begins to build up at a fast speed. While opting for the best course and college, it is very important, one must also know that it forms the basis of your career ahead. An alternative is to look for scholarships after 12th class that can overcome the mental load of paying a large amount of money to pursue higher education.

The available scholarships after class 12th can be of big help to students and particularly those who are trapped in financial constraints. Although taking up education loans is a choice available, scholarship opportunities after class 12th are more reliable and stable as students are not obliged to pay back the amount awarded in scholarships.

Before we go for further details of each scholarship, let us know the list of scholarships available for 12th qualified students.

  • NSF Scholarship
  • CCRT Scholarship
  • 26th NEST Scholarship 2023
  • Joseph Mundassery Scholarship
  • INSPIRE Scholarship
  • AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls
  • PM Narendra Modi Scholarship for 12th Pass Students
  • College Admission Scholarship Application (CASA)
  • Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarships
  • Foundation For Excellence (FFE) Scholarship
  • Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Ignite Awards
  • National Scholarship Test (NST)
  • Combined Counselling Board (CCB) Scholarship

Now, let us go through the scholarships individually in brief.

College Admission Scholarship Application (CASA)

College Admission Scholarship Admission (CASA) is a conventional scholarship for Class 12th pass students that help to maximize the advantages of quality higher education for the students. Students are invited to fill College Admission Scholarship Application (CASA) form which gives them the opportunity to avail themselves of scholarships based on merit. The CASA application process normally starts in the month of June and there is no application fee for this scholarship.

Eligibility for CASA

  • Students who have passed the Class 12th examination from CBSE, ICSE or any other recognized state board are eligible to apply.
  • Students who have finished graduation can also fill out the form for post-graduation courses.
  • The institute must be AICTE or UGC-recognized where the candidate goes for admission.

Note: CASA is mainly suggested for students who have made it through their class 12th and are looking for higher education.

Steps to Apply for CASA

  • Register through the official website with CASA.
  • Follow the guidance provided and fill the application form.
  • Upload the required documents.

After the submission of the application, the candidate will receive SMS and email alerts regarding their candidature and scholarships, suitable for them.

Reward: Several scholarship opportunities are available under this scheme, depending upon the credentials registered by the applicant in the form. In addition, the candidate is also eligible for INR 10,000 as per the university and provider’s responsibility.

Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarship

Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarship is a big opportunity for scholars belonging to rural regions and underprivileged classes. The scholarship is especially for class 12th pass students who are thinking to pursue diploma engineering courses like civil, electronics, telecommunication, mechanical and electronics.

The prime purpose of the Schindler Scholarship is to grant financial support to worthy students. Each year, about 75 students are granted this scholarship, worth Rs. 20,000.

Eligibility for Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarship

  • Students who have qualified for class 12th with science as a subject with a minimum of 65% aggregate.
  • The yearly family income of the student should not be more than Rs. 2 lahks.
  • Applicants looking forward to pursuing a diploma in engineering streams such as Electrical, Civil, Electronics, Mechanical, Electronics and Electronics, and Telecommunication can apply.
  • Applicants who are already persevering diploma courses can also apply for the scholarship.
  • The age of the applicant should not be more than 20 years.

Steps to Apply for Schindler Igniting Minds Scholarship

  • Visit the official website.
  • Login/Register using Facebook, Google Plus or Email account.
  • Once signed in, click on the link ‘Start Application’
  • Fill in all the details and upload the documents required.
  • Click on ‘Submit’

After the submission of the application, the candidates will receive SMS and email alerts regarding their candidature and scholarship approval.

INSPIRE Scholarship

INSPIRE Scholarship is also called Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE). This scholarship has been initiated by the Government of India to offer to students who fall in the top 1% rank in 12th board exams. Moreover, if a student attains the rank of up to 10,000 in NEET or JEE Advanced, then also they are eligible for it. The scholarship is awarded to 10,000 students and the amount of Rs. 80,000 is granted every year through this scholarship.

INSPIRE Scholarship has three components that are categorized into the following:

  1. Scheme for Early Attraction of Talent (SEAT): The principal aim of the SEAT is to pull young minds to study science. Under this scheme, the recipients are given an INSPIRE Award of Rs. 5,000 each. This scholarship is given to about one million science practitioners between the age group of 10 to 15 years (Class 6th to Class 10th). This scheme also provides summer camps for over 50,000 science scholars of Class 11th who can interchange ideas and experience innovations through the INSPIRE Internship.
  1. Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE): This points to attract creative talents to engage higher education in science-intensive programs. Along with the scholarship, elected candidates are also given in-depth supervision and mentorship. The plan covers 10,000 scholarships for the age group of 17 to 22 years to grant B.Sc and M.Sc courses. The scholarship is worth Rs. 80,000 per annum.
  1. Assured Opportunity for Research Careers (AORC): This scholarship is for applicants between the age group of 22 to 27 years with an intention to extend the R&D foundation. It is sub-divided into two components. The first one is the INSPIRE Fellowship which offers 1000 fellowships every year to carry out research in the field of applied sciences. The second component is the INSPIRE Faculty Scheme which is offered to 1000 post-doctoral researchers in the age group of 27 to 32 years through a contractual basis.

AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls

To encourage girl education in the country, AICTE offers a scholarship to female scholars who want to persevere in higher education in the technological field. AICTE awards Rs. 30,000 per year each to all the 4,000 girls.

Applications for the AICTE Pragati scholarships start in the month of August/September every year and AICTE does not conduct any entrance test for the scholarship.

Eligibility for AICTE Pragati Scholarships for Girls: Female students who are in their 1st year of the diploma or degree programs from any of the AICTE recognized colleges are suitable for the scholarship.

In case the applicant is chosen for the tuition fee reservation, the amount of Rs. 30,000/- may be reimbursed to the candidates through compensation for the purchase of books, vehicles, laptops, and desktops.

Foundation For Excellence (FFE) Scholarship

FEE Provides scholarships to students who have especially bright minds but have monetary constraints. It allows them to seek higher education in the science stream (engineers/doctors). FFE funds these students and helps them persevere in their careers in the field of their choice.

Eligibility for FFE

  • Applicants who want to pursue professional degree programs such as BE./B.Tech, Integrated 5-Year Dual-Degree M.Tech, and MBBS are eligible.
  • The marks of the applicants should not be less than 70% in the qualifying examination.
  • Candidate’s admissions to the programs should be on the grounds of merit to be eligible for the scholarship.

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Ignite Awards

Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam Ignite Awards is established by the National Innovation Foundation. This scholarship grants young minds with a stage to showcase their discoveries and thoughts. Students up to class 12th can participate in the same. All they need to do is to write their ideas in simple text and email it to the official website. The final date for enrollment is usually in the last week of August each year.

Ignite Awards is a yearly national competition intended to secure the creative and innovative spirit of children. While drawing the ideas, the National Innovation Foundation is seeming for original and creative technological approaches to solving everyday difficulties. The idea can be used to resolve many household problems or laborers or related to environmental pollution.

Eligibility for Ignite Awards

  • Students up to Class 12th can express their ideas for the Ignite Awards.
  • The maximum age of the applicant should be between 17-18 years. If you have freshly passed out of school, then also you are eligible to apply.

There is no special format in which the applicant is demanded to send the write-ups. However, while presenting the write-ups applicants should clearly specify their name, age, school name, address and contact number.

National Scholarship Test (NST)

National Scholarship Test (NST) is the most successful scholarship test administered by the Shiksha Education Trust. This exam is conducted every year both in online and offline mode to encourage meritorious students financially. Students could apply for the National Scholarship Test latest by 31st May every year.

Eligibility for NST: Students in classes 5th to 12th are eligible for NST.

National Scholarship test gives students a national and state-level scholarship. The top 3 national-level scholars are awarded Rs.75,000 each and the state scholars are awarded Rs.28,000 each.

PM Narendra Modi Scholarship for 12th Pass Students

Government Scholarship for 12th Passed Students 2023 is PM Narendra Modi Scholarship. PM Narendra Modi started a scholarship for 12th pass students who want admission in a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. This scheme grants financial support to the wards of ex-servicemen. It awards a total of 5,500 scholarships for programs such as B.Tech/B.E., B.A, BBA, BCA, M.A, M.Com, M.Sc, Ph.D., pharmacy and medical courses.

Eligibility for PM Narendra Modi Scholarship

  • Applicants should have qualified class 12th with a minimum of 75% to apply for the scholarship.
  • Candidates should between the age group of 18-25 years.
  • The candidate whose annual family income is below Rs. 6 lakhs are eligible to apply.

The last date to apply for PM Scholarship is normally in the 2nd week of November. Applicants who are elected for the scholarship will be granted Rs. 2,000 per month (boys) and Rs. 2,250 (girls). One can apply through the Kendriya Sainik Board website.

Combined Counselling Board (CCB) Scholarship

CCB Scholarship grants financially challenged background students with a stage to persevere higher education. CCB is linked with many leading educational institutes over the country where the students can get scholarship chances.

Eligibility for CCB Scholarship

  • Candidates of both classes 10th and 12th can apply for this scholarship.
  • Candidates should have passed the latest exam with a minimum aggregate of 33% to 50%.

Steps to Apply for CCB Scholarship

  • While applying online, applicants will have to choose their native state and continue with the corresponding application.
  • Read all the guidelines carefully and start filling out the application form and submit it.
  • Take a printout of the application receipt and counseling letter.

Reward: Selected students will be granted a scholarship of Rs.60,000 – Rs.70,000 for higher education.

Indian Oil Academics Scholarship

Indian Oil Corporation Limited grants scholarships to exemplary students from all over the country every year, under the Indian Oil Scholarships Scheme. The election of the applicants is done based on the merit list. Indian Oil Academic Scholarships are also given to first-year students of engineering and MBBS courses.

300 scholarships are awarded to engineering students while the left 200 scholarships are offered to MBBS students. The portion of the scheme also gives special support to girl students who wish to persevere in higher education along with physically challenged students and students from Jammu and Kashmir and other Northeast states.

Eligibility for Indian Oil Academics Scholarship

  • Candidates who have taken admitted in class 11th or (10+1) or the first year of the current academic year in ITI (2 years program) in schools/ institutions approved by the central/state government are eligible.
  • Candidates in the first year of full-time engineering degree courses and MBBS courses can also apply.
  • 11th class and those in the 1st year of 2 year ITI course are also eligible to apply.
