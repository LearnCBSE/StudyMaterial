E Medhabruti 2023 Scholarship Scheme | Dates, Eligibility, Application, Reward Amount

December 4, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Medhabruti Scholarship 2023-2024: e-Medhabruti, an ideal scholarship scheme of Government of Odisha, to encourage and motivate brilliant students of Odisha prosecuting higher studies in the field of General, Technical and Professional education, the Government of Odisha is awarding different scholarships every year through Higher Education Department on merit-cum-means basis. This scheme is also called an e-Medhabruti scheme.

Commenced to financially assist the exemplary and needy students, Medhabruti boosts and motivates students to seek higher education in the fields of general, technical and professional education. This scholarship can be availed by the students who are the citizens of Odisha and are studying in class 11 to postgraduation. A total of 24,500 scholarships are distributed every year under the e-Medhabruti scholarship scheme at the rate of up to INR 10,000. e-Medhabruti renewal last date 2023-24 will updated soon.

There are four types of scholarships that come under Medhabruti. They are Junior Merit, Senior Merit, PG Merit and Technical and Professional Merit scholarships which provide requirements of students seeking general courses, technical and professional courses. Find the list of scholarships here, that are distributed among qualified students who are seeking financial support under Medhabruti scholarship scheme.

The students picked for the scholarships are provided financial support to meet the cost of their higher education. e-Medhabruti Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

For each scholarship under the e-Medhabruti Scheme, there are eligibility criteria. Students who want to apply for this scholarship, should the requirements before applying for it.

Scholarship Name Eligibility
Junior Merit Scholarship The student must have passed 10+2 or HSC with 60% or more marks from a recognized institute.
Students must be pursuing regular +2 courses of 10+2 pattern.
Senior Merit Scholarship The student must have passed +2 of CHSE, Odisha/Class 12th or equivalent exam with 60%or more marks from a recognized institute.
The student must be studying a regular degree course/ +3 course from any recognized college/ institution/university.
PG Merit Scholarship The student must have passed a +3 degree or equivalent exam with 60% from a recognized institute or university.
The student must be studying a regular course in one of the disciplines mentioned above in any recognized college/ institution/ university.
Technical & Professional Merit Scholarships Students must have passed +2 of CHSE/CBSE/ICSE/ Equivalent exam or +3 degree/equivalent 3 year course  (only for MBA/MCA/MFC/MSW) with at least 60% marks from a recognised institute.
The student must be studying a regular technical & professional in one of the fields mentioned above in any college/ institution/ university recognized by the respective regulatory bodies.

Note: The parental income should not exceed Rs. 6 lakhs.

Students who are Not-eligible for e-Medhabruti Scholarship

  • Whose family income is more than Rs. 6 lakh per annum.
  • Who are pursuing higher studies through distance or open learning/correspondence
  • Who are availing scholarship from any other state
  • Who are pursuing diploma courses are not eligible for Technical/Professional Scholarship.
  • M.Phil., Ph.D., B.Ed., and M.Ed. students are not eligible for PG or Technical and Professional scholarships.
  • NRI students

e-Medhabruti Application Procedure

Candidates who are eligible and interested in this scheme can apply through the official website of the Medhabruti organization. To apply, follow the given steps below:

  • Visit the official website, medhabruti.org.

  • Click on the Application for the Medhabruti link on the right side of the screen and choose the type of scholarship according to your eligibility.
  • Fill your details as per asked in the form, such as name, address, contact number, signature, etc.
  • Fill your bank account details carefully where the scholarship amount will be credited.
  • Take the two print out of application form and sign one of them submit it to the principal of institution and keep the other copy with yourself for future reference.
  • The principal must send it further to the office of the Higher Education Department.

Documents Required for e-Medhabruti Scholarship

The candidates who like to apply for the scholarships require to keep with them the scanned copies of some important documents. These documents are uploaded online on the gateway while filling the Medhabruti scholarship application form. Find below the list of key documents to be uploaded:

  • A copy of the residence certificate issued by the accountable authority such as Tahasildar/Addl. Tahasildar
  • Citizen proof issued by the organization if the applicant’s parents are employees of Odisha State Govt.
  • Income certificate issued by the Revenue Officer, not below the rank of Tahasildar/Addl. Tahasildar
  • Marksheets and certificate of last qualifying exam as required by the different categories of scholarships
  • The first page of applicant’s bank account passbook containing the account number and IFSC

e-Medhabruti Scholarship Selection Procedure

The applicants applying for Medhabruti scholarships are selected based on their marks scored in the qualifying exam and family annual income. Given below are some factors that are examined while selecting a student for the distribution of the scholarship. The Medhabruti Selection list will be displayed online.

  • Candidates must meet the eligibility requirements as designated in the different divisions of merit.
  • The student record must be certified/authenticated online by the Principal of the respective college/institute.
  • The candidate’s marks in the last qualifying exam are also analyzed during the selection process.
  • In case of a tie in the marks under a particular type of scholarship, a student’s annual family income is considered, in which the student with lower family income is given preference.

e-Medhabruti Important Points to Remember

Candidates must adhere to certain rules and regulations while applying for the scholarship.

  • The scholarships are distributed among boys and girls in a 50:50 ratio.
  • Avoid multiple registrations/ applications for a special category of merit scholarships as it would drive to the dismissal of the scholarship application form.
  • Wrong data or documentation at any stage of authentication can head to the cancellation of the scholarship.
  • The candidates are suggested to keep a copy of the filled application form for future reference.
  • The applications which are not approved/authenticated by the institute are treated as ‘invalid’

FAQ’s on e-Medhabruti 2023-24

Question 1.
What is the e-Medhabruti Scheme?

Answer:
e-Medhabruti Scholarship is a scheme originated by the Department of Higher Education, Govt. of Odisha for the unique and meritorious students, to financially assist the meritorious and needy students and motivate them to pursue higher education in the fields of general, technical and professional education.

Question 2.
How to apply for e-Medhabruti?

Answer:

  • Go to the official website.
  • Click on the application link
  • Fill the required details
  • Upload the scanned copies
  • And submit your bank account details
  • Take the printout and put your signature
  • Get your parent’s and principal signature before posting it.

Question 3.
What are the eligibility criteria?

Answer:

  • Orissa resident
  • Family income not more than 6 lakh per annum.
  • 60% marks in the last qualifying exam.
  • Should apply for the scholarship only in the first year of joining

What is the reward amount granted?

Senior Merit Scholarship Rs.5000/-
