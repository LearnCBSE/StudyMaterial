Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Poland – Cons of Doing MBBS In Poland

September 13, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Poland : Poland is a European country in the heart of Europe. Studying medical education in Poland gives a complete European experience because of its rich historical and cultural legacy. Poland medical universities and colleges provide the quality of higher education with good infrastructure.

Continue reading this article to decide whether to go to Poland for MBBS or not. Know the disadvantages of studying MBBS in Poland here.

Limitations of Studying MBBS in Poland

Few people feel that MBBS in Poland is a beautiful opportunity for all medical students worldwide. Every year more than 1000 medical students want to study MBBS from Poland. However, there are disadvantages to doing MBBS from Poland. They are as listed here:

  • Medical students are not allowed to practice on real dead bodies. Instead, they have given dummies for practice.
  • Cultural differences can cause mental stress in students.
  • Students may feel homesick and it might lead to their concentration level dropping.
  • Students have to learn the local Polish language to communicate with the native patients.
  • The number of job options after completing MBBS is also less in Poland.
  • International students are not allowed to touch patients while doing an internship after the MBBS course.

Public Healthcare

The public healthcare system of Poland is in such a way that international students are not allowed to directly touch the patient while giving treatment. Waiting time for non-emergency treatments can be long and the language can also be challenging.

However, private healthcare options are available for those who need faster access to medical services. It is recommended for international students to have health insurance coverage to make sure that they have access to healthcare facilities and services.

Climate

The main disadvantage for Indian students is the climatic conditions of Poland. Poland has long cold winters and short summers which can be difficult for students to adjust to.

However, with proper preparation and the right clothing, students can adapt to the climate and reduce stress levels.

Language Barrier

The primary disadvantage for international students studying medical courses in Poland is they have to learn the Polish language. The universities made it compulsory to learn the Polish language which can be difficult for international students.

However, many universities teach subjects in the English language at the master’s level to make it easy for international students. Additionally, language support services and language courses are available on the campus for free.

Transportation

Poland’s transportation system, although fairly efficient, may pose some challenges for international students. Navigating the public transportation network, such as buses, trams, and trains, may require some time to get accustomed to.

Understanding routes, schedules, and ticketing systems can initially be confusing. However, with proper orientation and familiarization, students can effectively utilize the transportation system to explore the country and move around conveniently.

FAQs on Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Poland

  1. What are the disadvantages of living in Poland?

The disadvantages of living in Poland after MBBS are the cold climate, language barrier and lack of job opportunities for international students.

  1. Is MBBS in Poland good for Indian students?

Yes, MBBS in Poland is good for Indian students as they offer quality education at an affordable cost. The cost of living is also favourable for students all over the world.

  1. Is it worth doing MBBS in Poland?

Yes, it is worth doing MBBS in Poland because of its advantages.
