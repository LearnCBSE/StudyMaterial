JAC 10th Supplementary/Compartment Exam 2019 | Registration Form, Time Table, Admit Card, Result 2019

July 16, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

JAC 10th Supplementary Exam 2019: An Act to establish the Jharkhand Academic Council was enacted by the Jharkhand State Legislature and assented to by the Governor of the State on 26.12.2003, which was known as Jharkhand Academic Council Act. As per the official website, the Jharkhand Academic Council was established for holding and conducting examinations at the end of Secondary, Education, Secondary Education, Sanskrit Education, and Madrasa Education and for prescribing courses of studies for such examinations and for recommending for recognition of Secondary Educational Institutions, High Schools, Sanskrit Schools and Madrasas to the State Government and for carrying out such other or duties assigned to the council by the state government from time to time.

JAC Class 10 Compartment Exam 2019 Admit Card has been released on 17th June 2019. Students can collect it from their respective schools.

State Board Supplementary Exams

The JAC currently follows the CSBE board pattern, to improve education standards in the state in all branches of education viz. Secondary, Secondary Sanskrit (Madhyama) and Madarsa. They have been brought under the single leadership of Jharkhand Academic Council. The JAC has been constantly endeavoring to provide the equitable and unified attention at all the levels of education.

How To Register for JAC Class 10 Supplementary Exam 2019?

Students who have failed in a maximum of 3 subjects (including vocational subjects and electives) can apply for supplementary exam 2019. You need to apply in offline mode using your roll code and roll number. Please take note of the registration deadline on the official website before applying. Note that the last date will be extended for a few days and a late fee will be applicable then. After that, you won’t be allowed to take part in the exam, even if you have failed in a few subjects. Hence, don’t procrastinate and register online immediately. Follow these steps to register immediately:

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. Scroll down the homepage to spot the “Secondary compartmental exam 2019” section.
  3. Click on the “Secondary Compartmental 2019 Exam form” link.
  4. Fill in the relevant details carefully. Note that you can’t change the details later, hence be careful to avoid any errors.
  5. Paste your passport size photograph (matching the one appearing in the mark sheet) in the form.
  6. Attach a photocopy of the mark sheet with the form.
  7. Submit the filled in form to the institution for online data submission.

JAC Class 10 Supplementary Exam Application Fees

You need to also pay the following fees during the registration

  1. Application form fee – ₹60
  2. Supplementary exam fees – ₹125
  3. Local levy – ₹200
  4. Miscellaneous fees – ₹30
  5. Mark sheet fee – ₹80
  6. Late registration fee – ₹200

How To Download JAC 10th Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2019?

You need to make the best use of the time available to you by making a proper study plan, taking into account the mistakes you did in the main board exam and also your strengths and weaknesses. While doing this, you might want to check out the topper’s answer sheets available on the official website to see how they’ve answered the questions. Practice a lot of past year papers to understand the exam pattern better. Make sure that you do not waste this hard-earned opportunity. To plan effectively you need to download the date sheet of the exams as so:

  1. Visit the official website (link is given above).
  2. In the “Important news” header at the top, you will see the “Secondary Compartmental exam Time table 2019” link. Click on it.
  3. The date sheet will open as a pdf.
  4. Download and take a print out for convenience.

Please remember that the time table is not set in stone. It can change at any moment without any prior notification. Hence, it is advisable to stay updated with the notifications being posted on the main website until your exams are over.

JAC Class 10 Supplementary Results 2019

Once the exams are over, you can check the results from the official results website, the link is given above. Note that you can also reach the results website by clicking on the “results” tab at the top of the main website and then clicking on “Intermediate compartmental exam 2019 Result”. The results will be announced tentatively in the month of September. The process is very easy:

  1. Visit the official results website (link is given above).
  2. Click on “Intermediate Compartmental exam 2019 results”.
  3. Log in using your roll code and roll number to view your results.
  4. Download and take a print out of the same for future reference.
