NEET MBBS West Bengal Rank List 2019 | Check West Bengal Counselling Schedule Here

July 3, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

NEET MBBS West Bengal Rank List 2019: NEET MBBS West Bengal Rank List 2019 will be released by the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Kolkata by tentatively in the fourth week of June 2019. West Bengal Officials will release two rounds of NEET Counselling and hence two rank lists will be published. 85% of seats are filled by state quota on the basis of students NEET Score. The West Bengal MBBS Merit List 2019 will be created only for those aspirants who fulfill the West Bengal NEET eligibility criteria.

NEET MBBS West Bengal Rank List 2019 for 85% State Quota Seats

  • NEET MBBS West Bengal Rank List 2019 will be published by the Department of Health & Family Welfare, Kolkata.
  • Department of Health & Family Welfare, Kolkata will publish West Bengal NEET Merit List in two stages i.e Merit list for Round I and Round II.

NEET MBBS West Bengal Rank List – NEET Tie-Breaking Situation

If two or more persons will get the same scores in NEET 2019, West Bengal officials will use the following criteria to break the tie criteria:

  • Students with higher marks in Biology will be considered first.
  • If the tie still exists, candidates with higher marks in chemistry will be given preference.
  • If the tie still remains aspirants with fewer incorrect answers will be given consideration.
  • If the tie still remains even after following the above steps, students with older age will be considered.

NEET MBBS West Bengal Rank List – NEET Counselling

Kolkata’s Department of Wealth and Family Welfare releases the NEET MBBS West Bengal rank list in two phases. Any optimists who are included in the NEET MBBS West Bengal Merit List are eligible to attend the NEET counseling process. Candidates selected for the counseling process must fill the colleges and courses of their choice in online mode. Seat Allocation is based on the availability of seats, filling options and reservation criteria.
