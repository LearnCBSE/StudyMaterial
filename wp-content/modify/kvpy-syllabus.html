KVPY Syllabus 2019 | Get Complete KVPY Syllabus from Here

November 23, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

KVPY Syllabus 2019: Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana is a prestigious and national level scholarship that is running by the Indian Institute of Science. KVPY Registration will start from 8th July 2019. KVPY Examination will be on 3rd November 2019. For every student first step towards KVPY preparation is to go through KVPY Syllabus completely. There is no prescribed syllabus for the KVPY Aptitude Test.

The aptitude test aims to test the understanding and analytical ability of the student than the factual knowledge. However, students are tested for the syllabus up to class X/XII/1st year of  B.Sc./ B.S./ B.Stat./ B.Math./ Int. M.Sc./ M.S. as applicable. In this article, we are providing information about KVPY Syllabus 2019. KVPY Exam contains the KVPY Syllabus of Class 11 & 12. Read on to find more about the Syllabus of KVPY.

KVPY 2019 Syllabus Highlights

Exam Name KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana)
Conducting Authority Indian Institute of Science
Streams SA, SB, and SX
Exam Centres 54 Centers Pan India
Exam Date 3rd November 2019
Nationality Citizen of India
Official Website www.kvpy.iisc.ernet.in

KVPY Exam Syllabus – Negative Marking

In part I of both the streams 1 mark will be awarded for the correct answer and 0.25 marks will be deducted for the wrong answer. In Part II for every correct answer, 2 marks will be awarded and 0.50 marks will be deducted for the wrong answer.
