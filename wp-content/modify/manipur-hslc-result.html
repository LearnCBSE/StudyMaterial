Manipur Board Class 10th Results 2024 | BSEM Class 10th Result To be Released on Today (18th May 2024)

January 17, 2024 by LearnCBSE Online

Manipur Board Class 10th Result 2024: Manipur Board Class 10th Result 2024 will be announced by Board of Secondary Education, Manipur (BSEM), on its official website, manresults.nic.in. manipur Board Class 10th Result 2024 will be declared today i.e 18th May 2024 on its official website. The date for the release of results is expected by June 2024 and the announcement will be made by the Manipur Government. Students who have given the BSEM 12th class exams, results have been declared for them already and for class 10th, the results will be declared by the conducting body, very soon.

State Board Results

Candidates who want to check Manipur Board Class 10 Result 2024 have to enter their Roll Number as mentioned in their Admit Card. The exam for 10th class Manipur Board was conducted from 20th February till 15th March 2024. As soon as the results will be announced by the Manipur Board, we will be updating here regarding the same. By then, students are requested to have steadiness and wait for their results.

Students, who have appeared for the BSEM 10th exam in 2024, should calculate estimated marks as per they have written the exams. Mark yourself for all subject on the question papers, as per the exam, written by you. This will help you to match the marks obtained by you in the final results. If there is a much of difference between your estimated marks and the marks stated in the results, then you can apply for re-evaluation of the particular subject.

Here in this article, we are going to give complete details for the results of Manipur class 10th results for the exam conducted this year in 2024. Keep reading with us.

BSEM Class 10th Result 2024

The results for Manipur Class 10th 2024, is expected to be released by June 2024, as per estimation is done with respect to previous years results. Let us give here a general description of the Manipur Board HSLC(High School Leaving Certificate) exam 2024.

  • Examination Name – Manipur Board Class 10th Exam 2024
  • Board Name – Board of Secondary Education, Manipur (BSEM)
  • Conducting Body – Council of Higher Secondary Education, Manipur.
  • Examination Mode – Offline(Pen and Paper)
  • Exam Dates – 20th February till 15th March 2024
  • Exam Results – Expected by June 2024
  • Result Announcement Mode – Online
  • Result Website – http://manresults.nic.in/.

Steps to Check Manipur Class 10th Result 2024

Manipur State Board Class 10th Result 2024 will be declared on the official website and through online mode only. Later, students can collect their HSLC certificate from the respective schools. Below here are the steps to check the results through online mode, for Manipur 10th class exam conducted this year in 2024.

  • Firstly, the candidate has to visit the official result website, http://manresults.nic.in/.
  • Click on the link given for SSLC Exam 2024.
  • Enter the roll number and click on submit button.
  • The result of Manipur SSLC exam will be displayed on your screen.
  • Download the result for your reference and future use.
  • Students who have appeared for Class 12th exam can see their result, which was declared on 8th May 2024.

Details Mentioned on Manipur Board Class 10th Result 2024

Marksheet of Manipur HSLC consists of following Details

  • Roll Number
  • Name of the Student
  • Date of Birth
  • School Name and Code
  • Father’s and Mother’s name
  • Marks scored in each subject
  • Total Marks Secured
  • Percentage
  • Result status
  • Principal’s Signature

Manipur HSLC Compartment Exam 2024

If any student was not able to clear the HSLC examinations, keeping in mind the future of the lakhs of students, Manipur board provides the second chance in the form of the compartment examinations.

After the declaration of the result of the main examinations, the board of council, Manipur, conducts the compartment exams in the month of July. And by the end of August the BSEM announces the result of the compartment examinations. Previous Year in 2023, the Manipur board has declared the class 10th compartment result on August 23.

Some Important Facts For Manipur Class 10th Results

  • The results will be announced by the Board of Secondary Education, Manipur (BSEM).
  • Students can check the result through online mode, in the official website of Manipur board, http://manresults.nic.in/
  • Student’s Matriculation exam roll number, present in the admit card is required to check the results.
  • Students have to score the minimum marks in all the subjects, to pass in Manipur HSLC exam.
  • After the Manipur Board Class 10th results are declared, students can reach to their respective schools to get the secondary school certificate.

About Manipur Board

The Board of Secondary Education, Manipur, came into existence in 1972.

Functions Performed By The Manipur Board

  • Prescribing, preparing and printing relevant syllabus and textbooks for all the classes.
  • Fairly and Timely conducting board examinations for class HSLC and HSE along with evaluation and declaration of results.
  • To adequately provide to the Quality, Equity, Relevance and Access of school education.
  • Timely affiliation of schools for private and government both.
  • Appropriate preparation of Compartment exams.
  • Exploring means and ways of improving the application of the latest information and communication technology in the working of the Manipur Board.
