Let us Do (Page 2):

A. Look at the shops shown in the picture and fill in the blanks.

i. There are __________ necklaces of shells with __________ shells in each necklace.
Answer:
There are 10 necklaces of shells with 10 shells in each necklace.

ii. There are _________ groups of balloons with __________ balloons in each group and __________ loose balloons.
Answer:
There are 6 groups of balloons with 4 balloons in each group and 2 loose balloons.

iii. There are ______ bunches of bananas with _______ bananas in each bunch and loose bananas.
Answer:
There are 6 bunches of bananas with 12 bananas in each bunch and 4 loose bananas.

C. Surbhi has made __________ groups of shells with ______ number of shells in each group for her bracelets.

Surbhi has made 10 groups of shells with 5 number of shells in each group for her bracelets.

D. Her mother has made ___________ groups of shells with ___________ number of shells in each group for her necklaces.
Answer:
Her mother has made 5 groups of shells with 10 number of shells in each group for her necklaces.
