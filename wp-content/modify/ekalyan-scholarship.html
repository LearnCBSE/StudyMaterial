e-Kalyan Scholarship Eligibility

Before you go on to apply for a scholarship on the e-Kalyan portal, you require to make certain that you fulfill all the designated eligibility standards. The principal eligibility requirements relevant to all e-Kalyan scholarships comprise

  • The scholar must be a residence of Jharkhand state.
  • The candidate should belong to Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST) or Backward Class (BC) category.
  • The candidate must be studying at institutions recognized by the Government of Jharkhand/Competent government in Jharkhand.
  • The yearly income of the family should not surpass INR 2.5 Lakh (for SC/ST candidates) and INR 1.5 Lakh (for BC candidates).
  • The beneficiary of this scholarship program is not suitable for any other type of scholarship program.

e-Kalyan Application Procedure – How To Apply e Kalyan Scholarship?

The applicants can apply only through online means for the scholarship through e Kalyan student login. There are no offline means open to fill the application form. Follow the straight link given below to apply e Kalyan online form. The process to fill the Jharkhand E Kalyan or Post-matric scholarship 2023 has been given below:

Applicants are recommended to justly read the guidance for applying for the scholarship well before they apply for the scholarship.

  • Visit e kalyan website https://e.kalyan.cgg.gov.in/
  • Applicants have to register themselves on the Jharkhand E Kalyan portal by giving their basic information and a valid Phone number/email.
  • Applicants will receive the user ID and Password. After successfully enrolled, the candidate would have to fill the E Kalyan application and upload all the scanned copies of the required documents.
  • After uploading, the demanded documents in a designated format, candidates need to save the information provided in the application form.
  • Lastly, the candidate needs to get a hard copy (printout) of the same application. After taking out a printout, candidates have to put a signature in the documents.

Notes: Applicants are suggested to present only his/her bank account numbers (which is linked with AADHAR) while filling the application form.

Documents Required for e-Kalyan Scholarship

All the certificates listed below is necessary for students to have, it is needed while filling the E Kalyan Scholarship Form 2023. The scanned copies should be in JPG/JPEG format and the size of copies should be at least 150 KB. Below are some of the necessary documents required.

  • Photograph of candidate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Residential Certificate
  • Bonafide Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Mark sheet of the last examination
  • Application form with the signature of the candidate
  • Front Page of the bank passbook having Name of A/c Holder, Account Number, and IFSC Code

e-Kalyan Scholarship Application Status

Once the candidates have successfully presented their online scholarship application by the e-Kalyan portal, they can moreover follow the exact status of their application by following the below-given steps:

  • Go to the e-Kalyan portal.
  • Click on the option ‘Student Login’.
  • Log in to the user account using your particular login credentials.
  • Click on the ‘Application Status’ button
  • Now you can check the status of your application.

About e-Kalyan Portal

The e-Kalyan portal serves as a one-stop platform for the implementation and expenditure of different scholarship plans administered by the department. The portal records the scholarships open for SC, ST, and BC candidates who are learning at the post-matriculation level. It allows several characteristics for both the scholars and the institutions that comprise:

  • Easy approachability to the scholarship details
  • Disturbance-free online scholarship application form

FAQ’s on e-Kalyan Scholarship 2023

Question 1.
How can I apply online for scholarships?

Answer:
The Post Matric Scholarship Schemes for 2023-24 (Within the State/Outside the State) are online Schemes. In order to apply online, please visit the website through URL: ekalyan.cgg.gov.in

Question 2.
What is the last date for submitting applications online?

Answer:
For Post Matric Scholarship: In case of fresh and Renewal scholarships for the Year 2023-24 students can apply online From 27 October 2023 To 10 November 2023.

Question 3.
When Can Students apply for Renewals?

Answer:
The website is open for the Fresh & Renewal Scholarship applicants for the year 2023-24

Question 4.
What Documents to be uploaded on the Website?

Answer:
Only Scanned copies for all the Original Documents (Latest Documents only) should be uploaded. The List of Documents Includes

  • Student Photograph
  • Bonafide Certificate (with Fee structure )
  • The validity of Income Certificate should be from 1 April 2022
  • Residence Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Previous Year Mark Sheet
  • Bank Pass Book Scan
  • Scanned Copy of Application Form (Signature of Student and Parent/Guardian

Question 5.
Do I need to get my Aadhaar Card to apply for a Scholarship?

Answer:
Aadhaar Card Number is mandatory for the students in order to fill up the application form online. Students can also apply for Scholarships by entering their Aadhaar number.

Question 6.

How to check the status of my application?

Answer:
Students can check the status of the Online Application by logging in with his/her login details.
