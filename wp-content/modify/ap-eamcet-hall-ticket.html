AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020 | Download AP EAMCET Hall Ticket from Here

May 2, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020: Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada on the behalf of Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE) will issue the AP EAMCET hall ticket 2020. The hall ticket will be generated for those who have successfully filled and submitted the application form. Candidates will be able to download the admit card via online mode in the third week of April 2020 tentatively. In order to download the hall ticket of AP EAMCET, 2020 applicants have to visit the official website and enter the application number and date of birth.

AP EAMCET 2020 admit card is considered as an important document because it used to verify the identity of appearing students on the day of examination. The hall ticket consists of details related to the candidate and entrance examination. Apart from these details, it also contains some important instructions regarding the examination day.  Without the AP EAMCET 2020 Hall Ticket, an individual will not be allowed to enter the examination hall. Candidates must keep the hall ticket safe and secure it till all admission process gets complete.

AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020 – Important Dates

Candidates must check the official website on a regular basis to keep a track of all the updated dates. Generally, all the events and their corresponding dates are declared by the exam conducting body. The table below displays important dates related to AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020.

Events Dates (Tentative)
Releasing of Application Form Fourth week of February 2020
Last date to fill the Application Form Fourth week of March 2020
Issuing of Admit Card Third week of April
AP EAMCET 2020 Examination date Last week of April

AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020 Key Points

  • JNTU will issue the hall ticket via online mode
  • Applicants have to download the admit card from the official portal
  • The conducting body will not be responsible for sending the AP EAMCET Hall ticket 2020 via post or mail to any candidate
  • The hall ticket will be issued for only those students who have successfully participated in the application process.
  • Candidates have to produce the admit card in the examination hall
  • Entry without the hall ticket is not at all allowed.

AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020 Steps to Download

There are a number of steps which need to be followed by applicants to download the AP EAMCET Admit Card 2020. Although the steps are simple but skipping any of them will result in failure of downloading it. Therefore, while downloading the hall ticket students must pay attention. Applicants are advised to download the admit card as soon as it will be made available by the concerned authority. No candidate will be permitted entry without AP EAMCET hall ticket 2020.

  • Go to the official website of JNTU, Kakinada
  • Log in to the account by entering application number and date of birth
  • Then click on the “Submit” button.
  • The hall ticket of AP EAMCET 2020 will appear on the screen
  • Check each and every detail imprinted on the hall ticket carefully.
  • In case of no discrepancy, download the admit card.
  • Also, take a printout of the same and preserve it until all the admission formalities get over

AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020 Issues faced at the Time of Downloading

There are chances when candidates face some issues while downloading the online hall ticket of AP EAMCET 2020. Some of the issues that might be faced are listed below:

  • Server Error – If there is heavy traffic on the official website then it becomes little difficult to download the hall ticket.
  • Partially filled Application Form – Students must fill every single detail asked on the application form. If any detail left unfilled then it will create a problem at the time of generation of an admit card
  • Entering incorrect details – The application form must be filled with only correct and relevant details. If the provided details will not be correct then candidates will not be issued with the hall ticket.

AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020 Details Present in Hall Ticket

Aspirants will observe several details present in the AP EAMCET 2020 hall ticket. These details are regarding an appearing candidate and entrance examination which includes the following:

  • Name of the candidate
  • Father’s name
  • Date of birth
  • Roll number
  • Application number
  • Category
  • Photograph of the candidate
  • Signature of the candidate
  • Name of the exam
  • Date of the exam
  • Time of the exam
  • Address of the test center
  • Centre code
  • Guidelines related to the Examination day

NOTE – If applicants find any discrepancy in the AP EAMCET Hall Ticket 2020 then they must immediately inform the exam conducting authority and get it rectified before the date of an exam otherwise they will face problems in future.

Guidelines of AP EAMCET 2020 Examination Day

Candidates who will be appearing for the examination have to strictly follow each and every guideline imprinted on AP EAMCET 2020 hall ticket.  The important guidelines for the examination day are prescribed beneath:

  • All the students must report at the examination center at least 30 minutes prior to the commencement of the examination.
  • Candidates have to occupy only the allotted seats
  • Applicants will be allowed to leave the examination hall only after the conclusion of the entrance exam.
  • Entry inside the examination hall without the AP EAMCET Hall ticket 2020 is completely prohibited.
  • The students also have to carry any one of the id proofs among the following: Passport, Aadhaar card, Voter Id card, Driving License, etc along with the hall ticket.
  • Calculators, mobile phones, or any other electronic devices are not allowed inside the examination center.
  • Use of only blank ballpoint pen is allowed to marks the answers.
  • Candidates found using any unfair means or disturbing other applicants will not be allowed to take the exam.
