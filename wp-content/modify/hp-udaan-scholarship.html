HP Udaan Scholarship Program 2024 | Eligibility, Awards, Application Process, Apply Online

November 12, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

HP Udaan Scholarship 2023-2024: HP Udaan Scholarship is an initiative of the HP India. This scholarship offered to students who have completed their class 10th and 12th. This scholarship encourages backward class students who are class 10th or 12th pass outs for continuing their education. This scholarship offers financial support to students for pursuing their education in ITI/ Diploma/Degree courses. HP Udaan Scholarship 2024 Online registration will end in March 2024.

Students under these 3 categories can only apply for this scholarship. These categories are 10th class pass outs seeking support to complete their schooling, Undergraduates and Diploma/ ITI students. The application process for this scholarship will be started in February 2024 (Tentatively). So, all the eligible students can apply online for the HP Udaan Scholarship on its official website on or before March 2024. Read the complete article to get all the information about the HP Udaan Scholarship Registration. HP Udaan Scholarship 2024 Last Date is March 2024 (Tentatively). HP Udaan Scholarship 2024

HP Udaan Scholarship can be applied through online mode only. This scholarship offered by HP India through its CSR Program. This scholarship is a pan-India program. So, it is available for students belonging to all states of the country. This program will provide scholarships to over 1,300 students from the lower-income groups in the next 3 years.

This program also promoting girl child education by reserving a total of 50% of the seats for girls. A total of 750 scholars will be shortlisted under this program in its first year. These scholars will be awarded a scholarship amount of up to Rs. 30,000 per year. HP Udaan Scholarship Program provides 3 different types of scholarships as given below:

  • 1-Year Scholarships for Diploma/ ITI students
  • 2-Year Scholarships for students enrolled in 11th Class
  • 3-Year Scholarships for Undergraduate students

HP Udaan Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

Students must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to apply for the HP Udaan Scholarship:

One Year Scholarships for Diploma/ ITI students Eligibility

  • Students must have secured a minimum of at least 60% of marks in class 10th.
  • Students must have enrolled in a 1-Year Diploma/ ITI Course.
  • The student’s family income should not be more than 4 Lakhs per annum.

Two Year Scholarships for students enrolled in 11th Class Eligibility

  • Students must have secured a minimum of at least 60% of marks in class 10th.
  • Students must have enrolled in class 11th at a recognized school in India.
  • The student’s family income should not be more than 4 Lakhs per annum.

Three Year Scholarships for Undergraduate students Eligibility

  • Students must have secured a minimum of at least 60% of marks in class 12th.
  • Students must have enrolled in the first year of college at a recognized university in India.
  • The student’s family income should not be more than 4 Lakhs per annum.

HP Udaan Scholarship Selection Process

Refer to the following selection process for HP Udaan Scholarship Program 2024.

  • There will be an initial screening for document verification of the selected students.
  • After clearing the screening round, students have to attend a telephonic interview.
  • Then, the panel will make the final decision based on the scholarship applications and performance in interview rounds.
  • After this, the final scholar’s list will be disclosed by the panel.
  • Then, the HP Udaan Scholarship will be awarded to the selected scholars.

HP Udaan Scholarship Awards

HP Udaan Scholarship Program offers a total of 750 scholarships every year to deserving students. The following are the awards provided under the HP Udaan Scholarship Program.

HP Udaan Scholarship Program 2020 Apply Online

Refer to the below points to apply for the HP Udaan Scholarship Program.

  • Go to the official website of the HP Udaan Scholarship Program.
  • Click on the ‘Scholarship’ link that appeared on the homepage.
  • Students should log in using Facebook, Google Plus or Email account.
  • After login, click on the ‘Start Application’ to fill the application form.
  • HP Udaan Scholarship application form will be displayed on your screen.
  • Then, read all the given instructions and fill the correct relevant information in the HP Udaan Scholarship application form.
  • Upload all the supporting documents along with the HP Udaan Scholarship application form.
  • Click on ‘Submit’ to apply for the HP Udaan Scholarship Program.

Documents Required for HP Udaan Scholarship Program

Attach the below-listed supporting documents to apply for the HP Udaan Scholarship.

  • Class 10th Marksheet
  • Class 12th Marksheet (for 12th class pass out students or studying in graduation in the first year or 3-year Diploma)
  • Proof of Income (Income Certificate/Salary Slip/Ration Card/Affidavit/Food Security Card/BPL)
  • Passport size photograph
  • Admission Letter/Current Year Fee Receipt/Bonafide Certificate/College or School ID Card
  • Valid ID Proof Issued by Govt of India
  • Photocopy of the first page of bank passbook in the name of the student
