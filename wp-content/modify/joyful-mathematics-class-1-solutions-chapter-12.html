Let Us Do (Page 116)

A. One ball costs ₹ 20.
Riya gives _____ coins of ₹ 5 for the ball.
Sahil gives _____ a note of ₹ 10 for the ball.
Answer:
Riya gives 4 coins of ₹ 5 for the ball.
Sahil gives 2 notes of ₹ 10 for the ball.

B. Name the toys you can buy for ₹ 10.
Answer:
Flute

C. Name the toys you can buy for ₹ 20.
Answer:
1 ball or 2 flutes

D. If the cost of a car is ₹ 14 and Riya has ₹ 10 with her, how much more money does she need to buy the car?
Answer:
₹ 4

E. Riya and Sahil together have ₹ 30. Write the names of the toys that they can buy.
Answer:
1 ball and 1 flute or 2 elephants or 3 flutes.
