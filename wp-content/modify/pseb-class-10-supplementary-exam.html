How To Apply for Punjab Class 10 Supplementary Exam?

For those students who have passed but failed in any one subject in the Class 10th 2019 exam, have to apply for the Supplementary Exam through the official website.

  • Go to the official website, pseb.ac.in.
  • Click on the link given for Matric/Senior Secondary Supplementary Exam (Compartment/Additional), July-2019.
  • A new webpage will appear.
  • For new users, click to register yourself.
  • For existing users, enter the reference number and old roll number which has been sent to your email ID and mobile number at the time of registration.
  • After you have logged in, enter the necessary details.
  • Select the subject for which you want to give the compartment exam.
  • Click on the submit button to submit the application form.
  • Incomplete application forms could lead to rejection.
  • After the final submission of the online Compartment / Additional Form, Submit a hard copy of the Attested Application Form and copy of challan at PSEB District Sub Office/Head Office as per schedule issued by the Board.

About PSEB

The Punjab School Education Board (PSEB) came into presence through a legislative personification in November 1969 for the growth and promotion of school education in the state of Punjab. In 1987, the Vidhan Sabha improved the Board’s Act to allow it freedom. The scope of the Board’s functions is very wide and includes almost every phase/stage of school education. However, a brief description of functions, structure, and activities of the Board is mentioned as below:

Functions Of The PSEB Board

  • To make important arrangements for the stable conduct of public examination at the school level. At present, the Board escorts public examinations at Middle, Matriculation and Senior Secondary Level.
  • To guide the curriculum, courses of studies and textbooks for school education. The Board has a full-fledged educational wing, having subject experts in all the major subjects. The main duty of this wing is to develop and revise the syllabuses regularly and then provide textbooks accordingly.
  • To plan for the preparations, compilation, improvement, publication, printing, and sale of textbooks.
  • To make required arrangements for affiliations of schools to the Board.
  • To make attempts to bring about qualitative development in school education.
  • To act as a consultative body to the state Govt. regarding School Education.
