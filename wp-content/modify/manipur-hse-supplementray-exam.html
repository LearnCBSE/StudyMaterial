Manipur HSE Improvement Exam 2019 | Application Form,Time Table, Admit Card, Results

June 17, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

Manipur HSE Improvement Exam 2019: Manipur HSE Improvement Exam 2019 is conducted for students who have failed in 4th elective subject or exam of Class 12. No students want to get fail or repeat the same class, but if they get lose to pass in one subject in the board exam, they are given with an opportunity by Manipur board to re-attempt the exam or give the Improvement exam and get passed in the particular subject, improving their scores in the 12th or HSE certificate.

State Board Supplementary Exams

Manipur HSE Improvement Notification 2019 has been declared by Council of Higher Secondary Education Manipur(COHSEM) on 16th May. Students who have to appear for the same exam can check out the entire details related to it here. Students can download the Manipur HSE Improvement Time table or routine 2019 from the official website of the board- cohsem.nic.in.

Manipur Board HSE exam was conducted from 18th February to 20th March 2019 and results were declared on 8th May 2019. Therefore, if any student was not able to qualify the 4th elective subject exam or if any student who is not satisfied with their marks in any one subject can apply for the class 12th Improvement examinations. The Improvement exams for all the subjects will take place in the month of June 2019, subject wise. Students who have failed in a particular subject have to mention the subject name while applying for the Improvement exam. Go through the complete details related to class 12 improvement exam 2019, here in this article.

Manipur HSE Improvement Exam 2019 Overview

After the Manipur HSE Improvement exam notifications have been declared, the Board is providing students to appear in Improvement exams, who have failed in 4th elective subject or anyone subject for which they are not satisfied with the marks. This will be an opportunity for students to pass the exam with all subjects and after this, there will be no more chance given to them. Let us get an overview of this exam;

Name of the Exam Manipur HSE Improvement Exam 2019
Board`Name Council of Higher Secondary Examination Manipur(COHSEM)
Level State Level
Exam Date June 2019
Official Website cohsem.nic.in

How To Apply for Manipur Class 12th Improvement Routine 2019?

  • For those students who have passed but failed in the 4th elective subject in the Higher Secondary Education 2019 exam and placed under EIE (Eligible for Improvement Examination) category, have to apply for the Class 12th improvement exam through their respective schools, www.cohsemmanipur.nic.in/login
  • The respective Principals of the schools will be collecting the exams application form from the Council of Manipur Board from 18th May 2019.
  • The application form filled with important details along with necessary fees and documents has to submit to the Council Office of Manipur Board on or before 30th May 2019.
  • For students, who have passed in the HSE exam but are not satisfied with the marks, can appear for anyone subject in HSE improvement exam conducted by COHSEM.
  • Those student’s application form for improvement exam has to submit on or before 25th May 2019.
  • The Original statement of marks for HSE 2019 have to submit to the council along with the application form for the respective students.
  • Incomplete application forms could lead to rejection.

Manipur HSE Improvement Exam Time Table

To download the routine for HSE improvement exam 2019 conducted by Manipur Board, follow the below steps;

  • Go to the official website for the Improvement time table – cohsem.nic.in
  • Click on the Notice section available at the home page.
  • Students have to click on the Manipur HSE Improvement Time Table link.
  • Manipur HSE Improvement Time Table 2019 will appear on the new window.
  • Download the Time Table and keep it for future use.

Manipur HSE Improvement Exam Admit Card

  • The admit card for HSE Improvement exam 2019, conducted by Manipur Secondary Education Examination Board, will be available on 7th June 2019, by the Council.
  • The exams are going to be conducted in the month of June 2019.
  • The admit card will be collected by the respective School Principals from the council of Manipur Board from 7th June onwards.
  • Students who are appearing for HSE improvement exam can collect the admit card from their respective school from 8th June 2019.

Manipur HSE Improvement Exam Results

The results for HSE Improvement exam 2019, conducted by Manipur Secondary Education Examination Board, will be declared in July 2019, after the conduct of examinations. The exams are going to be conducted on June 2019. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, cohsem.nic.in.The level of the exam will be the State board level.

The official link to check the Improvement exam results is www.cohsem.nic.in. Students who want to check the results for HSE Improvement exam, follow the given steps

  • Go to the Manipur Board official website
  • Click on the Improvement Exam HSE Result 2019 link.
  • Students are required to enter roll number mentioned in admit card and click on Submit.
  • Manipur HSE Improvement Exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.
