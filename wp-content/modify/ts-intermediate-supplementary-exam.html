TS Intermediate Supplementary Result (Released) | Check TSBIE Telangana Supply Inter 1st & 2nd Year Results

July 23, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

TS Intermediate Supplementary Examination 2019: TS Intermediate Supplementary Exams is conducted for students who failed to pass in any subject of Inter 1st year or 2nd year. None of the students want to get fail or repeat the same class. If any of them fails in one or two subjects in the final exam, they are given with an opportunity by Telangana board to re-attempt the exam or give the supplementary exam and get passed in the particular subject and also improving the scores in the respective mark sheet.

Telangana State Intermediate Supplementary Exams 2019 has been declared on the official website, bie.telangana.gov.in. Students who have to appear for the supplementary exam can check out the entire time table provided below in this article.

The supplementary exams for all the subjects will take place from 25th May 2019, subject wise. And it will be finished by 1st of June, 2019. Students who have failed in a particular subject have to mention the subject name while applying for the exam. Go through the complete details related to Telangana Supplementary exam, here in this article.

TS Intermediate Supplementary Exams Time Table 2019

After the Telangana Intermediate results have been declared, the Board is providing students, who have failed in one or two subjects, to appear in supplementary exams and clear those subjects. This will be a chance for students to clear all subjects and score good marks in the final mark sheet.

Let have an overview of the supplementary exam going to conducted for TS Inter 1st year and 2nd year.

Examination Name Telangana Intermediate Public Advanced Supplementary Exam
Conducting Body Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education (TSBIE)
Mode of Examination Offline(Pen and Paper)
Starting on 25th May 2019
Ends on 1st June 2019
Official website bie.telangana.gov.in

Note: Exam Timing for Inter 1st Year supplementary exam is 9 AM to 12 PM.
