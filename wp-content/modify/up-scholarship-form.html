How To Apply for UP Scholarship?

To ease the application process, the Uttar Pradesh Government has launched its online scholarship and fee refund system. All qualifying students can apply for their respective scholarships through this online system only during the specified application period. Here is a short information on the application process that you need to pursue to apply for any of the scholarships provided by the UP Government. Know-How to fill scholarship form online UP

Step 1: New student enrollment

  • Use the UP Government Electronic Scholarship and Payment Reimbursement Program.
  • Click the “Student” section and select “New Registration.”
  • Choose the award you want to apply for.
  • Please fill in all mandatory details that are marked with a *.
  • Press “Send” to sign.
  • For future references, take the print out of the application sheet.

Step 2: Student Login

When enrolled, tap on the “Student” tab and pick the “New Login” choice for new applications and the “Renewal Login” option for renewal applications as per the scholarship you apply for.

  • Log in with your registration number, date of birth and password.
  • You will be led to a page containing essential guidelines for filling out the form.
  • Read all instructions carefully and press on the box at the end of the page and click “Proceed.”

Step 3: Fill in the scholarship application

  • Once you press the “Proceed” button, you will be led to the client dashboard.
  • Tap on the section called “Fill in the application form.”
  • Fill in the application form for a scholarship in more info.
  • Please click on apply.

Step 4: Upload all supporting documents

  • Once you click submit your application form, you will need to upload your photo and other supporting documents.

Step 5: Final submission online

  • Before proceeding to the final submission of the application form, applicants are advised to go through all carefully filled out information in order to avoid any kind of discrepancy thereafter. There is also no provision for changes to the information submitted by the applicant, once you have finally submitted the application form.

Step 6: Application of the form to the relevant educational institution

  • Following the final application of the scholarship application form, candidates are required to take the form and send it along with other supporting documents to their respective educational institutions.

FAQ’s On UP Scholarship Form 2020

Question 1.
How do you qualify for a scholarship in the UP?

Answer:
Applications for all UP scholarships can be made online through the ‘Saksham’ Scholarship and Payment Reimbursement Online Program.

Question 2.
If a UP student is studying outside the state, can he or she apply for any UP scholarship?

Answer:
Yes, there are scholarships that UP students can apply to study outside the state. When submitting, adhere to the eligibility criteria for each scholarship.

Question 3.
What’s the importance of UP Scholarship?

Answer:
The UP scholarship funds the training of students domiciled in Uttar Pradesh (UP). Pre-matric and post-matric scholarships are available for students in the SC/ST/OBC/General classes. The Minority Welfare Department, Backward Classes Welfare Department, Tribal Welfare Department, and Samaj Kalyan Vibhag are offered scholarships under this umbrella.

Question 4.
How can I register for scholarship in UP?

Answer:

  1. Visit the UP Government’s Scholarship and Fee Reimbursement Online System.
  2. Click on the “Student” section and select “New Registration”.
  3. Select the scholarship for which you want to apply.
  4. Fill in all mandatory details that are marked with an *.
  5. Click on “Submit” to register.

Question 5.
What is the last date to fill scholarship form?

Answer:
31st September 2019 is the last date to apply.
