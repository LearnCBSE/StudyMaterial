ICSE ISC Improvement Examination 2024 | Registration Form, Result, TimeTable

February 13, 2024 by LearnCBSE Online

ICSE ISC Improvement Examination 2024: The candidates who were unable to cross the qualifying marks in the ICSE Board exams will be given a second chance to improve their scores in the form of ISC Improvement Examination 2024. The Council for Indian School Certificate Examination (CISCE) will hold the improvement exam for the candidates who fail to clear the Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) examination. Read on to find more about the ICSE Improvement Exam

State Board Supplementary Exams

ICSE ISC Improvement Examination 2024 Schedule

The time table for the ISC Improvement Examination 2024 will be available soon on the official website www.cisce.org. The exam will most likely be held in July 2024. The candidates will be allowed to appear only for only one subject in the improvement exam.

ICSE ISC Improvement Examination 2024 Application Procedure

The application form will be available on the official website www.cisce.org. The application form has to be duly filled and the payment has to be made. The candidates are requested to visit the official website to keep up with the release of the application procedure.

ICSE ISC Improvement Examination 2024 Admit Cards

The admit cards will be released on the website soon after the application form is filled. The admit card has to be compulsorily carried to the exam hall to be allowed to sit for the exam. No exceptions will be made under any circumstances.

ICSE ISC Improvement Examination 2024 Results

The  Council for Indian School Certificate Examination (CISCE) will declare the results of the improvement exam soon after the end of the examinations. The dates will be released very soon.

Keep visiting the official website for the notification release. We wish you good luck for the examination!
