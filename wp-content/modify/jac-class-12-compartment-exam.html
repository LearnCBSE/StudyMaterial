JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam 2019 | Time Table (Released), Result, Admit Card

September 3, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

JAC Class 12 Compartmental Exam 2019: The Jharkhand Academic Council will give a second chance to the candidates who failed to qualify the JAC Class 12 examination, in the form of the JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam. The council will hold the exam in the month of July 2019. The exam will be held only for those candidates who failed to secure qualifying marks in the main exams. JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam 2019 Admit Card has been released on 17th June 2019. Students can collect it from their respective schools.

JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam 2019 Application Procedure

The application form will be available on the official website jac.jharkhand.gov.in. The following steps are necessary for the form filling

  1. Go to the official website jac.jharkhand.gov.in
  2. Visit the “Intermediate Compartment Session” tab
  3. The Application form will be available in the PDF format. The candidates should print the form and fill it properly.

Download JAC Class 12 Supplementary Exam Application Form 2019

JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam 2019 Time Table

The time table for JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam  2019 will be published on the official website jac.jharkhand.gov.in. Keep visiting the official website for the latest notifications

  1. Go to the official website jac.jharkhand.gov.in
  2. Click on the “Compartment Time Table” link under the “About Us” section of the website
  3. The time table for JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam 2019 will be available in PDF format for download.

JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam Admit Card 2019

The admit cards will be published on the official website  jac.jharkhand.gov.in.

  1. Go to the “Online Admit Card” tab at the bottom of the page.
  2. Click on the “Compartment Admit Card” link
  3. Enter the roll number and submit
  4. The admit card will be generated

The candidates won’t be permitted to enter the exam hall without the hard copy of the admit card with them.

JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam Results 2019

The results of the JAC Class 12 Compartment Exam 2019 will be declared on the official website jacresults.com. The students will have to enter the Roll Code and Roll Number on the page to generate the results.
