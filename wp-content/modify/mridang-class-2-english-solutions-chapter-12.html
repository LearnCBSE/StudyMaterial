Mridang Class 2 Chapter 12 Little Drops of Water Summary in English

Children! today we will learn about happiness, love, warmth that how little little drops of all these gesture can change one’s life like small or little drops of water makes a big ocean. A sweet message from someone can make his day. In this poem, we will sing and learn about it.

Mridang Class 2 Chapter 12 Little Drops of Water Hindi Translation

Little drops of water,
Little grains of sand,
Make the mighty ocean
And the pleasant land.

Little words of love,
Little acts of kindness,
Little deeds of warmth
Spread smiles and happiness.
