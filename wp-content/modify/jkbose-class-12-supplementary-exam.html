JKBOSE Class 12 Supplementary Exam 2019 – Time Table, Admit Card, Results

June 21, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

JKBOSE Class 12 Supplementary Examination 2019: JKBOSE Class 12 Supplementary Exam is conducted for students who fail to pass in one or two exams. No students want to repeat the same class, but if they get fail in one or two subjects in the final exam, they are provided with an opportunity by Jammu and Kashmir board to re-attempt the exam or give the supplementary exam and get passed in the specific subject, improving their scores in the 10th or 12 certificate.

State Board Supplementary Exams

Jammu and Kashmir 12 Supplementary Time Table 2019 has been declared by Jammu and Kashmir Secondary Education Examination Board (JKBOSE). A direct link to download Jammu and Kashmir 10th Supplementary exam Time Table 2019 is provided here. Though, the time table is available on the official website, www.jkbose.jk.gov.in.

Jammu and Kashmir Board 12 exam results were declared on 10th May 2019. Therefore, if any student was not able to qualify exams can apply for the class 12th supplementary examinations. The supplementary exams timetable has been given on the official website. Students who have failed in a particular subject have to mention the subject name while applying for the exam. Go through the complete details related to class 12 supplementary time table 2019, here in this article.

JKBOSE Class 12 Supplementary Exam Time Table 2019

After the Jammu and Kashmir 12 exam results have been declared, the Board is providing students, who have not cleared the exam, to appear for 12th supplementary exams and clear those subjects. This will be an opportunity for students to pass the exam with all subjects and after this, there will be no more chance given to them.

Time table for 12 supplementary exams of JK Board, 2019, for all the subjects, is provided on the official website and conducted by Jammu and Kashmir board. Students are requested to go and check on the JKBOSE website.

Direct Link to Check JKBOSE Class 12 Supplementary Exam Time Tables

Steps To Download JKBOSE Class 12 Supplementary Time Table

  • Step – 1: Visit the Official Website of Jammu and Kashmir i.e. jkbose.jk.gov.in
  • Step – 2: At the end of the home page you will find Student’s Corner, in that click on the Date sheet

  • Step – 3: You will be redirected to a new page
    • Students from Jammu Division can select Jammu Division
    • Students from Kashmir Division can select Kashmir Division
  • Step – 4: Now Click on JKBOSE 10th Supplementary Date Sheet 2019
  • Step – 5: Exam Date Sheet will appear in PDF format and students can download it for future reference.

Jammu and Kashmir 12 Supplementary Exam Admit Card

The admit card for 12 supplementary exams 2019, conducted by Jammu and Kashmir Secondary Education Examination Board, will be released on May 2019, before the start of examinations. The exams will be conducted in June 2019 before the start of the new academic session. The admit card for all the students will be released online on the official website, jkbose.jk.gov.in.

Students who have applied for supplementary exam for class 12th, have to download the admit card from the official website itself. No other mode will be available to get the admit card. Follow the below steps to download it.

  • Go to the official website for 12 Supplementary Admit Card- jkbose.jk.gov.in
  • Check the link given for admit card in the notifications and click it.
  • Enter your Registration Number and click on Submit.
  • Admit Card will appear on the new screen and students have to check for their mistakes. If any discrepancies exist, they must report to the school authorities immediately.
  • Take a printout of the admit card and keep it safe with you to present in the exam hall.

Jammu and Kashmir 12 Supplementary Exam Results

The results for 12 supplementary exams 2019, conducted by Jammu and Kashmir Secondary Education Examination Board, will be declared in July 2019 tentatively, after the conduct of examinations. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, jkbose.jk.gov.in.The level of the exam will be Jammu and Kashmir State board level.

The official link to check the supplementary exam results is www.karresult.nic.in. Students who want to check the results for 12 supplementary exams, follow the given steps

  • Go to the Jammu and Kashmir 12 official results website- http://jkbose.jk.gov.in/
  • Click on the Supplementary 12 Result 2019 link.
  • Students are required to enter Registration Number and click on Submit.
  • Jammu and Kashmir 12 Supplementary Exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.
