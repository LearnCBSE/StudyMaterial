How To Check ICSE 10th Result 2024?

Follow the steps as listed below to check your ICSE 10th Result 2024:

Step 1: Visit the official website of ICSE: cisce.org

Step 2: Click on Course and select ICSE from the drop-down menu.

Step 3: Enter your UID as mentioned in ICSE 10th Admit Card .

Step 4: Enter your INDEX No in the empty box.

Step 5: Now enter the Captcha as displayed in the screen.

Step 6: Click on the Show Result Button.

Step 7: Your ICSE 10th Results will be displayed on the screen.

Step 8: Click on Print Result to print your ICSE 2024 Class 10 Results.

ICSE 10th Result 2024 – Provision For Re-evaluation

Candidates who are not satisfied with their ICSE Class 10 Results 2024 have a provision to apply for Re-evaluation of their answer booklets. The link for ICSE Re-evaluation will be activated once the ICSE board exam results are announced. If the officials come across any changes in the answer sheet then they would make the necessary changes and update in the final Answer Sheet.

ICSE 10th Result – ICSE Compartment Exam

Candidates must obtain a minimum of 35 marks in each subject. If a candidate fails to secure the overall passing percentage (35%) then the candidate is considered to fail. If the overall percentage crosses 35% for all subjects and if a candidate gets less than 35 in one subject, i.e. below the passing percentage, that subject will be considered compartment and candidate should take that exam again. The compartmental examination of ICSE Class 10 will afford the candidates with a second opportunity to improve the scores in their examination without wasting an entire year.
