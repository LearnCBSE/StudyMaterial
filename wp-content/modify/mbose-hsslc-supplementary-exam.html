MBOSE 12th/HSSLC Supplementary Exam 2019 | Registration, Time Table, Admit Card, Result

July 1, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

MBOSE 12th/HSSLC Supplementary Exam 2019: Meghalaya Board of School Education (MBOSE) first started in 1973, Headquarters at Tura, to conduct Examination, frame syllabus and evaluation & certification of SSLC Examination for the first time in 1974. Higher Secondary Section was then called as P.U. (Pre-University) and its examination process was handled by NEHU-North East Hills University, head office at Shillong. But after 1996, when the University (NEHU) discontinued the Pre-University Course, it was taken over by the Board, since then all the financial matters, relating with SSLC and HSSLC branch were being dealt by the Board itself. Due to the increasing number of students per year, the workload is also increased. After 1996, the Board is handling all the academic matters related to framing of syllabus for all the classes, including SSLC and HSSLC.

State Board Supplementary Exams

MBOSE is committed to quality education for the holistic development of all students, culminating in attaining excellence in their lives, so that they will be a blessing to the society and the world at large.

Many students fail to do well in the main board exams due to stress and other factors. If you’re one of them, we’ve got good news for you: the MBOSE is conducting the supplementary exam for 12th standard students soon. Read on to find out more.

MBOSE HSSLC/12th Supplementary Routine Highlights 2019

Conducting Authority Meghalaya Board of School Education
Acronym MBOSE
Category Supplementary Routine
Mode of Routine Online
Official Website www.mbose.in

The MBOSE HSSLC Supplementary exam 2019 is an offline, subjective exam that will last for 3 hours. It will be held only for those students who have failed in one or more subjects in the main board exam. Note that you have to register to take part in the exam. An admit card will be provided to you only after registration.

How To Register for MBOSE HSSLC Supplementary Exam 2019?

It is mandatory to register to take part in the exam. Take note of the last date of application as you won’t be able to apply after that. They may extend the last date to a few more days with a late fee. But, it is safer to register as soon as possible to avoid any hassles.

  • The registration process is very straight-forward and will be in offline mode.
  • You can get the application form at your school and need to fill it in under their guidance, mentioning all relevant details.
  • Make sure to fill it incorrectly, as errors and discrepancies will lead to many complications later. Take some time and check through it once or twice before submitting.

MBOSE 12th/HSSLC Supplementary Exam Admit Card 2019

The admit card is a very important document without which you won’t be allowed to enter the exam hall. As the admit card is provided in offline mode, you can’t “redownload” it if you lose it and need to apply for a new admit card. Hence, take a photocopy of it to keep as a backup. Keep it safe and don’t forget to take it with you to the exam hall. Note that if there are any discrepancies in your admit card, you need to inform the concerned authorities immediately. Note that you will get your admit card only after submitting your application form. If you haven’t done so yet, check out the above section to learn more.

  • Your school authorities will provide the admit card.
  • It will carry the authorized signature and stamp.
  • It will hold the following details of the candidate
    • Student Name
    • Roll Number
    • Subject Name
    • Exam Date
    • Exam Centre
    • Centre Code
    • Students & Authority Signature

MBOSE HSSLC Supplementary Exam Result 2019

You can check the results tentatively in the month of June 2019 in online mode. The long wait is over. This is when all your hard work pays off! To check your results, just follow these easy steps:

  1. Visit the official results website (link given above).
  2. Click on “Higher Secondary School Leaving Certificate Supplementary Examination (HSSLC), 2019” link.
  3. You can log in using your roll number.
  4. You will now be able to view your results.
  5. Download it and take a print out of it for future reference.

If you have not passed in one or more of the MBOSE main board examinations, you need to register for the supplementary exams. You can do so by obtaining the application form from your school and filling it in. After registering, you will be provided the admit card for the exam, carrying the official signature and seal on it. Make sure to carry the admit card to the exam hall as you won’t be allowed to enter without it. The results after the exam can be checked on any of the sites linked above, tentatively in the month of July 2019.
