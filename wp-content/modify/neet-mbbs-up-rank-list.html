NEET MBBS UP Rank List 2019 Released | Check Uttar Pradesh MBBS Merit List Here

July 5, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

NEET MBBS UP Rank List 2019: UP MBBS NEET Merit List 2019 released on June 25, 2019, by DMET (Directorate of Medical Education and Training), Lucknow. 85% of NEET MBBS UP Merit List of state quota seats will release online. NEET Merit List will contain the names of the candidates who qualified NEET 2019 as per the state’s eligibility criteria (Domicile of UP) and will successfully be registered for UP MBBS Admissions 2019.

NEET Merit List

NEET UP MBBS Rank List will contain the information of candidate’s state rank, roll number, NEET marks, NEET percentile, category, sub-category, NEET rank and status of the fee payment. candidates whose names appear in NEET Merit List of UP MBBS will be able to attend the Counselling Procedure for admissions into 3920 MBBS and 2251 BDS Seats offered in government and private colleges of Uttar Pradesh. Here we have provided all the information about NEET MBBS Rank List including the steps on how to download NEET UP Rank List.

NEET MBBS UP Rank List 2019 for 85% State Quota Seats

NEET 2019 candidates can check their results as soon as it is released on June 5, 2019. After checking of Results start planning of Counselling Procedure. Checking of NEET Result 2019 will helps you in predicting your chances of getting admission in medical colleges and BDS of India.

NEET Merit List will be prepared for both quota’s All India Quota and State Quota. All India Quota NEET Merit List will be prepared by MCC for 15% of seats across the country. State Quota NEET Merit List will be prepared by the admission authority of  UP i.e Directorate of Medical Education and Training, Lucknow for 85% of seats.

How to Download NEET MBBS UP Rank List 2019?

Now you have got an idea about how seats are distributed and on State quota information. Let us know about the steps on how to download NEET MBBS UP Rank list online:

  • Step – 1: Visit the official website of UP NEET @ updgme.in
  • Step – 2: Search for “UP MBBS/BDS Merit List 2019” and click on it.
  • Step – 3: Now NEET MBBS Rank List will be opened in a new page.
  • Step – 4: Now on keyboard press Ctrl + F to find your name and registration number.
  • Step – 5: Once you have found your name download NEET MBBS UP Merit List 2019 for future purpose.

NEET MBBS UP Rank List Based on NEET 2019 Cut Off

Uttar Pradesh NEET Merit List for UG courses will be prepared on basis of NEET Cutoff 2019 scores gained by students. NEET Cutoff has been released along with NEET Result 2019 candidates can download NEET Cutoff once the result has been released on the official website. NEET UP MBBS Rank List 2019 Tiebreaking Procedure

If two or more students will get same marks tie – break will appear among those students, then certain rules have to be followed to pick the candidates for a certain rank.

  • Candidates who score high marks in Biology will be considered.
  • If the tie still exists, then candidates who score good marks in Chemistry will be considered.
  • If the tie Still Continous students with lesser wrong attempts in NEET 2019 will be taken.
  • Even after the tie continous, the candidate who is elder in age will be given the priority.

NEET MBBS UP Rank List 2019 Counselling and Seat Allotment

The counseling and allocation of seats to Uttar Pradesh are done by DMET in Lucknow based on the NEET MBBS UP rank list. DMET performs two rounds of counseling process for candidates appearing in the merit list. Merit-listed candidates must fill colleges and course options in the counseling process. The first round of counseling will take place in the first week of July 2019. After filling the seats from the first round, the authority conducts the second round of UP MBBS counseling. Selected candidates should report to their counseling centers at a specified time and date.
