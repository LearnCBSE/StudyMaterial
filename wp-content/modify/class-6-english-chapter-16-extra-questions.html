National War Memorial NCERT Class 6 English Chapter 16 Extra Questions and Answers

Question 1.
What is the theme of the poem?
Answer:
The central theme of the poem is the tribute to the brave soldiers of Bharat (India) who have sacrificed their lives for the country. It emphasizes the honour and respect given to them at the National War Memorial.

Question 2.
What does the National War Memorial symbolize in the poem?
Answer:
The National War Memorial symbolizes respect, honour, and eternal gratitude for the soldiers who laid down their lives for the nation. It stands as a testament to their bravery and sacrifice.

Question 3.
How does the poet describe the memorial?
Answer:
The poet describes the memorial as a grand sight, with names etched on stones, each telling a story of bravery. The eternal flame glows in silence, symbolizing the enduring spirit and sacrifice of the soldiers.

Question 4.
What emotions are conveyed through the poem?
Answer:
The poem conveys emotions of pride, respect, and reverence. It reflects the nation’s gratitude and the solemn remembrance of the soldiers’ sacrifices.

Question 5.
What imagery does the poet use to describe the soldiers?
Answer:
The poet uses imagery of soldiers standing together on battlefields, skies, and oceans, signifying their unity and courage in defending the nation. The eternal flame is also a powerful image representing their lasting legacy.

Question 6.
How does the poet suggest we should pay homage to the soldiers?
Answer:
The poet suggests that we should pay homage to the soldiers with pride and respect, by remembering their sacrifices and honoring their memory at places like the National War Memorial.

National War Memorial Class 6 Extract Based Questions

Read the following extracts and answer the questions that follow choosing the best option from among the given ones.

1. In Bharat’s heartland, a grand sight to behold, The National War Memorial, honours brave stories retold. With respect and grace, it stands with pride, Each name etched, each stone, each story to confide. From battlefields to skies, and to oceans wide. They stand for Bharat, side by side. The eternal flame, in silence it glows, Paying homage to the courage that shows. Their sacrifice, is always in our minds, In blessed memory, and forever reminds us. With pride and respect, let’s always remember, At the National War Memorial, their sacrifice forever. (Page 160)

Question 1.
What does the National War Memorial honor?
(a) Cultural heritage
(b) Brave stories retold
(c) Scientific achievements
(d) Political leaders
Answer:
(b) Brave stories retold

Question 2.
How does the National War Memorial stand?
(a) With elegance and charm
(b) With pride and respect
(c) With grandeur and opulence
(d) With simplicity and humility
Answer:
(b) With pride and respect

Question 3.
What is etched on the stones of the National War Memorial?
(a) Famous quotes
(b) Names and stories of brave individuals
(c) Artistic designs
(d) Historic dates
Answer:
(b) Names and stories of brave individuals

Question 4.
What elements are mentioned as part of the memorial’s tribute?
(a) Cultural artifacts
(b) Landscapes and gardens
(c) Eternal flame and stones
(d) Statues of leaders
Answer:
(c) Eternal flame and stones

Question 5.
What does the eternal flame symbolize?
(a) Joy and celebration
(b) Eternal life and glory
(c) Silence and respect
(d) Homage to courage
Answer:
(d) Homage to courage

Question 6.
Where do the individuals honored at the memorial stand for Bharat?
(a) In cities and villages
(b) On battlefields, in skies, and oceans
(c) In schools and colleges
(d) In temples and shrines
Answer:
(b) On battlefields, in skies, and oceans
