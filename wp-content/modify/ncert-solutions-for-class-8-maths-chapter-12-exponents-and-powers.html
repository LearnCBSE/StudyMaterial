Ex 12.1 Class 8 Maths Question 5.
Find the value of m for which 5 m ÷ 5 -3 = 5 5 .
Solution:
5 m ÷ 5 -3 = 5 5
⇒ 5 m-(-3) = 5 5 [∵ a m ÷ a n = a m-n ]
⇒ 5 m+3 = 5 5
Comparing the powers of equal bases, we have
m + 3 = 5
m = 5 – 3 = 2, i.e., m = 2
