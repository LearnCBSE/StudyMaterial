Chhattisgarh 10th Board Results How to Check the Results?

The results of Chhattisgarh Board Class 10 Examination will be available online on cgbse.nic.in . Follow the steps given below to check the results.

  1. Go to the official website cgbse.nic.in .
  2. Enter your roll number and the captcha.
  3. Click on “Submit”
  4. The result page will open

The following information will be listed on the results page of the candidate

  1. Student’s Name
  2. Roll Number of a student
  3. Examination Name
  4. Board Name
  5. Your Date of Birth
  6. Respective class
  7. Marks Obtained
  8. Total Marks
  9. Status of the Result- Pass/ Fail

Chhattisgarh 10th Board Retotaling

The Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education gives the candidates the option of getting their marks recalculated, in case of possible discrepancy. The candidates have to apply online for the same.  Then after this board will come up with CGBSE 10th result 2024 (after totaling).
