UP Board Class 10 Supplementary Exam 2019: UP Board Class 10 Supplementary Exam 2019 is conducted for students who fail to pass in one exam. The supplementary exam is also mentioned here as the compartment exam. Students who failed in any one subject of class 10th this year can apply for compartment exam. Also, there is Improvement exam conducted for the student, who wants to score better marks in any subject. Students can apply for one or two subjects, to improve their marks in the final result. The application form for compartment exam and improvement exam will be made available very soon.

UP Board Class 10 Compartment Exam Results has been released on July 27, 2019. Click Here to Check Class 10 Compartment Exam Results.

UP Board Class 10 Compartment Exams Admit Card 2019 has been released on July 10, 2019, Click Here to Check Class 10 Compartment Exams Admit Card.

State Board Supplementary Exams

No students want to get fail or repeat the same class, but if they get fail in any one subject in the final exam, they are given with an opportunity by Uttar Pradesh board to re-attempt the exam or give the supplementary exam and get passed in the particular subject, improving their scores in the 10th or SSLC certificate.

UP Board Class 10 Supplementary Exams Results have been released on June 27, 2019, Click Here to Check Class 10 Supplementary Exam Results.

UP Board Class 10 Supplementary exam Time Table 2019 will be declared by UP Board very soon after the class 10th results. Students who have to appear for the compartment exam can check out the time table which will be provided by the board, along with the application form on the official website, upmsp.edu.in. The application fees to apply for this exam is Rs.256.50/-

UP Board Class 10th exam was conducted from 6th Feb to 22nd Feb 2019. Students who have failed in a particular subject have to mention the subject name while applying for the compartment exam.   • UP Board Supplementary Time Table 2019 PDF will appear on the new window.
  • Download the Time Table and keep it for future use.

UP Board 10th Supplementary Exam Registration

Follow the below-given steps to apply for UP class 10th compartment exam in 2019.

  • Visit the official website of UP Board, upmsp.edu.in
  • On the homepage, click on the ‘Apply for High School 10th compartment exam’ link.
  • Fill the details which are asked in the registration form
  • Click on submit button and take a print out of registration form for future reference.

UP Board 10th Supplementary Exam Admit Card

The admit card for 10th supplementary exam 2019, conducted by UP Board, will be released on June 2019, tentatively, before the start of examinations. The admit card for all the students will be released online on the official website, upmsp.edu.in.

Students who have applied for supplementary exam for class 10th, have to download the admit card from the official website itself. No other mode will be available to get the admit card. Follow the below steps to download it.

  • Go to the official website for UP Class 10th Compartment exam.
  • Check the link given for supplementary exam admit card in the notifications and click it.
  • Enter your Registration Number and click on Submit.
  • Admit Card will appear on the new screen and students have to check for their mistakes. If any discrepancies exist, they must report to the school authorities immediately.
  • Take a printout of the admit card and keep it safe with you to present in the exam hall.
  • The admit card for 10th supplementary exam will carry details such as, name of the student, his/her parent’s name, exam center, roll number, subject name and code.

UP Board Class 10th Supplementary Exam Results

The results for 10th supplementary exam 2019, conducted by UP Board, will be declared in July 2019, tentatively, after the conduct of examinations. The time table for the exam will be declared soon. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, upmsp.edu.in. The level of the exam will be UP State board level.

The official link to check the supplementary exam results is upmsp.edu.in or http://upresults.nic.in/. Students who want to check the results for 10th compartment exam, follow the given steps;

  • Go to the official website of the UP 10th Board – upmsp.edu.in
  • Click on the Supplementary/Compartment 10th Result 2019 link.
  • Students are required to enter Roll Number and date of birth and click on Submit.
  • UP Board Class 10th compartment exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.
