PACE Scholarship Entrance Exam for IIT JEE, AIMS, NEET – Super 50 Programme

November 30, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

PACE Scholarship: Pace Scholarship Entrance Exam gives a chance to many students where they can show their academic intelligence and acquire scholarship on the tuition fee. PACE is a well-known coaching institute to prepare and train students for engineering and medical entrance examinations. With dedicated teachers and management continually striving to provide the best feature of education and mentorship to scholars, over the years, this coaching institute has consistently obtained outstanding results.

PACE in partnership with the Tribal Development Department is rendering the best training for competitive exams to Super-50 Tribal Caste students of Maharashtra in Engineering and Medical stream each.

The purpose of this scheme is to give top coaching for the welfare of tribal caste students of Maharashtra State and to focus on their educational development, especially for students who seek to be Engineers or Doctors and are soliciting guidance for the entrance exam like IIT JEE / NEET / AIIMS. Top 100 scholars (50 each for Engineering and Medical) will be chosen by an entrance exam and best practice will be provided to the selected students for competitive exams. Elected students will get their education, boarding and lodging did at Vagad Pace Global School located at Virar (E), Mumbai.

Scholarships for Students

PACE Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

  • All the tribal caste (Class 10th to 12th moving) students studying under 1000 schools that come under Tribal Development Department, Maharashtra will be eligible.
  • The student must have cleared the 10th board Examination in 2019
  • The student must possess Original Tribal Caste Certificate

PACE Super 50 Entrance Examination

The entrance exam will be conducted in 2 Phases.

First Phase

  • The first Screening test for both Engineering and Medical applicants will be conducted across all the 1000 schools on 28th June 2019. Timing for the entrance exam will be:
Engineering paper 11:00 AM – 12:30 PM
Medical paper 12:30 PM – 2:00 PM
  • Reporting time will be 1 hour before the exam.
  • 400 students (200 each from Engineering and Medical) will be chosen based on their performance in Screening Test.

Second Phase

  • Second and Final Entrance Exam of all the Selected 400 students will be conducted on 12th July 2019.
  • The timing of the entrance exam will be 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM for both Engineering and Medical paper.
  • Reporting time will be 1 hour prior to the exam.

PACE Super 50 Exam Pattern Phase 1

The format of question paper for the Screening test will be

Total no. of questions 30 each in Engineering & Medical paper
Total duration 90 minutes each for Engineering & Medical paper
Marking scheme +4 for every correct answer, No negative marking
Syllabus & Weightage For Engineering aspirants: Mathematics (up to class X SSC board) and Mental ability.
For Medical aspirants: 10 questions each on Physics, Chemistry & Biology (up to class X SSC board)
Language of question paper English & Marathi

PACE Super 50 Exam Pattern Phase 2

The format of question paper for the Entrance Exam will be

Total no. of questions 45
Total duration 120 minutes
Marking scheme +4 for every correct answer, No negative marking
Syllabus and Weightage For Engineering aspirants: Mathematics (up to class X SSC board) and Mental ability.
For Medical aspirants: 15 questions each on Physics, Chemistry & Biology (up to class X SSC board)
Language of question paper English & Marathi

PACE Scholarship Admission Procedure

  • Selected scholars can register themselves for the specified courses till 24th July 2019 at Vagad Pace Global School. They have to carry original and a copy of
    • Tribal caste certificate
    • Birth certificate
    • 10th mark sheet
    • School leaving certificate (or Transfer certificate)
    • Aadhaar card (if any student doesn’t have it, he/she can apply & submit till 25th Sep 2019).
  • The copies of all the above documents will be submitted by students to Vagad Pace Global School at the time of admission.
  • Pace will also give an extended list of 10 students each for Engineering and Medical who will be given a chance if any selected student doesn’t take admission before 24th July 2019.
  • No fee shall be charged by Iitians Pace/Vagad Pace Global School to any student selected for the program.

PACE Scholarship Direct Admission

PACE has set a percentage criterion for each course. Students scoring 90% or above in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/ Biology or rank 1, 2 or 3 in IX final exam or X prelims can get direct admission. Students who qualify for direct admission criteria can also appear for the Ace of Pace Scholarship Exam to avail of a scholarship.

If a student does not fulfill the percentage criterion, he/ she has to appear for the Ace of Pace Scholarship Exam. On the basis of score in the exam, a student is offered admission and scholarship.

About PACE Scholarship

Founded in 1999, PACE began with a few students, a few sessions old, the institute estimated new heights of success by providing coaching for Engineering and Medical entrance exam preparation. New breakthroughs were set each year and the institute attained a remarkable landmark with hundreds of selections in 2008. Besides the Engineering and Medical domain, PACE delivered outstanding results in the International Olympiads as well. The institute also began providing preparation courses for Study Abroad. Every year, our students get admission into top international universities. The institute has now become the preferred choice for students, who want to build a strong foundation for their higher studies in Engineering & Medical, or accomplish top ranks in competitive exams or Study Abroad programs.

PACE stands similar to quality education and presents a learning environment that extends learning beyond the bars of traditional classroom learning. We at PACE understand that education is the most effectual weapon to help bring the desired change in our society and to contribute to society at large. We also provide free education for commendable and deserving students from humble backgrounds.
