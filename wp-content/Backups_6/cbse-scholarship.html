CBSE Scholarship 2023 | cbse.nic.in Apply Online, Dates, Eligibility, Application Process

November 4, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

CBSE Scholarship 2023-2024: CBSE Scholarship or CBSE Merit Scholarship Scheme for Single Girl Child (only girl child of the family) awards financial support to exemplary girl students of class 10 to proceed with their studies in class 11 and 12. Administered by the Central Board of Secondary Education, the education board of the nation, the CBSE scholarship plan directs at promoting the higher education of the daughters who are only girl child in their family. The closing date to apply for the scholarship online has been being to October 2023, both for fresh and renewal. The last date to submit the hard copy of the application for renewal of scholarship has been extended to November 2023.

Central Board of Secondary Education awards scholarships to students under various categories. The award of the scholarship is done purely on the basis of eligibility criteria and merit. The application forms are scrutinized by the screening committees. The status of applicants for various scholarships is displayed on the website of CBSE (www.cbse.nic.in) from time to time and for any query/information please contact on E-mail ID of Scholarship Unit i.e. ( [email protected] ).

Question 1.
How I can apply for the scholarship?

Answer:
CBSE awards scholarships under various schemes. Details of these along with eligibility are available in the Guidelines and Application form page. The applicant should ensure that he/she submits the application duly completed in all respect before the last date.

Question 2.
What are the reasons for the rejection of an application?

Answer:
The rejection may be due to Application not in proper format and non-enclosure of relevant documents.

Question 3.
What is the definition of an SGC (Single Girl Child) applicant?

Answer:
Single Girl child means a girl who is the only child of their parents and there is no brother or sisters.

Question 4.
I am a single girl in the family. I have one brother. Can I apply for a scholarship under the CBSE Single Girl Child Scholarship 2019-20?

Answer:
No, As per the definition of Single Girl Children you will not be considered for scholarship under the scheme as single Girl child are those girl children whose parents have only one girl child and there are no other siblings.

Question 5.
I have received a cheque which has date expiring today only. How can I get a valid cheque?

Answer:
Normally CBSE sends a cheque through partner banks by post and chances of delay are not there. However, in such a case, you may send the cheque to the CBSE scholarship branch for getting a valid cheque.

Question 6.
Whether I will require to apply for renewal of scholarship again?

Answer:
Yes, you have to apply for renewal separately.
