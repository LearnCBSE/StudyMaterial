If cos A + cos 2 A = 1, prove that sin 2 A + sin 4 A = 1
Solution:

⇒ cos A = 1 – cos 2 A
⇒cos A = sin 2 A
Now, sin 2 A + sin 4 A = sin 2 A + (sin 2 A) 2
= cos A + cos 2 A = 1 = R.H.S.
