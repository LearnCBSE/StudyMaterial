Uttarakhand Scholarship 2023 | Dates, Eligibility, Rewards and Application Process

October 31, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Uttarakhand Scholarship 2023-2024: Uttarakhand Scholarship initiated by the Social Welfare Department, Government of Uttarakhand. This scholarship provides financial support to the SC/ST/OBC/PH/Minority students for pursuing their education. This scholarship helps students pursue their studies within or outside of Uttarakhand from recognized institutions. The SC/ST/OBC/PH/Minority students of Uttarakhand state can only avail of this scholarship. This scholarship will be awarded every year to the deserving students studying in any recognized institution in any district of Uttarakhand.

True

Latest Update:

The last date to apply for Uttarakhand Scholarship is extended to 30th September 2023. Candidates can apply to Uttarakhand Scholarship through the National Scholarship Portal . The online application process for the Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Minority (100 percent State Sector) is open now, the last date to apply is 30th November 2023.

Uttarakhand Scholarship 2023

Applications for the Uttarakhand Scholarship 2023 will be released in online mode only. All eligible students from any district of the state can avail of this scholarship. The Govt of Uttarakhand has organized this scholarship to support SC/ST/OBC/PH/Minority students from lower-income families for pursuing their education. The Govt of Uttarakhand aims to provide training and awards to students in the form of scholarships.

The E-scholarship portal verifies the eligibility of students and approves the applications after document verification. This scholarship provided to uplift the education and learning system of Uttarakhand state. This scholarship encourages the students of Uttarakhand to pursue their education without financial hassle. The selected students will be awarded scholarships for pursuing their education. There are different types of Uttarakhand Scholarships available for students of the state. So, interested students can check and apply for the following types of Uttarakhand Scholarships.

  • Post-Matric Scholarship
  • Pre-Matric Minority Scholarship Scheme (100% funded by State)
  • Gaura Devi Kanya Dhan Scholarship

Post-Matric Scholarship

This scholarship offered to SC/ST/OBC/PH or Girl students studying class 11 or above in any recognized institute of Uttarakhand. Students studying ITI, Polytechnic, Technical Education, Medical and other professional education can also apply for this scholarship.

Eligibility Criteria

Students must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to apply for the Post-Matric scholarship.

  • Students must be a domicile of Uttarakhand state.
  • Students who have got admission in private colleges under management quota are not eligible.
  • Students belonging to SC/ST/OBC categories and studying class 11 or above in any recognized institute are eligible.
  • Students must have passed the previous qualifying examination.
  • The family income of SC/ST students should not be more than 2.5 lakh per annum.
  • The family income of OBC students should not be more than 1 lakh per annum.
  • Students who are engaged in receiving scholarships from any other source are not eligible.

Rewards – The selected students will be awarded the following scholarship amount under the Post-Matric Scholarship.

Pre-Matric Scholarship

This scholarship offered to the SC/ST/OBC/PH/Minority students who are studying in the state, council and recognized schools/madrasas of Uttarakhand.

Eligibility Criteria

Students must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to apply for the Pre-Matric Scholarship.

  • Students must be a domicile of Uttarakhand state.
  • Students belonging to Jain, Parsi, Muslim, Buddhism, Sikh, Christian minority groups are eligible.
  • Students who are studying in class 1 to 10 are eligible.
  • Students must have passed the previous qualifying examination.
  • The family income of SC/ST students of class 9 and 10 should not be more than 2 lakh per annum.
  • The SC/ST students of class 1 to 8 have no income limit.
  • The family income of OBC students should not be more than 44,500 per annum.
  • The family income of Physically Handicapped students should not be more than 24,000 per annum.
  • The family annual income of Minority Community students should be less than twice of the current income slab of BPL.

Gaura Devi Kanya Dhan Scholarship

This scholarship offered to the SC/ST/OBC/BPL of General categories of girl students in any districts of Uttarakhand.

Eligibility Criteria

Students must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to apply for the Gaura Devi Kanya Dhan Scholarship.

  • Students must be a domicile of Uttarakhand state.
  • Only girl students who are studying in class 12th in any recognized institute of Uttarakhand.
  • Students must have passed the previous qualifying examination.
  • The annual family income of the student should be under the income slab prescribed by the Social Welfare Department.

Rewards – The selected students can be availed a One-time benefit on qualifying the grade 12th exam successfully.

Uttarakhand Scholarship Selection Process

The students will get selected on the basis of their academic performance and family income. The applications received from students will be scrutinized by the Institutes. After this, they will forward the applications to the DSWO. DSWO will sanction or reject the applications after verification. The final decision will be taken by the DSWO to shortlist the students. Then, the scholarship amount will be transferred to the shortlisted student’s given bank account.

Uttarakhand Scholarship Application Process

Refer to the following points to apply online for the Uttarakhand Scholarship.

  • Go to the official website of the Uttarakhand e-Scholarship Portal.
  • Click on the scholarship scheme that appeared on the homepage of the portal.
  • Click on the ‘Student Registration’ that appeared under the Registration menu.
  • Enter the correct mobile number and email to get the login credentials, application status, etc. in your email.
  • Fill other correct information like personal details, permanent address, etc in the form.
  • Then, click on ‘Save and Register’ to complete the registration process.
  • After this, take a printed copy of the application form.
  • Then, submit the application form along with the documents in the respective institute.
  • The institute will accept or reject the applications after the verification.
  • On acceptance, they will forward the sanctioned applications to DSWO for further verification.
  • The DSWO will sanction or reject the applications after verification.
  • After sanctioning, the scholarship will be disbursed to the selected students.

Documents Required for Uttarakhand Scholarship

The following documents must be attached along with the application to apply for the Uttarakhand Scholarship.

  • Aadhar Card/ Aadhar Enrollment Number
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • A recent passport size photograph
  • Parents Income Certificate
  • Copy of the first page of the bank passbook holding account in the name of the student or joint account with parents

Uttarakhand Scholarship Renewal

The scholarship will be renewed in the subsequent years based on the student’s performance in the current year. Each student can apply only one application for renewal in that particular academic year. All the duplicate or permanent rejected applications will be removed from the renewal process. That is why the login credentials of those students will get deactivated.

But, students who have already registered for the Uttarakhand Scholarship, no need to register again for renewal. They can log in with their existing credentials to renew their online application for the particular academic year. Then, they can edit the details in the existing application form.  After this, they can download the modified scholarship application form to apply for renewal.

Uttarakhand Scholarship Application Status

Students can check their Application Status online on the Uttarakhand E-Scholarship Portal. Refer to the following points to check the application status in the e-scholarship portal.

  • Go to the official website of the Uttarakhand e-Scholarship Portal.
  • Click on the particular scholarship scheme that appeared on the homepage.
  • Click on the ‘Search for Applications’ menu.
  • Then, select the academic year and Institute.
  • Enter your application number and captcha code.
  • Click on the ‘Search’ to see your application status.

FAQ’s on Uttarakhand Scholarship

Question 1.
What is the Uttarakhand Scholarship?

Answer:
Uttarakhand Scholarship initiated by the Social Welfare Department, Government of Uttarakhand. This scholarship provides financial support to the SC/ST/OBC/PH/Minority students for pursuing their education.

Question 2.
What are the different types of Uttarakhand Scholarship?

Answer:
Interested students can check and apply for the following types of Uttarakhand Scholarships.

  • Post-Matric Scholarship
  • Pre-Matric Minority Scholarship Scheme (100% funded by State)
  • Gaura Devi Kanya Dhan Scholarship

Question 3.
Is the Uttarakhand Scholarship available for all State students?

Answer:
No, only the SC/ST/OBC/PH/Minority students of Uttarakhand state can avail of this scholarship.

Question 4.
What are the starting and closing dates of application for the Uttarakhand Scholarship?

Answer:
The start date of application for the Uttarakhand Scholarship is September 2023. Whereas the last date of the Uttarakhand Scholarship application submission is November 2023.

Question 5.
What is the Gaura Devi Kanya Dhan Scholarship Scheme in Uttarakhand Scholarship?

Answer:
This scholarship offered to the SC/ST/OBC/BPL of General categories of only girl students in any districts of Uttarakhand.
