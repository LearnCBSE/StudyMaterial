Oasis Scholarship 2023 2024: Oasis Scholarship is an online scholarship scheme introduced for all underprivileged students of SC, ST and OBC categories. Oasis Scholarship is a dedicated scholarship portal for West Bengal students. These schemes are administered by the Backward Classes Welfare Department, Government of West Bengal. In this website, you will find both pre-matric scholarship and post-matric scholarships for students who are citizens of this state. The main objective to introduce this Oasis scholarship website is to build a dedicated online system for filling out the application form and managing scholarships offered by the government.   • Class 9th and 10th student who belong to SC category.
  • The family/guardian annual income should not be more than Rs. 2 Lakh per annum from all sources.
