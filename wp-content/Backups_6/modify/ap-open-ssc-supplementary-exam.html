AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam 2019 | Time Table (Released), Admit Card, Results

July 1, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam 2019: Andhra Pradesh Open school has released the time table for the July exam 2019. Thus, all the students appearing for AP Open SSC supplementary 2019 can check the entire time table here. The official time table has been released by Andhra Pradesh Open school on their main website which is apOpenschool.org. As per the schedule was given, the exams will be conducted from July 11 to 18.

AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam 2019 Important Dates

The time table for AP Open SSC supplementary is now available in PDF format. This exam is for the candidates that failed the main board exam. AP Open SSC supplementary exam time table is also available in the Telugu language. Below are the important dates related to AP Open SSC supplementary exam 2019

AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam Schedule Dates
Time table availability 15th June 2019
Examination Dates 11th to 18th July 2019
Practical Exam Dates 19th to 21st July 2019

Along with main dates of the exam, the AP board has also released the date of the practical exam. The practical exam is going to be conducted from 19th July to 21st July.

AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam Application Form

AP Open SSC supplementary exam is going to be conducted in the month of July. Thus, it is important for students appearing for the exam to fill out the application form. Only the students that have filled out the application form successfully will be allowed to appear for the exams in July. Thus, we have listed out the application fee which is also given in the table below.

Application Fee Dates
Without Late Fee 17- 21 Jun 2019
With Late Fee of Rs. 25/- (Per Subject) 22- 24 Jun 2019
With Late Fee of Rs. 50/- (Per Subject) 25- 26 Jun 2019

How To Download AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam Time Table?

  • Step 1: Visit the homepage of AP board from your desktop apopenschool.org .
  • Step 2: On the homepage, you will find a link about the AP Open SSC supplementary exam time table.
  • Step 3: When you click on the link a new tab will Open which will contain the time table in PDF format.
  • Step 4: Check the schedule of your subjects very carefully and download the PDF.

AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam Result

All those students that have not cleared the main board exam are given another chance of clearing the exam. Thus, the board conducts the supplementary exam for these candidates in July and releases the result by August month. For the supplementary exam, students have to register themselves soon after the result for board exam is declared. AP Open SSC supplementary exam result will be declared online for everyone. To check for the result, students need to end they admit it card number correctly.

How To Download AP Open SSC Supplementary Exam Result?

AP Open SSC supplementary exam board is going to announce the result online. So, know the steps below to check for the AP Open SSC supplementary exam result 2019:

  • Step 1: Go the official homepage of the AP board from your desktop. Remember that the link will always be present, but will be active once the official declaration is made.
  • Step 2: When you click on the result link in the homepage, a new tab will pop up.
  • Step 3: There will be a space provided where you need to enter your admit card details.
  • Step 4: When you submit your details, AP Open SSC supplementary exam result will be visible on your screen. Check the marks properly and download the result for any future purposes.

About Andhra Pradesh Open School

AP Open school was established in February 1991. The board was established with the aim of providing education to those who are not able to continue their regular classes. Thus, the AP board provides the facility of getting the education from distance at the immediate level. The motto of the AP board is to be empowered and be educated. Every year there are lakhs of students that appear for this exam at the state level. The AP board conducts the SSC board exam twice in a year. One of this examination is the main board exam and the other one is the supplementary exam.
