Nagaland (NBSE) HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019 | Results (Released), Time Table, Admit Card

June 22, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

NBSE HSLC Supplementary Exam 2019: Nagaland (NBSE) HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019 is conducted for students who have secured “Need Improvement” grade in 3 or fewer subjects. No students want to get fail or repeat the same standard, but if they get lose to secure passing grade in three or less than three subjects in the board exam, they are given with an opportunity by Nagaland board to appear in Compartmental Exam and get passed in the particular subject, improving their scores in the HSLC certificate.

State Board Supplementary Exams

Nagaland HSLC Compartment Examination Notification 2019 has been declared by Nagaland Board of School Education(NBSE) on 1st May 2019. Students who have to appear for the same can check out the entire details related to it here. The exam will be conducted from 29th May to 4th June 2019. Students can download the Nagaland  HSLC Compartmental Time table or routine 2019 from the official website of the board- nbsenagaland.com.

Nagaland  Board HSLC exam was conducted in February 2019 and results were declared on 2nd May 2019. Therefore, if any student was not able to qualify any subjects, not more than three, can apply for the class HSLC Compartmental Exam. Students who have secured Need Improvement grade in a particular subject have to mention the subject name while applying for the Compartmental Exam. The student who fails to qualify in compartmental exam, have to reappear for the same class in the next academic session, i.e. HSLC 2020. No separate merit list or topper list will be declared by the board for the compartmental exam. Go through the complete details related to HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019, here in this article.

How To Apply for Nagaland Class 12th Compartmental Exam?

For those students who have not qualified in three or less than 3 subjects in HSLC Main Exam 2019 have the opportunity to improve their grade by giving compartmental exam conducted by Nagaland Board.

  • Students who are eligible can reach their respective schools/HSLC 2019 Examination Centers for filling up the forms.
  • The last date to submit the application form to the respective centers by the school is 14th May 2019.
  • A designated center has been identified where there is multiple exam centers for the collection of the forms.
  • The last date to submit the application form to the designated center by the students is 15th May 2019.
  • The center superintendents of the designated centers shall submit the application form on or before 17th May 2019.
  • Nagaland Board will not accept the application form directly from the students.

Nagaland HSLC Supplementary  Exam 2019 Time Table

To download the routine for HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019 conducted by Nagaland Board, follow the below steps;

  • Go to the official website for the Improvement time table- nbsenagaland.com
  • Click on the Routine section available at the home page.
  • Students have to click on the Nagaland HSLC Compartmental Exam Time Table link.
  • Nagaland HSLC Compartmental Exam Routine 2019 will appear on the new window.
  • Download the Time Table and keep it for future use.

Nagaland HSLC Supplementary Exam 2019 Admit Card

  • The admit card for HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019, conducted by Nagaland  Secondary Education Examination Board, will be available on the official board.
  • Visit the official website and click on the link given for Nagaland HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019 Admit card.
  • Provide the registration number and roll number as per mentioned in the admit card and submit it.
  • The admit card for HSLC Compartmental Exam will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a print out.
  • Bring the admit card at the exam center, else you will not be permitted to give the exam.
  • The exams are going to be conducted in the month of May-June 2019.

Nagaland HSLC Supplementary Exam 2019 Results

The results for HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019, conducted by Nagaland Board of School Education, will be declared in June 2019, after the conduct of examinations. The exams are going to be conducted from 29th May to 4th June 2019. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, nbsenagaland.com. The level of the exam will be the State board level.

Students who want to check the results for HSLC Compartmental Exam, follow the given steps

  • Go to the Nagaland Board official website.
  • Click on the Results Section in home page.
  • Click on the Compartmental Exam HSLC Result 2019 link.
  • Students are required to enter roll number mentioned in admit card and click on Submit.
  • Nagaland HSLC Compartmental Exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.

About NBSE

The NBSE Board was established via an Act by the Nagaland Legislative Assembly and obtained the assent of the Governor on the 15th of November 1973. It started operating from the 1st October 1974, and in March 1975, the Board held its first High School Leaving Certificate Examination at 6 (six) centers, for 2008 candidates.

The main objectives of the board are

  1. To prescribe syllabus and textbooks for examinations
  2. To prescribe conditions of examinations, conduct examinations and be responsible for making necessary arrangements for the same
  3. To publish the results of external examinations
  4. To prescribe courses of instruction for examinations
