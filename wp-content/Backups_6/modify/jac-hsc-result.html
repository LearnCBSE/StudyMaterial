What after Jharkhand Board 12th Result 2024?

Once the students have downloaded the JAC 12th Result 2024 on their systems, they should save it for future reference. The online Jharkhand 12th Result 2024 is only to provide the early information to the students and is subject to change in case any discrepancy is found. The downloaded copy of the Jharkhand Board Class 12 result 2024 is meant to act as only a provisional status of result. Students will be required to collect their original marksheets from their respective institutions as soon as they are dispatched by the JAC. Authorities will always ask for the original mark sheets issued by the JAC to corroborate the online / printed scorecard before completing their admission formalities.

JAC 12th Result 2024 Re-evaluation/ Re-checking

A significant point to keep in mind is that the students can opt for re-evaluation if they believe there could be a counting mistake or rechecking if they wish their copies to be rechecked. In both the cases, they will be required to submit an application along with a fee as prescribed by the board. For more information, in this regard, they may contact their respective schools/institutions.

JAC 12th Result 2024 – Compartmental Exam

The students who do not manage to do well in the Jharkhand Board result 2024 despite their best efforts can appear for Jharkhand Board Compartmental/Improvement Exam. This exam provides the candidates with a second chance to improve their scores in a single or multiple subject(s). Students can apply for appearing in the Compartmental/improvement exam of Jharkhand board by filling in the application form that will be made available on the official website of Jharkhand Board of Secondary Education. The Jharkhand Board Compartmental examination 2024 will be held in the month of July/August.

About Jharkhand Academic Council

An Act to establish the Jharkhand Academic Council was enacted by the Jharkhand State Legislature and assented to by the Governor of the State on 26.12.2003, which was known as Jharkhand Academic Council Act 02.7.2003. As per the official website, the Jharkhand Academic Council was established for holding and conducting examinations at the end of Intermediate, Education, Secondary Education, Sanskrit Education and Madrasa Education and for prescribing courses of studies for such examinations and for recommending for recognition of Intermediate Educational Institutions, High Schools, Sanskrit Schools and Madrasas to the State Government and for carrying out such other or duties assigned to the council by the state government from time to time.

The JAC currently follows the CBSE board pattern, to improve education standards in the state in all branches of education viz. Secondary, Intermediate Sanskrit (Madhyama) and Madarsa. They have been brought under the single leadership of Jharkhand Academic Council. The JAC has been constantly endeavoring to provide the equitable and unified attention at all the levels of education.
