Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship 2023-24 | Dates, Application Process, Eligibility, Selection Process

November 4, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship 2023-2024: An initiative by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, the Atal Bihari Vajpayee scholarship is a program aimed at providing financial assistance to the needy and meritorious students who have completed their class 10 and 12.

Moreover, the Atal Bihari Vajpayee scholarship caters to students from middle-class families. From a socio-economic perspective, this is a big leap forward as it helps to alleviate the problem of poverty and illiteracy. Read on to explore how to apply for the scholarship and other important details of the Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship 2023 for 10th Students and 12th Students.

Students can also find more Scholarship Articles for 12th passed, 10th passed Students and many more.

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

Candidates are deemed eligible if they fulfill the following eligibility criteria:

  • Candidates must an Indian citizens.
  • Candidates must also have cleared their classes 10 and 12.
  • The candidates must be between 18 and 25 years of age.
  • The candidate’s annual family income from all sources should not be over INR 6,00,000.
  • The candidate must have secured not less than 50% marks in the last appeared examination.
  • Moreover, candidates must have at least 75% attendance.
  • The scholarship is not applicable to distance learning.

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship Amount

  • The Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship rewards candidates with INR 10,000 per annum. (INR 1,000 per month for 10 months).
  • 10th class candidates can renew the scholarship for class 12. However, the candidates have to ensure that they have 75% attendance in their previous class.
  • Moreover, if candidates managed to score more than 85% marks or more in class 12, they will be awarded a sum of INR 25,000 (INR 2500 per month for 10 months).

Opening of ICCR A2A Portal for admissions 20 February 2023
Last date of application to be submitted by students 30 April 2023
Last date of Universities to inform the decision (if confirmed, intimation to Missions; if rejected, intimation to students) 31 May 2023
Last date for allocating scholarship and generating Offer Letters by Indian Mission abroad 30 June 2023
Last date of acceptance by students 15 July 2023
Based on the number of acceptances in the first round (if acceptances are less than the scholarship seats allocated) Indian Missions to offer scholarship to other students who were not considered in the first round 22 July 2023
Second round students to convey their acceptances 30 July 2023

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship Application Procedure

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship 2023 online application can be processed by the following steps:

  • Interested candidates have to visit the National Scholarship Portal at https://scholarships.gov.in/
  • Click on “New Registration”.
  • Fill in the Application form with relevant details.
  • You will then be presented with your username and password.
  • Now, go back to the homepage and log in with the new username and password.
  • Choose your preferred scholarship.
  • Fill in the required details and upload prescribed documents.
  • Cross verify the information provided and then click “Final Submit”.

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship Selection Procedure

Candidates will be selected on the basis of their merit and need. The list of selected candidates will be updated on the website within a month or two of the application date.

Documents Required for Atal Bihari Vajpayee Scholarship

Candidates are instructed to keep scanned copies of the following documents:

  • Bank account details of the candidate
  • Candidate’s ID proof
  • Income certificate
  • Candidate’s university ID proof
  • Marks sheet of the last appeared exam along with class 10 and 12 marks sheet.
