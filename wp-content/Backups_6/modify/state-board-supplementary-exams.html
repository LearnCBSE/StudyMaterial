All State Board Supplementary Exams 2019: The details of all state board supplementary exam along with CBSE and ICSE in 2019 are available here for students. These exams are conducted for those students who have not passed in any of the one subjects in their Class 10th or Class 12th Board Exam. The results for the board exam was already declared by the respective boards in their official websites, for 10th and 12th standard. Now, its time for students to get themselves ready for supplementary exams, who want to get passed in the failed subjects.

All State Board Supplementary Exams 2019 | Class 10th And Class 12th | CBSE, ICSE And State Boards

The supplementary exams for all the boards are conducted in the month of June and July, every year. Apart from state boards, there are other boards like CBSE(Central Board of Secondary Education) and ICSE(Indian School Certificate Examination), which also conducts the supplementary exam for the schools, which are affiliated to these boards. Basically, students can improve their results or score in the mark sheet by giving these exams

Class 10th and 12th Supplementary Board Exams for CBSE And ICSE 2019

The Class 10th and Class 12th supplementary exam dates will be announced by the boards in their official websites. There will be no information sent via post or any hard copy to the students for the declaration of the exam dates. Each student has to check the exam date through the official websites. The admit card for these exams will also be released on the official website, itself.

The supplementary exam in CBSE Board is also denoted as Compartment Exam and in CICSE, it is denoted as Improvement Exam. Both these boards are the two major boards in India. These are the National Level Boards. Check the exam dates for the subjects, in which you have not cleared the exam and prepare yourself accordingly to score well.
