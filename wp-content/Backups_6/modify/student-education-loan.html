Why Take An Education Loan?

If you belong to EBC or another low-income based family then pursuing your dream course can be difficult. To overcome such problematic situations and to give education to everyone various lenders from government and private have come up with student education loan schemes.

Availing of education loans is beneficial to parents and students to pursue higher education in top colleges/universities/institutes either in India or abroad. With too many features and benefits an education loan will support you in studying higher studies.

Not only middle-income group people can avail of these study loans even many high range income group people are also opting for a study loan to avail of those offered benefits. The reasons to take an education loan are many but a few of them are as follows:

  • It’s a big chance to help parents by taking their financial responsibilities and maintaining their family savings.
  • Making a good credit history
  • Income Tax benefits under IT Act Section 80E.
  • Fastly rising education costs.

Things to Consider Before Applying for Education Loan

Below are a few important points to be aware of and considered before your education loan application process begins:

  • Some of the banks offers concessions on interest rates by checking their credit score.
  • Borrowers of education loan can get tax exemption under section 80E.
  • Also, some banks provides up to 1% concession in ROI, when customer paid full amount of interest while study period.
  • CGFSEL, Dr Ambedkar central scheme on education loans, Padho Pardesh Scheme are central government schemes that offer subsidies on ROI charged on the loan amount.
  • Moreover, some banks charges extra interest as risk premium when the lender has not recieved any credit life insurance, that says liabilities are settled after insurers death.

FAQs on Student Education Loan in India & Abroad

  1. Can I take education loan as a student?

Yes, education loan can be taken by any student in India to pursue their higher education in top institutes of India or Abroad.

  1. What is the limit of student education loan?

The maximum limit of student education loan is Rs.150.00 lakh for study abroad and Rs.125.00 lakh for study in India.

  1. How can I repay my education loan?

Students who took loan for studies can repay their loan in various modes like By Cheque/DD (Demand Draft repayment), By cash, or By Automated EMI payments (ECS/NACH).

  1. Can banks increase my education loan tenure?

Yes, banks will increase the tenure of your education loan when the course is extended by the college/university/institute.

  1. What are the latest RBI Guidelines for Education Loan?

The latest guidelines for education loan by RBI desire to offer many benefits for borrowers like flexible repayment options, transparent loan repayment schedules, handle ROL based on RLLR, or MCLR, simplify the application process, and promote the use of technology in the loan application process.
