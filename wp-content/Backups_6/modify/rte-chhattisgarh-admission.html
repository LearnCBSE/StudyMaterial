RTE Chhatisgarh Admission 2019 | Application Form, Important Dates

May 10, 2020 by LearnCBSE Online

RTE Chhattisgarh Admission 2019: The right to free and compulsory education which is a basic right under the right to education was enacted in August 2009, by the parliament of India. Under this right, every child between the age of 6 and 14 years have a right to get free education. Under this seat, many state governments have reserved a fixed quota of seats in government along with the private schools. These reserved seats are for the students belonging to a financially poor background. All the important details regarding the RTE Chattisgarh admission 2019 is provided in the article.

FAQs on RTE Chattisgarh

1.  What is RTE Rule?

Right to Education act makes elementary education free and compulsory for the candidates between the age limit 6 – 14. Candidates can get Admission as per the RTE Act depending on the economic status or caste-based reservations.

2. What is the Admission Procedure under RTE?

One needs to apply online and the department releases selected aspirants list via a lottery system. The selected aspirants’ list will be displayed on the official website.

3. What is the Official Website of RTE Chattisgarh?

If you want to check for info regarding RTE Admissions, you can refer to the Official Site eduportal.cg.nic.in/RTE

4. Where can I get the RTE Chattisgarh Online Application Form?

You can get the RTE Application Form Link on our page or even from the Official Site to get Admission.

5. How to Apply Online for RTE Admission in Chattisgarh?

Navigate to the official portal set by Chattisgarh Board and complete the student registration. Fill in the Registration Form with details required and save the application number for further use. By doing so you can successfully complete the Online Application Process for RTE Admission.
