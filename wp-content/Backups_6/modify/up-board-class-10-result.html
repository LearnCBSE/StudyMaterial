UP Board Result Class 10th 2024 | Results will be Available @ www.upresults.nic.in

January 17, 2024 by LearnCBSE Online

UP Board Result Class 10th 2024: The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) will declare the results of the UP Board Intermediate examination on 27th April 2024 at 12.30 PM . The date for the release of results was expected before, on 25th April but due to some reason, it has been postponed till 27th April 2024 at 12:30 PM. The results are ready but will be declared only after permission from the state administration.

State Board Results

Candidates who want to check UP Board Highschool result 2024 have to enter their Roll Number as mentioned on their UP Board Class 10 Admit Card. The exam for UP board class 10 was conducted in the month of February from 22nd Feb to 9th March 2024. Almost around 32 lacs students registered this year for the high school board examination.

Here in this article, we are going to give complete details for the results of the Uttar Pradesh 10th class exam conducted this year in 2024. Keep reading with us.

UP Board Result 2024 Class 10th

As per the notification announced by the government of Uttar Pradesh, the results for UP board class 10 2024 exams, will be released on 27th April, mostly. Let us give here a general description of the high school exam.

  • Examination Name-UP Board Class 10 Exam 2024
  • Conducting Body-UP State Board or Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Siksha Parishad
  • Examination Mode-Offline (Pen and Paper)
  • Exam Dates-22nd Feb to 9th March 2024
  • Exam Results-To be announced
  • Official Website – www.upresults.nic.in .

UP Board Class 10 Results – Steps to Check

UP Board Result 2024 Class 10th will be declared on the official website and through online mode only. Although, students can also check for the results via SMS mode. Later, students can collect their 10th mark sheet from their respective schools as per mentioned by the school. Below here are the steps to check the results for the high school exam conducted this year in 2024, through online mode.

Online Mode

  • Firstly, the candidate has to visit the official website of Uttar Pradesh, www.upresults.nic.in.
  • Click on the link given for UP Board 10th class results 2024.
  • A new webpage will get opened where you will be asked to enter the 7 digit roll number.
  • Enter your 7-digit roll number and click on submit button.
  • The result of class 10th UP board exam will be displayed on your screen.
  • Download the scorecard for your reference.

SMS Mode to check the results

  • Candidates can directly know their results for UP Board class 10th via SMS mode.
  • They just have to type UP10<space>ROLLNUMBER (Enter your roll number) in their messaging application of the phone and send it to the specified number as per mentioned by UPSMP.
  • The candidate will receive the result through an SMS.

Scorecard of UP Board Class 10 results carry following details

  • Roll Number
  • Name of the Student
  • School Name and Code
  • Parent’s Name
  • Marks obtained in each subject (Practical and Theory)
  • Total Marks Scored
  • Grade as per marks obtained
  • Result Status(Passed/Failed)

UP Board Class 10th Results Rechecking

If the students are not satisfied with their marks, they can choose for revaluation or rechecking of their results, declared by the board. To apply for it, students will have to download the scrutiny form available in the Uttar Pradesh online website. The revaluation fee is Rs. 100 for each subject and an extra Rs. 100 per practical paper.

Students have to send the filled up scrutiny form along with fee deposit slip and a self-addressed stamped envelope within 30 days of the announcement of UP Board class 10th result 2024. The form should be sent in the favor of the Deputy Secretary of the regional office of Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad. After the submission of the form, the UP state board authority personnel will recheck the answer sheets for the subject, which has to re-evaluated.  Again the cross-checked results will be sent to the student’s address.

UP Board 10th Results Some Important Facts
