Study MBBS in Russia for Indian Students 2023-24: Eligibility, Fee, Admission, Duration

September 8, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Study MBBS in Russia for Indian Students: Russia is the best choice to study MBBS. It has almost 57 medical universities. Most of the Indian students show interest in studying MBBS here as they offer high subsidised fees. The top universities offer high-quality of education with affordable fees.

The Medical Council of leading countries like the USA, the UK, and WHO recognizes all Russian universities offering an MBBS course. Here we are covering more details about study MBBS in Russia eligibility, admission, duration, fees, scholarship, and needed documents.

Why to Study MBBS in Russia?

These are the best reasons why Indian students prefer to study MBBS in Russia rather than in other countries.

  • The medical universities located in Russia are recognized by the National Medical Commission i.e. Medical Council of India (MCI).
  • The affordable tuition fees are also the main reason. The starting annual fee is INR 3 to 4 lakhs.
  • Medical education in Russia is taught in English to Indian students.
  • Affordable cost of living in Russia.
  • Hassle-free admission process without taking any entrance exams like IELTS and TOEFL.
  • No donations and capitation fees
  • Comfortable hostels are available with hygienic Indian mess facilities.
  • Local Russian language is taught during the MBBS in Russia course for smooth communication with Russian patients.
  • Top medical universities in Russia offer various specialisations to Indian students.
  • Russian medical universities have good faculty that includes research analysts, scientists, and doctors of science.
  • Russia is a safe country for pursuing MBBS as the universities have strong and well-developed security systems.
  • FMGE/NExT coaching is provided by Indian doctors and professors at Russian medical universities.

MBBS in Russia vs MBBS in India

This table compares the difference between studying MBBS in Russia vs Studying MBBS in India.

MBBS in Russia MBBS in India
The cost of studying medicine in Russia is low. The cost of studying MBBS in India is high.
No donations are needed. Donations are needed to get admission to the MBBS course.
Russian medical universities focus more on enhancing the practical skills and knowledge of their students. Indian medical colleges focus more on theoretical knowledge and practical skills training comes at a later stage.
The course duration is 6 years. The course duration is 5.5 years
MBBS program admissions are possible even with a low qualifying NEET score. MBBS admissions are not possible with a low NEET score.
The MBBS course fees in Russia range from INR 15 lakhs to INR 50 lakhs. The MBBS course fee in govt medical colleges is between INR 11,000 and INR 7.5 lakh and in private colleges is INR 25 lakhs to INR 1 crore.
The medium of instruction is mainly English but few universities teach Russian for communicating smoothly with patients during practice. English is the only medium of teaching MBBS in India.
Russian medical universities make special provisions for accommodation for Indian students in case of limited seats. There are a fixed number of MBBS seats in India allowing only a significant lot of students to get admission to them.
Students who have finished MBBS in Russia can pursue PG courses of their choice in Russia. Students who have completed MBBS in India can choose MD/ MS/ Diploma courses. Moreover, they have to quality NEET PG exams like PIGMER, JIPMER PG, AIIMS PG and so on.

MBBS Study in Russia

MBBS in Russia is a specialist degree program with a duration of 6 years. The goal of this general medicine program is to equip doctors with behavioural and clinical skills that can treat urgent and life-threatening conditions along with prevention, treatment, diagnosis, and rehabilitation.

After MBBS, the students can pursue higher education or choose careers in education, science, and other fields. The available specializations to study MBBS in Russia are here:

  • Microbiology
  • Neurology
  • Gynecology
  • Immunology
  • Pediatrics
  • Radiology
  • Surgery
  • Psychiatry
  • Ophthalmology
  • Immunology
  • Nursing
  • Biochemistry

Eligibility Criteria to Study Medical Education in Russia

These are the eligibility to study MBBS degree in Russian universities.

  • The student’s age should be more than 17 years.
  • The students must have scored a minimum of 50% marks in their 12th class from the science stream.
  • The students must have scored quality marks in the NEET exam.
  • They should have a valid passport with a student visa.

Russian Student Visa

Student visa is issued to international students who want to enter the Russian Federation to study at Russian universities. These visas are issued based on an invitation from FMS of Russia or a visa directive from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation. Students can also apply for a visa at the Russian Embassy. The needed documents for the student visa are here:

  • Valid international passport
  • Application form
  • Invitation to enter Russia
  • Photograph
  • HIV-negative status document/certificate
  • Parental consent to travel must be verified by a notary public for applicants under the age of 18.

MBBS Admission Process in Russia

Getting admission for MBBS in Russian universities is simple. Here are the detailed steps for MBBS admission process in Russia for Indian Students.

  • Initial step is to select the university.
  • Fill the application form carefully.
  • You will get an admission letter from the university.
  • Pay the enrollment fees.
  • Also, pay the MBBS fees and apply for the visa.
  • Get the visa and fly off to Russia.

Needed Documents

The list of documents needed to pursue MBBS in Russia is here:

  • Passport with a minimum of 18 months validity.
  • 10th and 12th certificates
  • Date of birth certificate
  • Passport size photographs
  • Admission letter from the Russian Medical University
  • Documents of HIV test
  • Proof of legalization of all of your documents from any Russian Embassy
  • Receipt of payment of the fees of visa
  • Receipt of university + 1st-year tuition fees

Russian Universities MBBS Curriculum

The detailed curriculum for MBBS in Russia is completed in 6 to 8 years. The following details are as per the European standards and the United States.

  • In the first 3 years, the theoretical concepts of medicine are taught to the students.
  • They are human biology such as pathology, physiology, microbiology, genetics, and microbiology.
  • Every year has 2 semesters.
  • In the last 3 years, students have to do internship along with the study of surgery, neurology, primary care medicine, gynaecology, paediatrics, psychiatry and emergency medicine.
  • The different teaching techniques used are seminars and small group discussions, assessments, lectures, clinical training, and practical and laboratory works.

Cost of Studying MBBS in Russia

Russia is one of the cheapest country to study medical education like MBBS, BDS, and so on. Since majority of MCI recognised Russian universities has started offering education in English. Russian universities ask international students to pay the semester wise course fees. The average MBBS semester fees in Russia for Indian students is INR 2,60,000 to INR 6,30,000.

Apart from the tuition fees and hostel fees, the major expenses will go into living in the country. Students have to manage the cost of monthly rent, food, transportation, books, internet charges, and others. They need INR 30,000 to 50,000 per month to manage these utilities.

Medical Education Scholarship in Russia

Russia is the primary preferance for the students who want to pursue MBBS. Here is the list of MBBS scholarships in Russia for Indian students:

  • The Government of Russian Federation Scholarships: It is a state-level scholarship offered to the students to study medical courses in Russia. It can cover the tuition fees, accommodation costs around 1300 to 1500 Rubles.
  • Medical Education Grants: Educational grants are offered by Russian Medical Universities to international students with good academic scores.
  • Benjamin A. Gilman Scholarships: Overseas students who study MBBS in Russia can apply for this scholarship.

Student Visa Process To Study MBBS in Russia

Students after getting the admission letter from the medical university have to apply for the student visa. Here is the step by step guide for the visa process.

  • Collect Needed Documents: Students have to gather all documents before applying for Visa.
  • Invitation Letter to Study in Russa: These invitation letters are issued by the Federal Migration Service of Russia and Ministry of Foreign Affairs to Russia.
  • Check Your Financial Ability: Students are advised to check whether you can afford the study cost or not. You can even take the education loan to study MBBS in abroad countries.
  • Apply for Universities: You can apply for a maximum of 6 universities but you need to qualify in the exam, interview, and under go the competitive selection process. After all, you are allowed to get a visa.
  • Wait for Visa: Your visa will be issued in 2 to 5 weeks.
