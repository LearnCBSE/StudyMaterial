Which Bank Gives Education Loan Without Collateral for Abroad? – How To Apply For Loan?

August 5, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Which Bank Gives Education Loan Without Collateral for Abroad?: Are you willing to study abroad but lack of security for an education loan? If yes, here you can check the list of banks offering education loan without collateral for abroad study. These banks offer better options with varying loan amounts and conditions to help the students financially.

Many public or private banks and NBFCs provide education loan without collateral but with different loan amounts. In this post, we will check which bank gives education loan without collateral for abroad, how to apply, and advantages of it.

What is Education Loan Without Collateral?

Education loans are of two types. They are secured loan and unsecured loan. Secured loan is with collateral and unsecured loan is without collateral.

A non-collateral education loan is taken without giving any collateral as a gaurantee to the lender or bank. Students can take the loan amount without any security like property or anything. They can take up to 50 lakhs for their education.

Eligibility Conditions for an Abroad Education Loan without Collateral

There are certain eligibility requirements that you should meet to ensure that you can get an unsecured education loan to study abroad. Some of the common parameters to consider under eligibility are along the lines:

  • Academic Profile of the Student: Banks does not see collateral as the primary factor to approve loan, your academic performance plays a key role. Your future academic performace is forecasted only through the past performances.
  • Selected Country, University and Course: All banks giving non-collateral education loans for abroad countries with a list of university and courses. All you need to check is whether your country, university and course are available in the list or not.
  • Co-applicant Finances: The co-applicant’s must have high CIBIL score and meet the prescribed requirements. They should also have a minimum income of INR 30,000 to INR 40,000 based on the lender.
  • Test Scores: The differest test scores like IELTS, GRE, GMAT, TOEFL, SAT, etc and the average of all of them are considered.

Documents Needed for an Unsecured Education Loan

Non-collateral education loan can cover your travel expenses, tuition fees, living expenses, study materials, and so on. The lenders provide easy study loan without collateral only if you have the following documents.

  • KYC documents
  • 2 passport size photographs of the applicant and co-applicant
  • Past 6 months bank statements
  • Copy of the admission letter from the university
  • Mark sheets of 10, 12, degree courses, and entrance exams.
  • Income proof of the guarantor or co-applicant.
