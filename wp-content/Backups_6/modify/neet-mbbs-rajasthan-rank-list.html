NEET MBBS Rajasthan Rank List 2019 (Released) | Check Rajasthan NEET 2019 Merit List From Here

July 2, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

NEET MBBS Rajasthan Rank List 2019: NEET MBBS Rajasthan Rank List to be released in last week of June. UG Medical and Dental Admission/ Counselling Board and Principal & Controller, SMS Medical College & Attached Hospitals, Jaipur will release NEET MBBS Rajasthan Merit List and is released in online in PDF Format. At the same time, second-round NEET MBBS Merit List will be released in the 3rd week of July 2019. Those students whose name is included in the merit list will be called for NEET Counselling.

Selected Candidates will get eligible for seat allotment in the Rajasthan MBBS Merit List. Rajasthan Merit List contains the information of candidates’registration ID, NEET ID, Name, Father’s name, domicile, category filled, category allotted, considered an additional category, NEET percentile, NEET All India Rank, and Rajasthan Combined State Merit Number details. The NEET MBBS Rajasthan Rank List 2019 is formed by selecting the eligible candidates from the NEET Merit List to the NEET Rajasthan Rank List.

NEET MBBS Rajasthan Rank List Tie Breaker

In case two students get the same rank in NEET MBBS, the tie is broken by considering the set conditions listed below

  • Candidates who gain more marks in Biology will be considered.
  • If the tie is not broken, a student who gains more marks in chemistry will be considered.
  • If the tie continues, the NEET will be preferred to those with fewer incorrect answers
  • If the above criteria fail to break the tie then the candidate who has more age will be taken.

NEET MBBS Rajasthan Counselling

Those students whose name is included in the merit list will be called for NEET MBBS Counselling Procedure which will be conducted in Online mode. Before counseling procedure starts candidates will have to choose their choice of seats. The seat allotment will be done on merit-cum-basis.
