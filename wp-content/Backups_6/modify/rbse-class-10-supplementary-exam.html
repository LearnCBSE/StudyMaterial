Rajasthan Class 10 Supplementary/Compartment Exam 2019 : Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE) Class 10th supplementary exam 2019 will be held most probably in the month of August. Read the article below to know more about the admit card, registration form, time table, etc of the RBSE class 10th supplementary exam 2019.

RBSE Class 10 Supplementary Application Form 2019

RBSE Class 10th supplementary application form will be available on the official website. All those candidates who could not pass the regular board exam can fill the application form for re-examination.

How To Download RBSE Class 10th Supplementary Exam 2019 Time Table?

The steps to download the time table are listed below

  • Step 1 – Visit the official website i.e rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Step 2 – Candidates are required click on the RBSE class 10th supplementary time table under the latest new section heading.
  • Step 3 – A new window opens with RBSE class 10th supplementary exam time table 2019 pdf.
  • Step 4 – Download the pdf and save it for future reference.

RBSE Class 10 Supplementary Admit Card 2019

RBSE class 10th supplementary admit card will be available on the official website of the board i.e rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in. All the candidates who could not pass the main exam in the first attempt will be granted the supplementary exam admit card.

How To Download RBSE Class 10th Supplementary Admit Card?

  • step 1- visit the official website i.e http://rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in/ .
  • Step 2- click on the admit card notification link under the latest news section.
  • Step 3-candidates are required to fill in all the information present on the admit card.
  • Step4- fil in the details like  Fill in the details such as Candidate’s Name, School of the Student, Date of Birth, Father Name and click on the submit button.
  • Step 5- RBSE class 10th supplementary admit card will be displayed on the screen.candidates are required to take out a copy of the admit card to take to the examination hall.

RBSE Class 10 Supplementary Result 2019

RBSE class 10th supplementary exam result will be declared most probably in the month of August (tentatively). Candidates need to fill in the details like name of the candidate, date of birth, roll number to check the result.

How To Download RBSE Class 10 Supplementary Result 2019?

  • Step 1 – Visit the official website i.e rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in
  • Step 2 – Candidates need to click on the  RBSE class 10th result link under the latest update section.
  • Step 3 – Enter the details like the name of the candidate, roll number, etc, and click on the submit button.
  • Step 4 – RBSE class 10th supplementary result 2019 will be displayed on the screen. Candidates are required to take a printout for future reference.
