WB HS Result Released @ wbresults.nic.in | Check WB Board Class 12 Results From Here

January 17, 2024 by LearnCBSE Online

WB HS Result 2024: The West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE) will declare the results of WB HS Class 12 in May 2024. The examination was held in February. The results will be declared online on the official website wbresults.nic.in. WB HS Result 2024 has been declared today on 27th May 2024. Read on to find out all the details about the results.

Steps to Check WB HS Results 2024 for Class 12

The results can be checked online by following the steps below

  1. Go to the official website wbresults.nic.in
  2. Click on “West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education”
  3. Enter your roll number and registration number
  4. Click on submit to generate the result
