RBSE Class 12 Result – How to Check RBSE Result 2020?

In order to search for RBSE Result 2020, the students will have to follow certain steps. The students will be able to check the result by using the link provided on our page. Otherwise, you can search for the result from the official website rajresults.nic.in. The first way is very simple to find the result. To check the result in a second way, you can refer to the steps given below:

  • Step – 1: Go to the official website of Rajasthan board i.e http://rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in/
  • Step – 2: As the second step, you will reach the homepage of the website. On the homepage, you will be able to see the various links of the result for the various classes. Here, you will have to click on the relevant link according to your class.
  • Step – 3: After g the link, you will be redirected to a new page. On that page, you will be able to enter the credentials. So, enter your roll number and then hit the “Submit” button.
  • Step – 4: Once, you will submit the details, your RBSE Result 2020 will appear to you. It will be available in the pdf format.
  • Step – 5: At last, you will be able to check the result. So, check your result and then take the printout of it for future use.

RBSE Supplementary Result 2020

Candidates who will not be able to pass the exams, Rajasthan Board will provide the second chance. The second chance is known as the supplementary exams. For the supply exams, the candidates will have to submit the application form. To fill the application form successfully, the students will have to submit the required application fee.

The exams for the supplementary process will be held in the month of August 2020. Last year, the supply results were declared on September 20 and September 23, 2018, for class 12 and class 10 respectively. So, the students will get the supply result for class 10 and class 12 in the third week of September 2020.

About Rajasthan Board

The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan manages all the activities of Rajasthan state. The board takes care of the activities of the educational field. It is well known as BSER. The board came into existence in the year, 1973. Firs, it was established in Ajmer in the year, 1961. In the present building, it is spread over 3,42,239 sq. km. There are total 6,000 schools affiliated to BSER situated in 32 cities.
