PSEB Class 12 Supplementary Exam 2019: PSEB was established in the year 1969. The board is responsible for each and every decision for the educational system of the state. It designs and regulates the course and curriculum of Punjab. It also maintains the education policies of the various schools which are affiliated to it. PSEB manages the schemes of secondary and sr. secondary status i.e for class 10th and 12th. It conducts the exams for those classes once a year in the month of March. After the completion of the exams, the board also releases the result. PSEB Class 12 Supplementary Exam Date sheet has been released on 4th July 2019.

How To Register for PSEB 12th Supplementary Exam 2019?

The registration form will tentatively open in June/July 2019. Register as soon as possible by following the below steps:

  1. Visit the official website (linked above).
  2. Click on “Online form for Matric/Senior secondary Supplementary exam (Re-appear/Compartment/Additional) July 2019” link.
  3. A new page will open.
  4. Click on “New user (Click here to register)”.
  5. Register using your roll number and other personal details.
  6. After online registration, submit the hard copy of the Attested Application Form and copy of challan at PSEB District Sub Office/Head Office as per schedule issued by Board.

How To Download PSEB Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2019?

The date sheet has not been released yet. As soon as it is, it is important that you plan accordingly and not waste any time. Follow the below steps to download it

  1. Visit the official website (linked above).
  2. Under the “latest news” section, look for the link saying “PSEB 12th Supplementary exam Date sheet 2019”.
  3. Click on it.
  4. Save and download the date sheet in PDF format.

PSEB 12th Supplementary Exam Admit Card

The admit card will be provided after registration. Note that you won’t be allowed to write the exam without your admit card. Hence, take a print out and keep it safe. Follow the below steps to download it from the official website:

  1. Visit the official website (linked above).
  2. Under the “latest news” section, look for the link saying “PSEB 12th Supplementary exam Admit 2019”.
  3. Click on it.
  4. Save and download the admit card in PDF format.
  5. Take a print out and don’t forget to take it with you to the exam hall.

How To Check The Results of PSEB Class 12 Supplementary Exam 2019?

The results will be released after the exams, tentatively in the month of October. You can check your results from the official website using your login details. Follow these steps to get your results

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. In the “Latest News” section, click on the link saying “PSEB 12th Supplementary result 2019”.
  3. A new window will open.
  4. Enter your Supplementary roll number and other details as required.
  5. Click on “Submit”.
  6. Your result will open in a new window.
  7. Download and take a print out of the same and keep it safe.
