KVPY Answer Key 2019 – How To Calculate KVPY Score?

KVPY Score will be calculated by using the below formula:

(Number of correct attempts in Part I x 1 – Number of incorrect attempts in Part I x 0.25) + (Number of correct attempts in Part II X 2 – Number of incorrect attempts in Part II X 0.50)
