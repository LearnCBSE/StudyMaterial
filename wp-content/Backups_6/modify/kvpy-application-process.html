KVPY 2019 Application Form (Out) | Dates, Pattern and Syllabus

July 8, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

KVPY Application Process 2019: KVPY Application Form has been released on 8th July 2019. Every year KVPY Exam will schedule on first Sunday of November, this year KVPY 2019 will conduct on November 3rd, 2019, and mode of exam will be online.

Direct Link to Apply for KVPY 2019 Exam

  • The KVPY consists of three streams like SA, SB, SX, Application form is available for all the three streams.
  • The application process will consist of three steps like registration, entering details and Payment.
  • Students who had met with eligibility can fill the KVPY application form in online mode, Check KVPY Eligibility Criteria 2019.
  • Students who have registered successfully for the scholarship program can download KVPY Admit Card from October 12 onwards.

SA Stream – Students applying for KVPY after Class XI have to apply under this stream.

SX Stream – Students applying after Class XII have to apply under this stream.

SB Stream – Students pursuing B.Sc. Basic Sciences (1 st year) must apply under this stream.

KVPY Registration – Instructions

  • A credit card/ debit card or net banking (application fee for General & OBC category is INR 1000 and INR 500 for SC/ST/PH/PWD).
  • Proper Internet Connection
  • A valid mobile number and phone number.

KVPY Eligibility Criteria 2019

Students can only apply for KVPY Exam, only after the checking of complete eligibility to appear in the examination.

  • Nationality: Students must be an Indian Nationals.
  • For Stream SA: Students, those studying in 11 th standards with science subjects & secured 75% aggregate marks (65% marks for reserved categories) in Mathematics & science subjects in Class 10 th can apply.
  • For Stream SX: Students who studying in class 12 th & score 75% marks (65% marks in case of SC/ST/PwD category) in aggregate in Mathematics & science in 10 th standards can also apply.
  • For Stream SB: Students, those studying in the 1 st year of UG course in basic sciences & score at least 60% marks (50% for reserved categories) are also eligible.

How to Apply for KVPY 2019?

Follow the step by step procedure to apply for KVPY Exam 2019. Students must visit the official website of KVPY, Now click on Login for Application.

Step – 1: Registration

  • Students have to register by filling the application form of KVPY 2019,
  • After that, they have to enter the details like basic details, including email id, mobile number, and date of birth and enter the captcha to complete registration.
  • After registration students will be given a registered user Id and password.

Note: KVPY will send important information to the registered mail id/phone number.

Step – 2: Filling KVPY Application Form 2019

Students have to login by using a login id and password to fill the application form of KVPY 2019. here students must fill the following details.

  • Personal Details: Candidates have to fill their basic details in the KVPY application form, such as gender, category, and address and then click on save and next
  • Academic Details: Students have to fill educational qualification details, name and address of the school or college and then click on save and next button
  • Uploading Documents and Exam Preference: Here, students have to select the language, preference for the test centers (maximum 4), and upload their photograph signature. Now candidates can submit the application form after preview.

Note: Before applying for KVPY 2019, students must check KVPY eligibility criteria and other important instructions and keep the required documents ready before proceeding to fill KVPY application form 2019

Step – 3: KVPY Application Fee

  • Application Fee can be paid through online only.
  • Through online mode, candidates can make payment through credit card/debit card/net banking.
  • After the completion of Fee, Payment Candidates can check the updated status of the KVPY application form 2019 after logging in.
