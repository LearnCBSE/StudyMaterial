UK Board Class 10 Supplementary Exam 2019 | Registration Form, Result, Time Table, Admit Card

June 22, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

UK Board Class 10 Supplementary Exam 2019: UK Board 10 Supplementary Exam dates are out. Students can now start preparing for the supplementary exam. UK board is conducting the supplementary exam for the students who could not pass the main board exam. Students that have failed in the 10th exam in March/April have another chance to pass through UK board 10th board supplementary exam. Here students can check UK board 10th supplementary exam structure, schedule, application fee, and other details below in the article. After the UK board 10th results are declared, the candidates that have failed the exam can check their exam dates for the supplementary exam.

State Board Supplementary Exams

The Uttarakhand board of school education have published the notification for UK board 10th supplementary exam 2019 on their official website which is ubse.uk.gov.in. Through this website, candidates can download the UK board 10th supplementary exam date sheet 2019 online in the form of a PDF. To appear for the supplementary exam, candidates need to finish their UK board 10th supplementary exam application form. It is advisable that for the candidates to fill their UK board 10th supplementary exam application form under the guidance of school authorities.

Additionally, candidates that have filled their application form will be provided with UK board 10th supplementary exam admit card. Without this admit card, no student will be allowed to sit in the exam hall. The UK board 10th supplementary exam result 2019 will be released by the Uttarakhand board in August 2019. For results, candidates can check it online on their official website which is results.nic.in.

UK Board 10th Supplementary Exam Time Table 2019

The Uttarakhand board will release the UK board 10th supplementary exam time table on their official website. Candidates can download this time table as a reference in the form of PDF. It is announced that the UK board 10th supplementary exam is going to be conducted in July, tentatively.

How To Download UK Board Class 10 Supplementary Exam Time Table?

  • Step 1: Go to the official website of UK board from your desktop which is ubse.uk.gov.in.
  • Step 2: On this website, on the right sidebar menu, you will find an examination scheme option.
  • Step 3: In this option, look out for UK board 10th supplementary exam time table 2019 window and click on it.
  • Step 4: In the new tab of your browser, UK board 10th supplementary exam time table will appear.
  • Step 5: It is advisable for the candidates to take a printout of this time table and start preparing for the supplementary exam.

UK Board Class 10 Supplementary Exam Registration Form 2019

The Uttarakhand board will release the UK board 10th supplementary exam registration form 2019 online on their official website. Candidates can apply for this application form through their online portal. It is important to fill this application form within the deadline and under the guidance of school authorities.

How To Download UK Board 10th Supplementary Exam Registration Form 2019?

  • Step 1: Go to the official website of UK board from your desktop.
  • Step 2: On the homepage, there is a registration option on the left side of the menu.
  • Step 3: The candidates will be asked to enter their credentials like user ID and password for logging into the portal.
  • Step 4: After the required details are entered, candidates have to pay the application fee and complete the application process.
  • Step 5: It is advisable to takeout a print copy of the application form for any future purposes.

UK Board 10th Supplementary Exam Admit Card 2019

Uttarakhand board releases the UK board 10th supplementary exam admit card 2019 through their official website. School authorities can download this admit card and provide it to their respective students.

How To Download UK Board Class 10 Supplementary Exam Admit Card 2019?

  • Step 1: Go to the official website of UK board from your desktop.
  • Step 2: On the homepage, there is a admit card option on the left side of the menu.
  • Step 3: Lookout for UK board 10th supplementary exam admit card 2019 link and click on it.
  • Step 4: The school authorities will be asked to enter the credentials like user ID and password for logging into the portal.
  • Step 5: School authorities cand download the admit card and provide it to the students with an authorized stamp and signature.
  • Step 6: It is advisable to takeout a print copy of the application form for any future purposes.

UK Board 10th Supplementary Exam Result 2019

The UK board will announce their UK board 10th supplementary exam result 2019 on the official website in August 2019.

How To Download Uk Board 10th Supplementary Exam Result 2019?

  • Step 1: Go to the official website of UK board from your desktop.
  • Step 2: On the homepage, there is a result on the left side of the menu.
  • Step 3: The candidates will be asked to enter their credentials like user ID and password for logging into the portal.
  • Step 4: After the required details are entered, the UK board 10th supplementary exam result will appear in the new tab.
  • Step 5: It is advisable to takeout a print copy of the application form for any future purposes.
