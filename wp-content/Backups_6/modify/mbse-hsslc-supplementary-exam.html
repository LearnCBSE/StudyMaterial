How To Apply for MBSE HSSLC Compartment Exam?

For those students who have passed but failed in any one subject in the Class 12th 2019 board exam, have to apply for the Supplementary Exam through the official website.

  • Go to the official website, www.mbse.edu.in.
  • Click on the link given for MBSE HSSLC Compartment/Supplementary Exam 2019.
  • Enter the roll number and registration number.
  • The application form will appear on the screen.
  • Fill the required details and click on submit button.
  • Download the filled application form and keep it for reference.

MBSE HSSLC Compartment Exam Time Table

To download the routine for HSSLC supplementary exam 2019 conducted by Mizoram Board, follow the below steps;

  • Go to the official website for the time table- http://www.mbse.edu.in/
  • Click on the Examination link at the top of the page.
  • Then click High School under Routine.
  • Students have to click on the MBSE HSSLC Supplementary Exam 2019 link.
  • Time Table will appear on the new window in PDF format.
  • Download the Time Table and keep it for future use.

MBSE HSSLC Compartment Exam Admit Card

  • The admit card for the HSSLC Supplementary exam 2019, conducted by the Mizoram Board of School Education, will be available in June 2019, by the Board.
  • The exams are going to be conducted in the month of July 2019, tentatively.
  • The admit card cannot be downloaded online.
  • Students have to reach their respective schools to get their admit card.
  • The school authorities will provide the admit card with a signature and official stamp.
  • Please note if there are any discrepancies present in the admit card.
  • The student has to bring the admit card in the exam center along with him/her, failing which they will not be allowed to appear for the exam.

MBSE HSSLC Compartment Exam Results

The results for HSSLC/12th supplementary exam 2019, conducted by Mizoram Board of School Education, will be declared in July 2019, after the conduct of examinations. The exams are going to be conducted in June 2019. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, www.mbse.edu.in.The level of the exam will be the State Board level.

The official link to check the compartment exam results is www.mbse.edu.in. Students who want to check the results for HSSLC supplementary exam 2019, follow the given steps

  • Go to the Mizoram Board official website
  • Click on the result section.
  • A new webpage will get open
  • Click on the link given for HSSLC Compartment/Supplementary Exam 2019 results.
  • Students are required to enter the roll number and registration number mentioned in the admit card.
  • Mizoram HSSLC Compartment Exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.
