Gujarat Class 12 Board Results 2024 – Steps to Check

The results can be checked online by following the steps below

  1. Go to the official website of GSEB gseb.org
  2. Click on “Gujarat Board Class 12 Results”
  3. A login window will appear on the screen, where you have to enter your “Name” and “GSEB 12th Roll Number.”
  4. Click on submit to generate the result
  5. The GSEB HSC Result will appear on the screen in PDF form.
  6. Find your roll number in the list and generate the result

Scorecard of Gujarat Class 12 Board results consist of following details

  1. Name of the applicant
  2. Roll number of the applicant
  3. Registration number
  4. Mother’s name
  5. Father’s name
  6. School
  7. Category of the applicant
  8. Subjects
  9. Applicant’s stream
  10. Marks in theory subjects
  11. Marks in practical subjects
  12. Grade in the subjects
  13. Total marks obtained by the applicants in all subjects
  14. Status of the result
  15. Overall grade obtained by the applicant
