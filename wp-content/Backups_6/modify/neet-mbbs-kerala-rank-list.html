NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List 2019 (Released) | Check Kerala MBBS Merit List Here

June 30, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List 2019: Commissioner of Entrance Examinations (CEE) will release Kerala MBBS NEET Merit List 2019. NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List 2019 has been released on 26 June 2019. 85% of seats are given to state quota MBBS and BDS seats will be compiled based on NEET Score of students. Around 3182 MBBS and 2077 BDS seats are available in various medical and dental colleges of Kerala. Those candidates who fulfill NEET Eligibility criteria for Kerala and have filled the admission from within the dates which are released by the authority.

NEET Merit List

Check Kerala Merit List Here

NEET MBBS Kerala merit list 2019 will include the name of the candidate, rank, category, NEET score, and category rank. Students whose names are included in NEET MBBS Kerala Merit List will be eligible for to appear in NEET Counseling Procedure for seat selection as per the merit position. The allotment seats will be done strictly based on candidate’s merit position in the rank list, choice opted, availability of seats, and other factors like reservation and eligibility.

In 2019, around 114214 students have registered for NEET from Kerala. Around 108907 students appeared for the exam out of which 72682 students qualified in the exam. NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List 2019 for 85% State Quota Seats

It is important to know about Kerala NEET MBBS Merit List 2019:

  1. CEE, Kerala will publish Kerala NEET MBBS Rank list 2019 on its official website.
  2. State Merit List will be prepared on the basis of marks secured by the students in NEET 2019.
  3. Those students who have registered in NEET 2019, NEET MBBS Rank List will be published for all eligible and registered students.
  4. Candidates will be called for NEET Counselling on the basis of Rank List which is released by CEE, Kerala.
  5. 85% of Seats are covered by state quota for the state wise applicants.
  6. 15% of seats are covered by All India Quota (AIQ). for applicants across the country for the government and Dental Colleges.

How To Download NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List 2019?

Kerala NEET MBBS Rank List will be released in PDF format. Here are the steps to download NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List 2019:

  • Step – 1: Visit the official website of cee.kerala.gov.in.
  • Step – 2: Click on the link provided to know the results of NEET Kerala Rank List 2019, NEET Rank List will be displayed on Screen in PDF Format.
  • Step – 3: Now, Search for your name and Registration Number by pressing Ctrl + F.
  • Step – 4: Now download the NEET Rank List 2019 and keep it with you for future reference.

NEET Inter-se-Merit

When two or more students will score the same marks in NEET Exam 2019, CEE Kerala will implement the Inter-se-merit to break the tie between students who score the same marks. Find the Tie-breaker method from the below steps

  • Students who get high marks in Biology will be considered first.
  • If the tie still exists applicants with higher marks in Chemistry will be considered.
  • If the tie still raises, students who have less number of incorrect answers will get a chance.
  • Even after this tie exists, candidates older in age will be considered.

NEET MBBS Kerala Rank List – NEET Counselling

After releasing of Kerala MBBS NEET Merit List, Candidates whose names are there in the list will be called for NEET Counselling 2019 . Selected students will have to fill their choices of colleges and Courses in Online Mode. Kerala MBBS admission will be granted to the candidates on the basis of their merit rank, availability of seats, reservation criteria, and choices filled.

The NEET Counselling comprises 6 steps as below

  1. Registration
  2. Fees Payment
  3. Choice Locking
  4. Seat Allotment Result
  5. Report to the College
  6. Payment of Registration Fee
