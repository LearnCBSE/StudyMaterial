This article starts with an explanation of what is meant by a collateral loan, a brief about education loans without collateral, required documents, eligibility conditions, features & benefits, and the procedure on How to apply for an Education Loan for Abroad Studies without Collateral.

What is Collateral Loan?

A collateral loan is a loan that is sanctioned against specific valuable assets as a security to the lender for the borrowed loan amount. Study loans are of two types such as secured loans and unsecured loans.

A secured loan is a loan with collateral and unsecured means a loan with no collateral. If you want to avail of an education loan without collateral then there are numerous banks and lender options to offer. Simply get into this guide and read the shared data without fail.

Difference Between Secured & Unsecured Education Loans

Knowing the basic difference between the abroad education loan with and without collateral is important for students who are planning to study courses in foreign universities. Check the below table for deep knowledge:

Basis of difference Education loan with collateral (Secured) Education loan without collateral (Unsecured)
Eligibility Criteria Relaxed as the collateral serves as security The lender must be strict and base their decision on the borrower’s creditworthiness and the co-applicants financial stability.
Requirement of Collateral Property or fixed deposit, as security for the loan Collateral, is not necessary, but a financial co-applicant may be required
Interest Rates May be low due to the security pledged Interest rate is higher because of no security
Loan Amount Generally higher depending on the value of the collateral Lower than the collateral loan amount
Loan Processing Time It takes more time because of lengthy documentation, property valuation, etc. It takes less time because of relaxed requirements.

Benefits of an Abroad Education Loan with No Collateral

There are various advantages of getting an education loan without collateral. Some of them are listed below:

Overseas Student Loan Without Collateral – Eligibility Criteria

For availing any public or private or government-provided education loans to study abroad, students should fulfill some required eligibility conditions, which are mentioned below:

  • Students should score more than 300 in the GRE exam, to get an admission in USA country universities.
  • If you want to study in other countries, then scoring above 6 or 6.5 bands in IELTS exam is must.
  • Co-applicant financial income must have above the loan amount.
  • After all debts and expenses, annual income must be more than 4 lakhs.
  • Applied student parents need to own a resident house.

List of Needed DocumentsTo Apply For Study Loan without Collateral

  • University offer letter mentioning tuition fees
  • Academic documents
  • Employment documents
  • KYC documents of both applicant and co-applicant
  • Address proof along with the electric bill
  • Two Photographs

For salaried co-applicant, any of the income proofs should be provided:

  • ITR
  • Form 16
  • Salary slip
  • Salary account detail

For business owners, income proof should be provided and it can be anyone from the below:

  • ITR
  • Profit & loss account
  • Balance sheet
  • Business registration certificate
  • Business current account
  • Applicant’s saving account details
