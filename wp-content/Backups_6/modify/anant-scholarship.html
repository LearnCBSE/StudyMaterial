Anant Scholarship 2023 Last Date | Important Dates, Application Process, Eligibility Criteria

November 2, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Anant Scholarship 2023-24: Anant Scholarship falls under the purview of the Anant Education Initiative, a CSR initiative of the Infinity Group. The primary aim of this scholarship is to empower education and remove economic hurdles that stand in the way of education.

Anant Scholarship is awarded to meritorious students from economically weaker sections of society, irrespective of their caste, creed, or faith. Moreover, poverty is one of the biggest banes of society. One of the most effective ways to eliminate poverty is by providing education. Moreover, education improves employment rates, which negates the effects of poverty to some extent.

Anant Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates MUST be studying in schools located in West Bengal
  • Candidates must have secured not less than 70% aggregate in their class 10 and class 12.
  • The candidate’s total family from all sources should not exceed INR 5,000 per month
  • Madhyamik/ICSE /CBSE/Higher Secondary/ISC/College Students are eligible for the scholarship.
  • Applicable to all undergraduate courses

Anant Scholarship Amount

  • Anant Scholarship disperses an amount of INR 6,000 in monthly installments of INR 500.
  • Special preference will be given to deserving physically challenged students.
  • 50% of the scholarships are reserved for girl students

Anant Scholarship Important Dates

The last date to submit the scholarship application is 31st July 2023.

Distribution to students is December 2023.

Anant Scholarship Application Procedure

  • Students can apply for a scholarship from Anant Education Initiative’s official website. Click here to apply.
  • Note: the candidates must possess a Google account to fill and submit the online form.
  • Alternatively, an offline form can be downloaded from the website and sent by mail after duly filling all details. The address to send the application is as follows:

Anant Scholarship Selection Procedure

The selection process involves the following processes:

  • Anant Education Initiative selects deserving candidates from its pool of applicants
  • Special preferences are given to deserving physically handicapped students.
  • Anant’s call center initiates them for interviews
  • Selected students will complete banking formalities, with the Initiative opening an individual bank account for each student
  • The scholarship amount will then be disbursed to these accounts.

Important Documents for Anant Scholarship

Please ensure that candidates possess these documents before moving forward with the application process.

  • Attested Copy of Antyodaya Card or a BPL Card/OR Income Certificate (in original) issued by  Panchayat Pradhan or the Councillor
  • Attested Copy of Mark Sheet of CBSE/Madhyamik /ICSE/CBSE+/Higher Secondary/ISC/Others.
  • Attested Copy of Proof of Residence or Ration Card.
  • Proof of Date of Birth – Birth Certificate or Attested Copy of Class 10 and 12 Admit Card
  • Original Income Certificate.
  • Disability certificate (in the case of disabled candidates)
  • Aadhar Card (attested copy).

In case any of the above-prescribed documents are missing from the application form, the institution will cancel the candidature of the applicant without any prior notice.
