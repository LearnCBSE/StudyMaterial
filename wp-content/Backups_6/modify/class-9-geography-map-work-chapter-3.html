INDIA: Drainage and Lakes

This map shows various rivers that flow across the states, national capital, union territories and international boundaries. It also shows the major lakes of India.

Question 1.
Locate and label the following items on the given map
1 The strait separating Sri Lanka and India
2 Salt water lake in Kerala
3 Largest fresh water lake in India
4 A group of islands lying East of the mainland of India
5 Islands group lying close to Malabar coast and Kerala
or
Islands in the South-West of the mainland of India

Answer:
1. Palk strait
2. Vembanad lake
3. Wular lake
4. Andaman and Nicobar islands
5. Lakshadweep
