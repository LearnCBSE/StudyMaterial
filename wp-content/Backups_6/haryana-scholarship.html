Haryana Scholarship 2023-24 | Scholarship for SC, ST, OBC, Eligibility, Rewards, Application Process and Documents

October 31, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Haryana Scholarship 2023-2024: Haryana Scholarship is an initiative of the Welfare of Scheduled Caste & Backward Classes Department, Government of Haryana. This scholarship is offered to all meritorious students of the affiliated colleges of the state every year. This scholarship was initiated with the aim of encouraging SC/ST/Backward class students of the state for pursuing education. This scholarship offers financial support to the SC/ST/OBC/ Minority and merit students of Haryana state. Haryana Scholarship online form 2023-2024 last date is 8th May 2023.

SC,

Dr. Ambedkar Medhavi Chhatra Yojana 2023 : This scholarship has been implemented by the Welfare of Scheduled Caste & Backward Classes Department, Haryana. This scholarship provides monthly financial aid to the meritorious students of the SC and Backward Classes of the state.

> Haryana Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students: This scholarship offered to the OBC students pursuing post-matric classes under Govt of India. This scholarship amount ranges from Rs. 160 to Rs. 750 per month in Post- Matric to Post-Graduate classes. However, this scheme has a family income criteria. The family income of OBC students should not be more than Rs. 1 lakh per annum.

Haryana Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students: This scholarship offered to the SC students pursuing post-matric classes under Govt of India. The amount of scholarship varies from Rs. 230 to Rs. 1,200 per month in Post- Matric to Post-Graduate classes. However, this scheme has a family income criteria. The family income of SC students should not be more than Rs. 2.50 lakh per annum.

Haryana NMMS Scholarship

National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship ( NMMS Scholarship ) is a centrally sponsored scholarship. This scholarship offers financial assistance to meritorious students of economically weaker sections. So that they can continue their studies after class 8th. Students pursuing class 8th can apply for this scholarship. Students have to appear in MAT (Mental Ability Test) and the SAT (Scholastic Aptitude Test) test to get selected for this scholarship.

Haryana NTSE Scholarship

Students pursuing class 10th can apply for the NTSE Haryana scholarship. National Talent Search Examination (NTSE) conducted in 2 stages. Students who will clear the Haryana NTSE will have to appear in NTSE stage2 conducted by NCERT. Haryana NTSE conducted on November 2023.
