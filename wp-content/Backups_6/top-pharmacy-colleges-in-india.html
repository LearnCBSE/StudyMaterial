Top Pharmacy Universities in India: Admission Process

I know you all are looking for top pharamacy colleges in india for reviewing them and selecting the best ones as a choice during the admission for your eligible courses. But, Admissions in top universities is not at all easy to get. Students need to be eligible with the college requirements like qualification, minimum grip in core subjects etc. If students satisfy their requirements then only they can apply for the entrance test which will be accepted by that particular colleges.

Some of the top entrance exams conducted for admission to the graduation, post Graduation & diploma courses in India’s Top Pharma Colleges are GPAT, UPSEE, and AU AIMEE. Here, we have given eligible details for all courses that you need for admission at India’s top pharmacy universities.

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharma):

Most of the colleges offer this Diploma in Pharmacy Course as per the performance in the related entrance exam, often followed by the personal interviews round, wherein their general aptitude for the course is tested. D. Pharma Admission Process commonly differs from college to college. A few institutes also offer direct admission based on the candidate’s performance at the 10+2 level.

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharma):

For admission to the B Pharmacy courses, candidates need to pass class 12th with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics/ Biology. Students have to secure a min of 50% marks in the qualifying examination. Aspirants who pass the 3-year (D.Pharm) are also eligible for taking admission to B Pharmacy Course.

Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharma):

M Pharmacy Admission Process may vary from institute to institute. Most of the colleges or Institutions intake students based on marks scored in GPAT. After that, they have to undergo robust counseling and personal interview. Some of the institutes choose candidates based on their merit i.e, the marks obtained in one’s bachelor’s degree in the same field. Some colleges may also conduct their private entrance exams for the admissions.
