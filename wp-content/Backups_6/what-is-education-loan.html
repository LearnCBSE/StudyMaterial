What is Education Loan? – How Does Education Loan Work? – Process, Interest Rates, Eligibility

August 8, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

What is Education Loan?: Students who are passionate about their education but economically backward can avail the support to study & fulfill their dreams with the help of education loans.

Domestic Education Loan: This loan is specifically for those students who want to study their course in India. If the applicant is having the Indian admission form then only he/she is eligible to apply for the education loan under this type.

Overseas Education Loan or Study Abroad Education Loan: Students who want to pursue their desired course in a foreign institution, should apply for an education loan under this type. They can be offered airfare, accommodation, tuition fee, and other benefits to study abroad after fulfilling all the eligibility criteria.

Check the guide on which bank gives education loans without collateral for abroad and gain some knowledge.

academic

Third-Party Guarantee: A bank employee or a home bank should provide the guarantee letter for sanctioning the education loan to the student.

Loan Against Property, Deposits, and Securities: To get a loan for pursuing your studies, you should provide a guarantee by agricultural land, residential land, flat, house, and others, fixed deposit certificates, recurring deposits, gold deposits, bonds, debentures, and equity shares.

careers

Undergraduate (UG) Loans: Undergraduate Loans are another type of education loan where students get financial help to pursue their UG degrees.

Postgraduate (PG) Loans: Students who got finished their UG Courses and desired to pursue their postgraduate program in India can apply for a study under this type.

Career Development Loans: Applicants who are working hard in corporate jobs, desired to level up their skills, and reach the top in their career can take help from this loan. With this loan type, they can pause their job and continue to pick up professional courses and training for better employment opportunities.

1st: Offline Application Process

  • First choose the lender that you want to apply for a loan.
  • If you choose a bank, get the types of education loan details from the nearest branch. Choose a suitable loan type and fill out the application form. Along with that, know the documents required for submission and complete the process in person.
  • If you choose another lender, discuss the details by visiting their branch, or else call the lender and discuss the loan types, terms, and application process.

2nd: Online Application Process

  • One of the most convenient methods to apply for an education loan is by applying online. You can apply to study loan online in two ways.
  • Initially, the simple way is to download the application form online from valid sources or at the official website.
  • Fill out the loam form and submit it by attaching the other required papers too.
  • Second method is to apply by providing the requested information and then the lender will directly come to you for discussing the loan terms before applying it.

  • The benefits provided by Section 80E of the Income Tax Act are tax deductions on the amount of interest paid toward education loans.
