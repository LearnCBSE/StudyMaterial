New CBSE Date Sheet for Class 10 and Class 12 (Released) | Download CBSE Time Table from Here

March 11, 2022 by LearnCBSE Online

CBSE Date Sheet Class 10 & 12 2023 : CBSE Class 10 & 12 exams are scheduled to be conducted from February 15, 2023. The board will release the CBSE Class 10, 12 Date Sheet 2023 for both minor and major subjects soon. According to the latest notification released by CBSE, the CBSE 10 & 12 Practical exams will start from January 1, 2023. It is important for students to follow the board exam timetable and prepare for the exams accordingly.

The CBSE Class 10, 12 Date Sheet 2023 includes subject names, timing, exam dates, and other details. The CBSE Examination Controller announced that the board examinations for classes 10 and 12 will be held in an offline mode. The CBSE exam date sheet will be released by the board on December 2022. Continue reading to learn more about the timetables for the Class 10 and 12 Board exams.

CBSE Date Sheet Class 10 & 12 2023: Overview

The officials of CBSE have announced the exam dates for Classes 10 and 12 for board exams. According to the announcement the CBSE 2023 Theory Exam will commence on February 10th and 12th, 2023. The exam date sheet is available on the official website for students to download. Let us look at the overview of CBSE Class 10 and Class 12 exam 2023 below:

CBSE Class 10 & 12 Practical Exams: Details

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) released a notification that has asked all the schools under CBSE Board to conduct practical exams for Classes 10, 12 from January 1. The last date of these practical exams will be based on the last date of the theory papers for that respective class. The last date of practical exam is 10 days before the last examination for that respective class. The Board will not entertain any requests for further extension in dates from schools.

How to download CBSE Class 10, 12 Date Sheet 2023?

Below are the instructions for downloading the CBSE Class 10, 12 Date Sheet 2023. Students can easily get the Class 10 and 12 date sheets by following these steps:

  • Step 1: Go to the official website of CBSE – https://www.cbse.gov.in/
  • Step 2: Click on ‘Academic Website’
  • Step 3: Click the link that reads CBSE 12th date sheet 2023’ PDF and CBSE 10th date sheet 2023 Download’.
  • Step 4: Once the pdf page of the date sheet appears on the screen, take a printout and save it for further use.

CBSE Class 10 & 12 Exam Pattern

Below we have provided some of the important points regarding the CBSE Class 10 & 12 exam pattern:

  1. CBSE exam will be in MCQs (Multiple Choice Questions) format.
  2. The CBSE Class 10, 12 question papers will have a fixed number of questions.
  3. The CBSE Class 10, 12 exams would be of 2 hours, and students will have to fill the OMR sheets provided by boards to schools.
  4. The CBSE will decide to conduct the exams online/offline depending on the COVID-19 situation across the country.
  5. The CBSE will club both Term 1 and marks in the board results for 2022.

New Updates regarding CBSE Class 10 Exam Pattern

Following are some of the latest updates regarding the CBSE Class 10 exam pattern:

  • Class 10 students do not need to put their responses on the answer sheet this time. They will be given Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) Sheets.
  • Students have to use a pen to mark the right circle.
  • The board has now given five circles instead of four circles for each question on the OMR sheets.
  • The fifth circle is for the answer correction.
  • With the help of an extra circle, students can strike out the incorrect circle, shade the proper circle, and then write the correct option’s response number in the extra circle.
