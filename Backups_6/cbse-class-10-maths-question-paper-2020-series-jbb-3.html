Question 3.
The zeroes of the polynomial x 2 – 3x – m(m + 3) are
(a) m, m + 3
(b) -m, m + 3
(c) m, -(m + 3)
(d) -m, -(m + 3)
Answer:
(b) -m, m + 3
x 2 – 3x – m(m + 3)
= x 2 – 3x – mx + mx – m(m + 3)
= x 2 – x(3 + m) + m[x – (m + 3)]
= x[x – (m + 3)] + m[x – (m + 3)]
= [x – (m + 3)] [x + m]
x – (m + 3) = 0 or x + m = 0
∴ x = m + 3 or x = -m
