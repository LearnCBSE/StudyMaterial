KVPY Result 2019 (Released) | Check Out Results for SA, SB & SX Stream

January 3, 2020 by LearnCBSE Online

KVPY Result 2019-2020: Every year, the Indian Institute of science conducts a scholarship program under the name of Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana. This year’s registration for KVPY Exam 2019 will start in the second week of July. The main exam will be conducted on November 3, 2019. After the exams are done, candidates can check the KVPY result in 2019 through the login ID and password.

The KVPY result 2019 will be separate for SB/SX and SA stream aspirants. This is because the paper would also be different and held in two different sessions. For the final result, there will be two separate lists which aptitude test and final merit list. The KVPY result 2019 will be announced only for the candidates that have qualified the exam. KVPY Result 2019 Date is 11th December 2019.

It should be noted that no request for re-evaluation of KVPY 2019 result will be considered under any circumstances. Candidates that secures the KVPY cutoff as per their category are safe and secure for the fellowship program. KVPY result 2019 for aptitude tests will be announced by the concerned authorities tentatively in December 2019. While the final result for the candidates that will be appearing in the written and interview process will be announced by April 2020.

How To Check KVPY Result 2019?

Below is the step by step process for checking KVPY result 2019

  1. Go to the official website of KVPY which is kvpy.iisc.ernet.in.
  2. A new homepage will be visible on your desktop screen.
  3. Fill in all the required details like the date of birth and application number carefully.
  4. Here, the date of birth should be provided in the format DD-MM-YYYY.
  5. After all the details are filled, click on the button given below ‘continue’.
  6. This will take you to a new tab where the result will appear.
  7. You can check the result as well as download the result for any future purposes.

How is KVPY Final Result Calculated?

The candidates that are shortlisted for KVPY fellowship are the ones that are qualified for the aptitude test as well as the interview. The criteria for KVPY evaluation in 2019 is below:

Final result = 25% marks in interview + 75% of total marks in aptitude test

Re-evaluation of the OMR Sheets

The concerned authorities might also provide the facility for a re-evaluation of the OMR sheets. For this, candidates have to submit a request letter and mention their seat number and application number on or before the due date. Candidates should also have to make fee payment via DD in favor of the concerned authority. The request for re-evaluation after the due date will not be accepted by the authorities.

KVPY Selection Procedure 2019

  • The candidates whose KVPY application forms are accepted will be called for the first stage of the selection procedure which is the aptitude test.
  • The aptitude test for KVPY 2019 will be in online mode on 4th November 2019.
  • In the test, the questions asked will be from chemistry, physics, maths, and biology.
  • For stream SA, SB, and SX, the questions in the aptitude test will be the same.
  • Candidates that qualify the aptitude test will be called for a further round of interview.
  • During the interview, the candidate’s knowledge in science and research field will be tested.
  • After the interviews are conducted, the final list of candidates selected will be released.
  • For KVPY award, candidate’s overall performance in the interview and aptitude test is considered.
  • The interview round for KVPY 2019 will be mostly in the 3rd week to January to February 2020.
  • Students will be informed about the dates and further schedule shall be provided through KVPY website.

KVPY Result 2019 – Aptitude Test

Aptitude test will be carried out in 2 parts which are part 1 and part 2. There will be four sections in each of these parts. Out of these, candidates need to attempt 3 sections in 1st and 2 in part 2. Thus, candidates should prepare their subjects accordingly as the per syllabus provided on KVPY.

  • The first stage in the KVPY scholarship program is the KVPY aptitude test.
  • The aptitude test will happen in the pen paper format.
  • KVPY aptitude test is designed such that the candidates for stage 2 will be filtered.
  • Stage 1 for the KVPY scholarship program is the same for all candidates.

KVPY Result 2019 – Interview

The KVPY interview procedure is conducted to test the knowledge of the candidates in the science and research field. The knowledge in both fields will check if the candidate is capable and deserving of the scholarship. The combined performance in the interview and KVPY test will determine the basis of the scholarship grant.

Documents Required at the day of KVPY Interview Process

  1. KVPY admit card: The original KVPY admit card along with a copy should be carried to the interview center.
  2. KVPY application form: The copy of the KVPY application form filled online should be carried. Also, the KVPY application form can be downloaded online through the official web link using the password and ID.
  3. Original marks list: Candidates need to bring all the original marksheets of class 10 and 12 in the original form along with the photocopies. These are required for document verification purpose.
  4. Medical certificate: The medical certificate is required to be provided by the candidate for indicating physical and visual fitness. Candidates that have not issued the visual and physical fitness should get a certificate indicating the degree of disturbance.
  5. Cast certificate: The cast certificate should be provided in the original format and is compulsory. The OBC certificate provided should not be older than 6 months.
  6. Self-appraisal form: Candidates should mention their good qualities which must be considered for him to award with the scholarship certificate.
  7. Study center form: A form which is obtained from the last school that indicates the completion of schooling must be brought. It should have signed by the principal or the head of the school.
  8. Teacher’s recommendation letter: This form is available on the KVPY official website and must be signed by the teacher.
