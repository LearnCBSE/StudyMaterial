Assam HS Compartment Examination 2019 | Registration Form, Time Table, Admit Card, Result 2019

June 20, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

Assam HS Supplementary Examination 2019: Assam Class 12 Supplementary Examination 2019 is conducted for students who have not passed in all subjects. No students want to get fail or repeat the same standard, but if they get failed in any subject in the board exam, they are given with an opportunity by Assam board to appear in Supplementary Exam and get passed in the particular subject, improving their scores in the HS certificate.

State Board Supplementary Exams

Assam HS Supplementary Examination Notification 2019 has been declared by Board of Secondary Education, Assam on 16th May 2019. The exam date for HS supplementary examination is from 1st July to 6th July. Students can download the Assam HS supplementary time table or routine 2019 from the official website of the board- ahsec.nic.in.

Assam Board HS exam was conducted from 12th February to 14th March 2019 and results were declared on 25th May 2019. Therefore, if any student was not able to qualify any subjects, can apply for the Assam 12th Supplementary Exam. Students who have failed in a particular subject have to mention the subject name while applying for the Supplementary Exam. The student who fails to qualify in the Supplementary Exam also, have to reappear for the same class in the next academic session, i.e. HS 2020 Exam. No separate merit list or topper list will be declared by the board for the Supplementary Exam. Go through the complete details related to AHSEC HS Supplementary Exam 2019, here in this article.

After the Assam HS Supplementary Examination 2019 notifications have been declared. How To Apply for Assam Class 12th Supplementary Exam?

For those students who have not qualified in any subjects in Assam HSLC Main Exam 2019 have the opportunity to improve their grade by giving Supplementary Exam conducted by Assam Board.

  • Students have to reach the official website first, sebaonline.org.
  • Click on the link given as, HSLC/AHM Compartmental Examination 2019.
  • You will be redirected towards a new page, where your login id and password will be asked.
  • Log in with your credentials and fill the application form.
  • Mention the subject name and code for which you have to give the supplementary exam.
  • Submit the form and download a copy of it for future use.

Assam HS Supplementary Exam 2019 Time Table

To download the routine for HS Supplementary Exam 2019 conducted by Assam Board, follow the below steps;

  • Go to the official website for the Improvement time table-  sebaonline.org.
  • Students have to click on the Assam HS Supplementary Exam Time Table link.
  • Assam HS Supplementary Exam Routine 2019 will appear on the new window.
  • Download the Time Table and keep it for future use.

Assam HS Supplementary Exam 2019 Admit Card

  • The admit card for HS Supplementary Exam 2019, conducted by Board of Secondary Education, Assam will be available on the official website.
  • Visit the official website and click on the link given for Assam HS Supplementary Exam 2019 Admit card.
  • Provide the login id and password as per mentioned in the admit card and submit it.
  • The admit card for HS Supplementary Exam will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a print out.
  • Bring the admit card at the exam center, else you will not be permitted to give the exam.

Assam HS Supplementary Exam 2019 Results

The results for HS Supplementary Exam 2019, conducted by Assam Board of School Education, will be declared in August 2019, after the conduct of examinations. The exams are going to be conducted from 1st July to 6th July 2019. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, sebaonline.org. The level of the exam will be the State Board level.

Students who want to check the results for HS Supplementary Exam, follow the given steps

  • Go to the Assam Board official website.
  • Click on the Results Section on the home page.
  • Click on the Assam Supplementary Exam HSLC Result 2019 link.
  • Students are required to enter roll number mentioned in admit card and click on Submit.
  • Assam HS Supplementary Exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.

About Assam Board

The Assam Secondary Education Act, 1961 was passed to provide for the establishment of a Board of Secondary Education to regulate, supervise and develop Secondary Education in the State of Assam. The main objectives set by the board are;

  • To prescribe curriculum, syllabus, and courses of instruction for Higher Secondary stage which may be in a college / Higher Secondary school.
  • To conduct examination based on courses
  • To admit to its examinations, on condition that may be prescribed by regulations, candidates who have pursued the prescribed courses of instruction and also to take such disciplinary action against candidates as may be prescribed by regulations.
  • To demand and receive such fees as may be prescribed by the regulations.
  • To publish the result of its examination.
  • To grant a certificate to students passing the examinations.
