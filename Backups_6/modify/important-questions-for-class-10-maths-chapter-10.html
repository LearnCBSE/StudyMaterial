Question 1.
In the given figure, O is the centre of a circle, AB is a chord and AT is the tangent at A. If ∠AOB = 100°, then calculate ∠BAT. (2011D)

Solution:

∠1 = ∠2
∠1 + ∠2 + 100° = 180°
∠1 + ∠1 = 80°
⇒ 2∠1 = 80°
⇒ ∠1 = 40°
∠1 + ∠BAT = 90°
∠BAT = 90° – 40° = 50°

Question 2.
