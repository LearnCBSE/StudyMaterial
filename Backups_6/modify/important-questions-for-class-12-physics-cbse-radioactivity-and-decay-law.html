2.Define the activity of a given radioactive substance. Write its SI unit. [All India 2013]
Ans .The activity of a sample is defined as the rate of disintegration taking place in the sample of radioactive substance.
SI unit of activity is Becquerel (Bq).
1 Bq =1 disintegration/second
