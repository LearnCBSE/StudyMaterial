Study MBBS in Philippines in Low Tuition Fees – Benefits, Admission, Eligibility

September 11, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Study MBBS in Philippines: The Philippines has one of the best education systems in Asia. It is the best place to choose for Indian students to complete their medicine at an affordable cost. The Philippines is the largest English-speaking country in the Southeast Asian region. Students will get quality education in the English language at the best universities at low fees.

Studying MBBS in Philippines has 2 parts they are BS and MD, and the overall course duration is 6 years. The fees for the MBBS course are around 2.5 lakhs to 6 lakhs per year and the cost of living is 12,000 to 15,000 per month. Check the complete details in Study MBBS in Philippines Guidance such as eligibility, duration, fees, syllabus, admission process, and so on.

Why Study MBBS in Philippines?

A lot of students and parents from India are willing to study medicine in the Philippines as it offers quality education with other benefits. Here we are giving the best reasons for why MBBS in Philippines.

  • The Philippines universities offer the MBBS at low tuition fees. The fee can be paid on an instalment basis.
  • Affordable living and accommodation expenses make middle-class students more comfortable.
  • The Philippines is largely an English-speaking country. So there is no language barrier occurs while interacting with locals and faculty.
  • A variety of Indian food is made available at low cost around every corner of the country. You can even find Indian cuisine on the campus.
  • Education in the Philippines is based on the US education system. So it focuses on problem-based learning methodology.
  • MBBS in the Philippines awards you an internationally recognized medical degree.
  • The Philippines has WHO, NMC, and FAIMER-recognized medical universities.
  • The universities provide the students with a medical program that is recognized worldwide.
  • Students can easily do postgraduation or choose careers in countries like the USA, UK, and Australia after completing MBBS in the Philippines.
  • The Philippines have a tropical climate just like India. So, people can adjust easily.
  • Faculties in the Philippines are English-speaking only, and a large share of them are employed in countries like the USA and India.

Advantages of Doing MBBS in the Philippines

The following listed are the benefits of studying medical education in the Philippines.

  • Low tuition fees and cost of living lower the financial burden of completing MBBS in the Philippines for Indian students without compromising on the quality of education.
  • Medical universities follow the US education pattern which is helpful for students who want to appear for the USMLE exam.
  • The colleges in the Philippines had higher FMGE passing percentages than China, Ukraine, Russia, Kyrgyzstan, Nepal and Germany in 2016-17.
  • MCI-approved medical colleges in the Philippines have remarkable passing percentages in MCI screening test.
  • No donation or caption fee is needed.
  • IELTS and TOEFL exams are also not needed.

Eligibility to Study Medicine in the Philippines for Indian Students

Students have to fulfil the below-mentioned eligibility conditions to study MBBS in Philippines.

  • Students’ age should be more than 17 years.
  • They have to score at least 50% marks in PCB in the 12th class for the General category.
  • A minimum of 40% score in PCB in the 12th class for SC/ST, OBC category students is needed.
  • Applicants have to clear the NEET exam.

Admission Process for Studying MBBS in Philippines

Here is the detailed MBBS in the Philippines admission process.

  • The initial step is students have to qualify National Medical Admission Test (NMAT). It is mandatory for getting admission into medical colleges in the Philippines.
  • Students can apply online with the NMAT score.
  • The Philippines universities will approve your admission and send the acceptance letter.
  • Now, deposit the admission and tuition fees.
  • Lastly, you have to apply for a student visa.

Documents Needed for Studying MBBS in the Philippines

  • Valid Passport

  • 3 passport-size photographs of the applicant.
  • Medical health certificate
  • NOA (Original Notice of Acceptance)
  • CEA (Police Clearance Certificate authenticated by the Philippine Embassy)
  • Visa Fee

MBBS Course Duration

The MBBS course duration is 6 years including the 1-year internship program. The first 16 months of the medicine will undertake the education in BS Biology as per the norms. The next 4 years will be focused on medicine and clinical subjects.
