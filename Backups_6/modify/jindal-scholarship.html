Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship Scheme 2024 | All You Need To Know About Jindal Scholarship

December 2, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Sitaram Jindal Scholarship 2023-24: Sitaram Jindal Scholarship invites all learners who are qualified to apply for this Jindal Scholarship. The scholarship offers many variable benefits that provide economic assistance for their greater studies to qualified learners. To learn more about the last date, application form, and benefits, read the full article.

Sitaram Jindal Scholarship 2024 is a merit cum scheme that Sitaram Jindal Foundation launched as a scholarship. This is a charitable trust in Bengaluru aimed at supporting the education of deserving and needy applicants. The aim of this scholarship is to provide financial assistance for their bright future to underprivileged learners. Only Class 11 students are provided this scholarship to PG. Sitaram Jindal foundation scholarship scheme 2023-24 will provide monetary assistance to those applicants who are worthy and ready to carry forward their studies. Rs.2500 per month and other allowances will be provided to selected learners. It should be observed that this scholarship includes courses in technical Polytechnic and Diploma technical education.

Students can also find more Scholarship Articles for 12th passed, 10th passed Students and many more.

Jindal Scholarship – Who Can Apply for Jindal Scholarship?

An applicant must

  • Be studying in class 11, 12, ITI, Diploma, Graduation and Postgraduation.
  • Fulfill the eligibility of percentage marks in their previous class as specified in the notification.

Jindal Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

The scholarship is split into categories depending on the applicants’ academic level. To get maximum advantages, students must meet the eligibility requirements. This scholarship’s eligibility criteria include family revenue, academic background, and much more. Check your eligibility through the table below.

Category Eligibility Criteria
Category ‘A’ Applicants must be class 11th to 12th Male applicants in the past exam must have scored at least 65 percent marks and girls candidates must have scored at least 60 percent marks.
In the event of girls, students from Karnataka and West Bengal must have a minimum score of 75% mark (boys) and 70% marks.
The annual earnings of the family (who are employed) must not exceed 4 lakh.
Annual family earnings should not have exceeded 2.5 lakhs for other applicants Must not have passed 30 years of age.
The candidates are required to complete ITI classes from any government or private institution.
Category ‘B’ ITI Students The candidates are required to complete ITI classes from any government or private institution.
Male applicants must have a minimum score of 505 marks and female applicants must have a minimum score of 40 percent marks.
The annual earnings of the family (who are employed) must not exceed 4 lakh.
Annual family earnings should not have exceeded 2.5 lakhs for other applicants.
Must not have passed 30 years of age.
Category ‘C’ Candidates are required to study in PG and are qualified to apply for graduate courses.
They must have obtained 60 percent marks for applicants for women.
They must have secured marks of 65 percent for boys applicants.
Karnataka and West Bengal students must have secured marks of at least 70% marks (boys) and 65% marks (girls)
The annual earnings of the family (who are employed) must not exceed 4 lakh. Annual family earnings should not have exceeded 2.5 lakhs for other applicants Must not have passed 30 years of age.
Category ‘D’ Diploma courses Candidates must attend classes such as Civil Engg, Computer Science, Chemical, Automotive, Nursing, Pharmacy and Physiotherapy, and classes linked to the environment.
At least 60 marks (boys) and a minimum of 55 percent marks (girls) must be obtained by applicants.
The annual earnings of the family (who are employed) must not exceed 4 lakh.
Annual family earnings should not have exceeded 2.5 lakhs for other applicants Must not have passed 30 years of age.
Category ‘E’ Engineering & Medicine Courses Candidates are required to study classes in engineering, medicine, and architecture.
This system is not eligible for MDS learners.
In the last qualifying exam, male applicants must have scored at least 70 percent and females must have received at least 65 percent marks.
Karnataka and West Bengal students must have obtained a minimum of 75 percent marks (boys) and a minimum of 70 percent marks (girls) The annual family income (who are employed) must not exceed 4 lakh.
Annual family earnings should not have exceeded 2.5 lakhs for other applicants Must not have passed 30 years of age.

Note: The eligibility percentage criterion for applicants with different capabilities is that in all categories they should have scored passing marks. The minimum necessary percentage of relaxation for Jindal scholarships to continue is 5 percent. Last date to apply is 31st December 2023

Jindal Scholarship Category Wise Scholarship Rewards

  • Category – A: 11th and 12th Classes students Rs.500/-.

    • General Category: For Girls – Rs.1000/-, For Boys – Rs.800/
    • Physically Challenged or Handicap Students – Rs.1000/-
    • Widows & unmarried wards of Ex-servicemen – Rs.1200/-
  • Category – D: Diploma courses for all streams Girls – Rs.1200/-, Boys – Rs.1000/-
  • Category – E: Engineering & Medicine Courses
    • Graduate Engineering Courses (all streams): For boys – 1500/- for girls 1700/-
    • Graduate Medicine Courses including Dental, B.Pharma, M.B.B.S, Homeopathy, and Ayurveda: For boys – 1800/- for girls 2000/-
    • PG Courses Engineering & Medicine Courses including Naturopathy, M.Pharma, Homeopathy, Surgery (excluding MDS): For Girls – Rs.2500/-, Boys – Rs.2300/-.
  • For students staying in the hostel
    • Additional amount for hosteler of ITI/Diploma, Grad./PG Courses – Rs.800/-.
    • Medical & Engg.Courses (including PG Courses) – Rs.1200/-.
