How To Check Maharashtra HSC Result For Class 12?

Students of Class 12 can check Maharashtra HSC Results in two ways, that is

  • Maharashtra HSC Result for Class 12 in Online Mode
  • Maharashtra HSC Result for Class 12 through SMS

Online Mode

Follow the steps as under to check Maharashtra HSC Result for Class 12 in online mode

  • Step – 1: Visit the official website of Maharashtra board @ mahresult.nic.in
  • Step – 2: Click on the Results tab and select HSC Examination Result March 2024 from the drop-down menu.
  • Step – 3: The page will be directed to HSC Examination Result 2024. Now enter your Roll Number and Mother’s First Name as mentioned in Admit Card/Hall Ticket.
  • Step – 4: Click on “View Results”. Your HSC Result Maharashtra Board will be displayed on the screen. Download it for future reference.

Offline Mode – Through SMS?

Follow the steps as under to check your Maharashtra HSC Result for Class 12 through SMS

  • Step 1: Open the SMS application on your mobile.
  • Step 2: Click on “New Message”.
  • Step 3: Type the following message (according to your Network Operator) and send it to the respective numbers.
  • Step 4: You will shortly receive your Maharashtra HSC Result for class 12 on your mobile phone

Maharashtra Board 12th – Compartmental / Supplementary Exam

Soon after the declaration of Maharashtra Board 12th Result 2024, the MSBSHSE will also issue an official notification about the Compartmental and Supplementary Examinations. The Maharashtra Board Compartmental / Supplementary Exams are held for students who have failed in the Maharashtra Board 12th Result 2024. Such students can reappear for the exam in the month of June / July and clear them to seek admission to colleges without waiting for an entire year. Students can get more information about Maharashtra Board Compartmental Exams from the official website or from their respective schools.

About Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education

The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education, Pune has been in existence since the year 1965, was established under the Maharashtra Act No. 41 of 1965.  The jurisdiction of MSBSHSE extends over the nine divisional boards located at Pune, Mumbai, Aurangabad, Nasik, Kolhapur, Amravati, Latur, Nagpur and Ratnagiri.

Every year more than 30 lakh plus students participate in the bi-Yearly examination conducted by The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education, Pune at HSC and SSC level. Around 21000 SSC level and 7000 plus Higher secondary level schools and institutions are affiliated with MSBSHSE. The board holds the power to regulate, supervise academic policies and prescribe syllabus in the affiliated schools.
