NBSE HSSLC Compartmental Exam 2019 | Registration Form, Time Table, Admit Card, Result 2019

July 3, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

NBSE HSSLC Compartmental Exam 2019: Nagaland Board of School Education is the state level education board in Nagaland. The board started working from October 1, 1974. The board conducts examinations for HSLC and HSSLC. It imparts school level education in the state. The board looks after the development of school education in the state. It also releases the result for class 10th and 12th. It tries to provide quality education to the students. The board also prescribes syllabus and curriculum to its affiliated schools.

State Board Supplementary Exams

They had conducted the main board exams in February 2019. Many students could not pass them due to various reasons. Hence, the supplementary/compartment exams are being conducted soon. This is a wonderful opportunity that cannot be obtained again. Hence, you should make the best use of it. To aid you in the same, here we have provided all the details that you should know about the exam.

NBSE HSSLC Supplementary Exam 2019 overview

You can get a brief overview of the exam by taking a look at the table given below.

Exam conducting board Nagaland Board of School Education
Exam Name HSSLC Compartmental exam 2019
Exam start date 29th May 2019
Exam end date 4th June 2019
Examination fee ₹350 per subject
Registration mode Offline
Registration last date 15th May 2019
Official website link www.nbsenagaland.com

NBSE HSSLC Compartmental Exam Registration 2019

If you have not passed in one or more exams in the main NBSE exams, you need to apply for the compartmental exam. Note that you need to apply for all the subjects you failed in (less than or equal to three). You need to apply if you have secured a “needs improvement” grade in an exam. Note that you can’t register for these exams if you were absent during the main board exam. The registration process will be in offline mode and is very straight forward

  1. Your school/HSSLC 2019 exam centers will provide you with the registration forms.
  2. Fill them in carefully as any mistake in the registration form would reflect later on your scorecard and could hence lead to a lot of trouble. Check the details once before submitting.
  3. If you have a doubt about something, consult the school authorities present. They are sure to guide you.
  4. Note that you will also have to pay an examination fee of ₹350 per subject.
  5. Also, remember that the last date of submission is 15th May 2019. No forms will be entertained after then. Even if you have failed in a subject, you cannot take the compartmental exams.

If you have a doubt regarding your HSSLC exam center, consult the NBSE official notification, Click Here

Alternatively, to make sure that any recent announcements/changes reflect on the notification, you may want to download the pdf from the website directly. You can do so by visiting the official website (link given above) and clicking on “Notification (Compartmental)”.

How To Download NBSE HSSLC Compartmental Exam Routine 2019?

You need to make a proper study plan to do well in the exams. First, you need to make sure that you need the syllabus properly. You can do so by visiting the homepage and clicking on the “Curriculum and Syllabus” section on the left. You can then select your stream (Arts & Science/Commerce) from the links given to view your syllabus.

Secondly, you need to understand the exam pattern. This will help you in preparing the material properly as you would know what to focus on. It will also help you better manage your time during the exam. We suggest that you take a look at your own year main board exam paper, and determine where you went wrong. This would help you avoid those mistakes in the future.

Thirdly and finally, you need to know the exam routine. This will make sure that you have enough time for every exam. To download the same, just follow these easy steps

  1. Visit the official NBSE website (link is given above).
  2. Click on the “Routines” section on the left of the homepage.
  3. Click on “HSSLC Compartmental exam routine 2019” link.
  4. This will open the time table in pdf form.
  5. Download it and take a print out of it for future reference.

Note that any suddenly announced government holidays will have no impact on the routine and it will remain unchanged. However, this is not to say that the routine once released is set in stone. There could be some changes in the date at any point. Hence, stay updated with any recent notifications by visiting the website regularly.

NBSE HSSLC Compartmental Exam Results 2019

The results will be announced after the exam via online mode on the official website. Unfortunately, no “topper” or “merit list” will be released for the compartmental exams. You can check the results as so:

  1. Visit the official NBSE website (link is given above).
  2. Click on the “Results” section on the left of the homepage.
  3. Click on “HSSLC Compartmental 2019” link.
  4. This will open the results in pdf form.
  5. Use the “Ctrl + F” shortcut to search for your roll number in the list.
  6. Download the results and take a print out of it for future reference.

You need to register for the NBSE HSSLC Compartmental exams 2019 if you have failed in one or more subjects in the main board exam. You can do so by collecting the form at your respective school/HSSLC exam centre and filling it in. Note that an exam fee of ₹350 per subject is applicable. You can obtain the exam routine and results from the official website, once released, by following the steps provided here.
