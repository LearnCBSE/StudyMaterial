About KSEB

The Kerala State Education Board (KSEB) was established in the year 1990, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The aim of the Board is to restructure the operations of Secondary and Collegiate Education in Kerala. The main objective is to conduct the board exam for SSLC and DHSE classes and to prescribe syllabus for all the schools, private or government, which are affiliated to the board. The board is responsible to affiliate the private and government schools in Kerala, also.

Kerala board is responsible for the improvement of the secondary and higher secondary education so that it meets the required needs and standards of economic and social development. KSEB provides adequate facilities for the educational and professional advancement of socially and educationally backward communities. Apart from the class 10th and 12th examination, Pareeksha Bhavan also conducts other examinations, for certification of qualified teachers through KTET and other similar examinations.
