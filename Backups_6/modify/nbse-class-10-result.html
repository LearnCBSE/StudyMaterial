NBSE Class 10 Result 2024 (Released) | Get NBSE HSLC Results from Here

January 12, 2024 by LearnCBSE Online

NBSE Class 10 Result 2024: Nagaland Board Class 10 Result 2024 will be announced by Nagaland Board of School Education on its official website, nbsenagaland.com The date for the release of results is confirmed by the board as on 2nd May 2024, at 2:00 PM. Students who have given the Class 10th and Class 12th exams, the results will be declared for them by the conducting body.

State Board Results

Candidates who want to check Nagaland Board Class 10 and Class 12th result 2024 have to enter their Roll Number as mentioned in their Admit Card. The exam for NBSE HSLC and HSSLC academic exams was conducted from 12th Feb to 06th Feb 2024 and 12th Feb to 4th March 2024, respectively. The results for both the exam will be declared on the same day.

Students, who have appeared for NBSE Class 10th and Class 12th exam should calculate approximate marks as per they have written the exams. Get your questions paper, and start mentioning marks for each correct answer and do the total for each subject. This will help you match the marks obtained by you in the final results. If there is a notable disagreement between your expected marks and the original marks, they can apply for re-evaluation.

Here in this article, we are going to give complete details for results of Nagaland Board Class 10 and Class 12th exams conducted this year in 2024. Keep reading with us.

NBSE Class 10th Result 2024

The results for Chhattisgarh Class 10th 2024, will be released on 2nd of May 2024, as per the announcement done by the Board. Let us give here a general description of the NBSE HSLC Results 2024.

  • Examination Names – Nagaland Board HSLC Exam 2024
  • Conducting Body – Nagaland Board of School Education
  • Examination Mode – Offline (Pen and Paper)
  • Exam Dates – 10th Feb to 06th Feb 2024(HSLC)
  • Exam Results – 2nd May 2024
  • Result Website – www.nbsenagaland.com

NBSE Class 10th Result 2024 – Steps to Check

Nagaland Board Class 10th Result 2024 will be declared on the official website and through online mode only. The Board will be distributing the mark sheets to the recognized institutions from 2nd May to 7th May. Later, students can collect their Class 10th and 12th mark sheet from their respective schools. Below here are the steps to check the results through online mode, for the 10th and 12th class exam conducted this year in 2024.

  • Firstly, the candidate has to visit the official Nagaland board official results website,  nbsenagaland.com.
  • Click on the link given for HSLC and HSSLC results 2024.
  • Enter your roll number in the space given.
  • The result of Nagaland Class 10th and Class 12th exam will be displayed on your screen, respectively.
  • Download the result for your reference and future use.

Nagaland Class 10th Result Via SMS

Students who appeared for the NBSE Class 10th exam in 2024, will have the option to check the results via SMS also. The results can be accessed by sending SMS to the numbers given below in the following pattern;

For Class 10th or HSLC results

SMS – NB10<space>Roll Number to 56070

Or

SMS-RESULT<space>NBSE10<space>Roll Number to 5676750

NBSE Class 10 Marksheet Contains

Marksheet of NBSE Class 10 consists of following details

  • Roll Number
  • Name of the Student
  • School Name and Code
  • Father’s and Mother’s name
  • Marks obtained in each subject
  • Total Marks Scored
  • Percentage
  • Signature of school Principal

Some Important Facts For Nagaland Board Class 10th and Class 12th Results

  • The results will be announced by the Nagaland Board of School Education(NBSE).
  • Students can check the result through online mode, in the official website of Nagaland Board.
  • Student’s class 10th and Class 12th exam roll number are required respectively to check the results.
  • Students have to score the minimum marks in all the subjects, to pass in the exam.
  • After the Nagaland Board HSLC and HSSLC results are declared, students can reach to their respective schools to get the mark sheet.
  • The full form for HSLC is High School Leaving Certificate and of HSSLC is Higher Secondary School Leaving Certificate

About NBSE

Nagaland Board of School Education(NBSE) was established on 15th of November 1973. It started functioning from 1st October 1974, and in March 1975. The main objective is to conduct the board exam for HSLC and HSSLC. Also, to prescribe syllabus and textbooks for all the schools, private or government, which are affiliated to the Nagaland board. Responsible to publish the results of external examinations. The board is also responsible to affiliate the private and government schools in Nagaland.

Nagaland board is responsible for the improvement of secondary education so that it meets the required needs and standards of economic and social development. NBSE provides adequate facilities for the educational and professional advancement of socially and educationally backward communities.
