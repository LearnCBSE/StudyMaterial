Vodafone Scholarship 2023 | VI Notification, Apply Online, Dates, Eligibility, Registration Process

December 4, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Vodafone Scholarship 2023-2024: Vodafone scholarship is a brand new initiative meant to bridge the gap between academic excellence and quality education. It aims to provide access to countless other scholarships which are tailored to fit the needs of many students. Vodafone scholarship entitles students, right from lower primary classes, all the way up to Ph.D. courses. Moreover, their scholarships are open to all categories and classes of society. Providing access to education is one of the fundamental ways of improving employability, thereby reducing poverty. Vodafone Scholarship 2023 last dates and further information to be updated.

Vodafone Scholarship 2023 Registration Form is available online at its official website, All the eligible candidates who are going to apply for Vodafone Scholarship 2023 can fill the application form before or on the last date. Students who have completed Class 12 or during engineering can apply for Learning With Vodafone Scholarship 2023. While applying for Vodafone Scholarship students need not pay a single rupee as registration fee. If your application is rejected students can recheck the application and can re-apply it. Students need to confirm all the details before submitting the Vodafone Scholarship application form.

Vodafone Scholarship portal into two main categories

  • Need-Based Scholarships
  • Merit-Based Scholarships

Need-Based Scholarships: There are so many students who come from financially and economically weak backgrounds. Need-based scholarship’s main target is providing scholarships from low-income families. Vodafone Scholarships are meant to provide financial support to their families and reduce the burden to the family.

Merit-based Scholarships: Many government and private organizations offer merit-based scholarships to those students who have an excellent academic record and are aspiring for higher studies in India as well as abroad. Read the complete article to know more about Vodafone scholarship for kv students.

Vodafone Scholarship Awards

The awards given to the candidates depends on the course and the scholarship provider. Some scholarships award the students in United States dollars while most others pay in Indian Rupee. The amount of scholarship starts from INR 1,000 all the way up to INR 1 lakh.

Vodafone Scholarship Application Process

Vodafone Scholarship Registration Process is as follows

  • Interested candidates are required to visit the official Vodafone scholarship site at https://www.learningwithvodafoneidea.in/ .
  • Note: Please turn off the ad blocker, if any.
  • Select one of the two types of scholarships – Need-based or Merit-Based scholarships.
    • Need-based scholarships are primarily meant for students from economically weaker sections of society. It helps to complete their education without adding more financial burden on their families.
    • Merit-based scholarships, on the other hand, are meant to motivate meritorious students to continue their education.
  • Select the scholarship that is relevant to you.
  • Sign up if you do not have an account, Vodafone Scholarship login if you have an account.
  • You will then be presented with various fields, which you are required to submit.
  • Further instructions will be provided regarding specific scholarship programs.
  • Enter the prescribed details to avail that particular scholarship.

Vodafone Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria vary depending on the type of scholarship chosen by candidates (either Need-based or Merit-based scholarship).

Documents Required for Vodafone Scholarship

For initial registration on the Vodafone scholarship portal, there is no need to upload any documents. However, once the candidate selects their relevant scholarship, they may be required to submit documents such as:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Pan Card
  • Bank Passbook
  • Income Certificate
  • Relevant marks cards

Note: No scholarship program will ever ask for any kind of fee or “processing charge”. Furthermore, ensure that the details provided in the application form is accurate and submitted before the specified deadline.
