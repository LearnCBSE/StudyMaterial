Top 8 Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Russia – MBBS Abroad

September 6, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Russia: Thinking of Russia to study MBBS? Russia is the second largest country and it is the popular alternative for many students to study MBBS for its high-quality education and affordable tuition.

There are a lot of advantages to studying MBBS in Russia, but at the same time, there are a lot of disadvantages too. There are several hardships that many international students go through when they decide to study MBBS in Russia.

Before thinking to study MBBS in Russia , let us take a closer look at the disadvantages of studying MBBS in Russia. Scroll Down to Read!!!

Breaking Up the 8 Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Russia For International Students

Studying abroad is a great opportunity for students, but still, there are some risk factors and challenges. Here we are going to provide you with the disadvantages of studying MBBS in Russia for international students. Look into them carefully and think about the study in Russia for MBBS.

  • Language Barrier
  • Quality of Education
  • Climate Change Problems
  • Traveling Distance
  • Culture and Addictions
  • Difficulty in Finding Jobs and Lack of Support
  • Lack of Information
  • Cheating Agents

Language Barrier

The main disadvantage of studying MBBS in Russia will be the language barrier. Normally, colleges in Russia will have the medium of English and Russian. People who are from international countries can choose to study in English medium, but for clinical rotation and observation, students need to meet with locals.

At that time, it will be very difficult for people who do not know the Russian language. It will be very challenging to connect with their classmates and professors. However many Russian institutions have kept Indian chefs for Indian students do not have a problem with the language or food at lodging and food.

Quality of Education

Another main disadvantage of studying MBBS in Russia is the quality of education. The standard of education in Russia may not be as good as in other countries. Even studying in Russia, takes a longer time, to study MBBS, like it takes approximately 5 years and 8 months.

And even the medical curriculum will not be as good as in other countries. But the entire course of duration contains internship/observation as well. It is difficult to get practical training in Russia for Indian students as it is not very advanced compared to other countries.

Climate Change Problems

As you know, Russia is a cool country as it has a chilly climate with dropping temperatures below freezing in the winter months. So, some students find it hard to adjust to the cold climate and weather in Russia. So it will be the biggest disadvantage for the students who cannot adapt to all weather conditions.

Mainly, Indian students cannot cope with this climate and may fall ill very easily. However, all buildings have central heating systems and cars with air conditioning will not be any problem till they are inside of them. Adapting to the cold climate is not that hard.

Traveling Distance

One more disadvantage that stops many international students from studying MBBS in Russia is traveling to Russia. It takes a longer time to go to Russia from India, and even in Russia, it is a problem for newcomers, to explore the routes to their university in that vast country.

But as there are many direct flights to Russia, and vice versa, the journey will be easy even though it takes time. Even when it comes, to university routes, most of the universities in Russia provide clear information about routes. In most cases, they also arrange pickup and drop facility.

Culture and Addictions

Studying MBBS in Russia will be a problem for many students due to cultural hindrances. Russia offers a vibrant and addictive nightlife. So many students who go to Russia might fall into that addiction and distract from their studies and even that will affect their health in the long run.

For many students, coming to Russia to study will be a huge challenge for some people as cultural norms and values are different in Russia from other countries. One solution to this problem is choosing to live in the accommodation provided by the university.

Difficulty in Finding Jobs and Lack of Support

Many of you may think about what will be the difficulty of finding jobs after studying at such a prestigious and high-quality university or institution. But yes, once after completion of your studies in Russia, you will definitely find it difficult to find jobs in India.

Because the medical sector in India is highly competitive and the demand for doctors is high. Even families and friends who are away from the student may feel isolated and there is a chance of a negative impact on their health and studies.

Lack of Information

Even though most of the students get familiar with the new place, there are some students who cannot able to adjust due to homesickness.

Without any clarity, many people go to Russia to study MBBS, and this makes the situation complicated between what was promised to them and what they actually have.

So, it is very important to research the country or other important information when you think of studying in another country rather than depending on consultants. Because most of the consultants will tell you about benefits and attract you to study.

Cheating Agents

When you are consulting consultants to study abroad in Russia you need to be very careful as there are many consultants who act as official representatives for many Russian medical universities and cheat students to take money from them.

Even in the past many years, many scams and frauds have been done. They take the high amount of fees than actual from the students and keep that money in their pockets.

This is why students need to do good research on universities and consultants and reach the right consultant who has a direct tie-up with the universities.

FAQs on Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Russia

  1. What are the disadvantages of studying MBBS in Russia?

There are a lot more disadvantages to studying MBBS in Russia, some of which are language barriers, climatic differences, risk of fraud, and many others.

  1. Is the cost of Living high in Russia for Students who want to study MBBS?

Yes, students who want to study MBBS in Russia have a high cost of living. It may be education, food, or traveling.

  1. Does Russia MBBS have value?

Yes, of course, the student who completes their MBBS is very beneficial almost all medical colleges in Russia have a high level of education.
