Study MBBS in Georgia: Georgia is one of the popular places to pursue a career in the medical field. It is the ideal decision for Indian students to study MBBS. The Georgia MBBS universities offer the best facilities for all international medical students and allow admission to candidates who have not qualified for IELTS and TOEFL.

The medical colleges focus on various medical examinations, skilled resources, looked-after medical clinics, and high-quality food. MBBS is a 5-year course with 100% English medium in Georgia. Study MBBS in Georgia for Indian Students

Medical universities in Georgia provide high-quality education to international students. On average more than 500 Indian students are going to Georgia yearly to fulfill their dreams. The educational plan is intended for students to provide theoretical knowledge as well as practice-based learning.

The total duration of the MBBS program in Georgia is 5+1 years. One year is mandatory for the internship program. Georgian universities offer a perfect look at the Soviet Union and European foundations. The language of teaching in all universities is 100% English.

The reason many students prefer to pursue MBBS in Georgia is universities are recognised by WHO and MCI. The staff have good experience and skills.

Medicine From Georgia Eligibility

It is mandatory for students to fulfil the eligibility criteria for studying MBBS in Georgia.

  • The candidate’s age should be between 17 and 25 years.
  • They must have passed the 12th exam with 50% marks from a science background.
  • The NEET exam is mandatory to take admission to the universities.
  • Students need to have a passport.
  • Students belonging to the SC/ST/OBC category have to score at least 45% marks.

MBBS Admission Process in Georgia

Go through the step-by-step instructions to get admission into the medical universities of Georgia.

  • Candidates have to enter details in the online application form at the university portal.
  • After filling out the application, submit the needed documents.
  • Once the process is finished, the university will issue the invitation letter from the university.
  • The NMC will approve your admission letter.
  • Your university will send the admission letter after verifying your documents.
  • Later, the university will conduct the English test online.
  • After obtaining the approval letter, you need to pay the tuition fees.
  • After receiving all needed documents, apply for the visa.
  • This whole process takes 30 to 45 days.
