NCERT Solutions for Class 4 EVS Chapter 14 Basva’s Farm

NCERT Solutions EVS Maths English Hindi Paryavarana Adhyayan

NCERT PAGE NUMBER 113
Find Out
1. In Basva’s area an implement called Khunti is used to loosen the soil. What is
this kind of implement called in your area? Find out.
Ans. In my area also such implement is called Khunti.

2. Find out from a farmer or some elders in your family what kinds of crops are grown in your area.
Ans . In my area crops of wheat, rice, maize, mustard, gram, vegetables, etc., are grown.

3. Basva helps his father in the field. Do you help the elders in your family in their work? What do you help with?
Ans. Yes, I help the elders in my family in their work. I help my mom to clean and arrange the house. I help my father while purchasing the essentials for our house.

4. Do you enjoy doing that work?
Ans. Yes, I like to do those jobs.

NCERT PAGE NUMBER 116
Write answers in your notebook
1. Why is Basva not able to attend school for some days?
Ans . Basva is not able to attend school for some days because he needs to help his father in the field.

2. Are there any fields near your house? What is grown there?
Ans . Yes, there is a field near my house. Vegetables are grown in that field.

3. Basva’s Appa takes the onions to the market in a truck. Think, how would fruits and vegetables be taken to another place, if there were no proper roads.
Ans. It would be difficult. In olden days people used to carry fruits and vegetable to such places by bullock carts, donkeys, boats, etc.

4. What kind of vehicles are used to carry fruits and vegetables?
Ans. Usually rikshaw, thela, tempo, tractor, trucks, etc are used to carry fruits and vegetables.
