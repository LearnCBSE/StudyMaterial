Andhra Pradesh Board SSC Supplementary Examination 2019 | Registration Form, Result, TimeTable ﻿

June 14, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

Andhra Pradesh Board SSC Supplementary Examination 2019: AP SSC Result 2019 has been released today i.e May 14, 2019. The candidates who are not able to qualify the examination can now apply for the compartment examination. As per the reports, the Directorate of Government Examination (DGE) is going to conduct the class 10 compartment examination from June 17, 2019. However, there is no official confirmation regarding this. The board is going to release the dates of supplementary time table soon on the official website.  The application form will be available on the official website: main.bseap.org. Read on to find more details about the examination.

State Board Supplementary Exams

AP SSC Supplementary Time Table 2019

The prior year, the board released the time table for supplementary examination in the month of May. However, the examination started on June 11. In 2019, the board is going to start the supplementary examination a bit late i.e from June 17. However, the board has not yet released the time table. Now let us have a look at the table below to know the important dates related to the class 10 supplementary examination

AP SSC supplementary Exam 2019 Important Dates(tentative)
Availability of Time Table May 2019
Commencement of Examination 17 June 2019
Last Date of Examination 28 June 2019
Announcement of Result July 2019

How to Check AP SSC Supplementary Time Table 2019?

Every candidate needs to score the minimum qualifying percentage in order to pass the exam. However, if any candidate is not able to score the minimum marks, then they can appear for the re-examination. Moreover, the schedule for the compartment exams will be available online only. Candidates have to follow these basic steps to download the supplementary time table

Step.1 – Firstly, candidates need to go to the official website: http://main.bseap.org/.

Step.2 – After that, candidates will be able to see the new page.

Step.3 – On that page, candidates have to click on the “Supplementary Time Table 2019” link.

Step.4 – After that, the candidates will see the time table. Download it for future purposes.

Andhra Pradesh Board SSC Supplementary Examination 2019 Admit Cards

The admit cards will be published on the official website: http://main.bseap.org/.The candidate will be required to enter the Registration Number, Date of Birth, School Information  The admit card will have the following details

  1. Candidate’s Name
  2. Name of the School
  3. Name of the Father/Mother
  4. Code of Every Subject
  5. Examination Center
  6. Rules & Regulation
  7. Original Signature of Student

The candidates won’t be permitted to enter the exam hall without the hard copy of the admit card with them. No exceptions will be made under any circumstances.

Details Mentioned on Supplementary Time Table 2019

The supplementary time table plays a very important role for the students who have to appear for the examination again. However, time table also consists of some important details. The list of details is as follows:

  • Date and Day of the examination
  • Subject and Paper Name
  • Time of the Examination
  • Paper Code

Note: As per the previous year’s schedule, the examination will be held in the morning session from 9:30 am to 12:15 pm.

Important Instruction for Examination

While appearing for the re-examination, candidates need to keep in mind the following instructions which are as follows:

  • SSC Advanced Supplementary Examinations timetable will follow strictly even if the government declares a holiday on the scheduled dates.
  • Examination of the candidate will cancel if he answers the wrong combination of papers.
  • The performance of the candidate will cancel if he appears in the examination center which is not allowed to him.

Andhra Pradesh SSC Supplementary Examination 2019 Results

The Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education will declare the results of the AP SSC Supplementary Examination 2019 tentatively in the month of July 2019.

Keep visiting the official website for more updates. We wish you good luck for the examination!
