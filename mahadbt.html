MahaDBT Scholarship 2023 | Scholarship List, Eligibility, Rewards and Application Procedure

October 30, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

MahaDBT Scholarship 2023-2024: MahaDBT is a famous online scholarship portal of Maharashtra state run by the Government of Maharashtra. It is also said to be as MahaDBT – Aaple Sarkar DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer). It entertains around 38 post-matric scholarships administered by different departments under the state government. The scholars who are domiciled in Maharashtra can apply for relevant scholarships through MahaDBT gateway.

MahadBT

  • The application will open for 2023-24 session for merit-based schemes from 1st August – 31st August 2023 and for non-merit schemes, the deadline is from 1st August 2023 – 15th November 2023.
  • MahaDBT Scholarship 2023-24 last date for Merit Schemes is 31st August 2023.
  • MahaDBT Scholarship last date for Non-Merit Schemes is 10th October 2023.
  • Application process started for General category, Post-Matric scholarships 2023-2024 from 11 th October 2023. Last Date may be 31st Jan 2024.

Question 1.
What is MahaDBT?

Answer:
MahaDBT is a famous online scholarship portal of Maharashtra state run by the Government of Maharashtra. The eligible candidates can apply to the scholarship provided by different departments.

Question 2.
How to apply for MahaDBT Scholarships?

Answer:
IN order to apply for various scholarships available in MahaDBT Schemes, candidates have to visit the Aaple Sarkar MahaDBT portal and register themselves. Then follow the steps as instructed.

Question 3.
Who can apply to the Aaple Sarkar DBT Portal?

Answer:
Any Citizen of India can register on the portal. Eligibility and disbursement will be according to the schemes as displayed.

Question 4.
Is Aadhaar Number necessary to apply for MahaDBT?

Answer:
Yes, the Aadhaar number is necessary since the 2021-22 academic session. Without, Aadhaar students will not be allowed to fill the scholarship application form of MahaDBT.

Question 5.
What is Aaple Sarkar DBT?

Answer:
Aaple Sarkar DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) is a portal launched by the Government of Maharashtra to transfer the benefits and subsidies of various social welfare schemes like E-Scholarships, Pension, Disaster, etc., directly into the bank account of the beneficiary.

Question 6.
How Long is the OTP valid?

Answer:
OTP is valid for 30 minutes during registration to fill MahaDBT Scholarship Form 2023-24.

Question 7.
What is the last date of Mahadbt Scholarship?

Answer:
MahaDBT Scholarship form last date is October 2023.
