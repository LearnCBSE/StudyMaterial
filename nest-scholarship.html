NEST Scholarship 2023 | Nationwide Education and Scholarship Eligibility, Awards, Application Dates

November 3, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

NEST Scholarship 2023-24: Every year SEMCI INDIA conducts the Nationwide Education and Scholarship Test (NEST) in the month of January at their test centers in all major cities in India. The aim of carrying out a series of such periodic tests is to stimulate the interest of students in the pursuit of accurate knowledge and to prepare them for competitive skills such as CAT, GATE, GRE, TOEFL, and various other tests for admission to higher education in good institutes as well as for employment in reputable companies. Only bonafide students enrolled in colleges will be eligible for this scholarship for examinations in 2023. Class 12 Science students may also qualify for the NEST I test.

Question 1.
Who’s supposed to give the NEST test?

Answer:
Applicants need to take the NEST exam to be accepted to the five-year comprehensive MSc course in basic sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics) provided at NISER and UM-DAE CEBS.

Question 2.
Who is qualified for the NEST exam?

Answer:
Applicants who have obtained more than 60 percent of the aggregate marks (55 percent for SC/PwD candidates) in their Class 12 exams are eligible to take the NEST exam, provided they have a science stream at the 10 + 2 level. In addition, applicants should be born on or after August 1, 1999 (General and OBC categories) in order to meet the NEST eligibility criteria. The age waiver for five years is extended for members in the SC/ST/PwD class.
