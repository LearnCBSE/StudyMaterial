100 Top Engineering Colleges In India 2019-2020: NIRF Ranking Parameters

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is an MHRD authorized framework/methodology that is employed to rank educational institutes in the country. NIRF Ranks the top engineering colleges in India on the basis of the five most important parameters and their weightage. The following five parameters are considered by the NIRF-MHRD to rank all the best 100 engineering colleges in India :

  1. Teaching Learning and Resources
  2. Research and Professional Practice
  3. Perception
  4. Outreach and Inclusivity
  5. Graduation Outcome

Placements and Professional Scope provided by the Best Engineering Institutes in India

In terms of placements, engineering courses play an important role to improve the software boom in the country. All top IITs, government-owned universities like DTU, IIIT Hyderabad, IIIT Delhi offer the best placements for the engineering students.

Also, a few other Top Private India’s Engineering Colleges such as BITS, VIT, SRM, Amrita, etc. counted as the best engineering institutes in India when it comes to career opportunities. While choosing the engineering college, it is mandatory to check about the college in terms of placement aspect.

In 2019, 74.72 from the registered students got placed from IIT Bombay with an average salary of 12.32 LPA . In 2018. IIT Madras also saw good placements with salaries ranging from Rs. 4.8 lakhs to Rs. 20 lakhs per annum with an average salary of 10 LPA . Besides IITs, public engineering institutes and private colleges in India also provide good placements for all engineering students after their graduation.
