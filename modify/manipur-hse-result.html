Manipur 12th Result 2024 (Declared) | COHSEM HSE Result 2024 Check Here

January 12, 2024 by LearnCBSE Online

Manipur HSE Result 2024: Manipur Board 12th result 2024 has been finally declared on the official website of the Board. The result of Manipur Class 12 exam has been released online at manresults.nic.in. As confirmed by sources, COHSEM declared Manipur 12th result 2024 at 3 PM today.

State Board Results

The notification on the official website presently reads “Higher Secondary Examination 2024 (Declared on 08/05/2024)”. Students from across the state will be conveniently able to access their Manipur 12th result 2024 as it has been declared in the online mode. The Council of Higher Secondary Education, Manipur has published the Manipur Board Class 12 result 2024 online after its formal declaration.

Over 25000 students can finally access their Manipur Board Class 12 result 2024. COHSEM conducted the Manipur HSE 2024 examination from 19th February to 20th March 2024. The admit cards of Manipur HSE result 2024 were released in the month of February

The result is also available on the official website: http://manresults.nic.in/

Manipur HSE Result 2024

Last year, COHSEM Result was announced on May 03, 2024. But this year the board has released the result a bit late. Manipur Board has released the result for science, commerce and arts stream simultaneously. To know more important dates of COHSEM HSE Result 2024, check the table given below

Events Important Dates
The Commencement of the Examination 19 Feb 2024
The Last Date of Examination 20 Mar 2024
Result Announcement 03 May 2024 (at 3:00)

How to Check Manipur 12th Result 2024?

The link to check the result of the different stream will be the same. There will be a single link to view Science, Commerce, and Arts result. To know the steps to check COHSEM HSE Result 2024, you can follow the points given below:

Step – 1: Go to the official website. For this cause, you can click on the direct link provided on this page.

Step – 2: On the homepage, you will get the tab “Results”. So, hit that tab.

Step – 3: After clicking the tab, you will be redirected to the page where you will get the list of the various results.

Step – 4: Now, all you need to click the link of “Manipur 12th Result 2024”. Once, you will hit the link, you will be able to see a new page.

Step – 5: On that new page, you will have to enter the credentials. Thus, enter your roll number and search for your result.

Step – 6: After checking the result, you are advised to take the printout of the result for the future reference.

Manipur 12th Science Result 2024

The candidates who have chosen Science stream will get the results very soon. it is very important to know for them that what are the options will be available for them. The students of Science stream will be able to make their career in technology such as B.Tech, BCA, BBA, etc. You can also opt the stream in MBBS, BDS, etc.

Manipur 12th Arts Result 2024

There is a myth that the Arts stream does not has many career options. But in this section, we will clarify this illusion. Arts Stream is as popular as Science and Commerce among the students. The result of this stream is increasing every year. After the exams, hopefully, this number will get increased. The students should keep track of this page. It will be updated on the official announcement.

Manipur 12th Commerce Result 2024

This stream has become very popular among the students. There are various career options after Commerce. So it is very beneficial for the candidates who have their interest in this stream. Students will be able to make a career in Charted Accountancy, B.Com, B.Sc., etc.

Manipur 12th Supplementary Result 2024

The candidates who will not be able to secure the passing marks will be called for the supplementary exams. The students will have to fill out an application form for it mentioning the subjects for which you will have to give the exams. Once, the exams will over, the board will announce the Manipur 12th Supplementary Result 2024. The supplementary result will be declared online on the official website i.e. manresults.nic.in. In order to get the supplementary results, you will have to submit the roll number and the result will appear to you.
