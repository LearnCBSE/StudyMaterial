Top 4 Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Kazakhstan For Indian Students

September 11, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Kazakhstan: Preparing Yourself ready to study MBBS in Kazakhstan? Then look into this article below to know the disadvantages of studying MBBS in Kazakhstan. No country has only advantages without disadvantages when it comes to studying MBBS Abroad.

In that, Kazakhstan is also one. Kazakhstan has rapidly grown in popularity for Indian students. In this article, we are going to look at the disadvantages that you will face if you go to study MBBS in Kazakhstan. Read and decide!!!

Top Most Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Kazakhstan

As we all know, every country has advantages and disadvantages of studying MBBS in Abroad for Indian students. And we need to look at both of them and then we need to decide whether to study in that country or not.

You may have seen the advantages of Kazakhstan, Here look at the disadvantages of studying MBBS in Kazakhstan for Indian students.

  • Climatic Conditions
  • Foreign Language Study
  • Fewer Experience Universities
  • Low FMGE Passing Percentage

Climatic Conditions

Climate change is one of the biggest disadvantages of studying MBBS in Kazakhstan. Because The weather condition is different in India and Kazakhstan. Kazakhstan is colder than India but it is less colder compared with Russia and Ukraine.

Most popular MBBS universities are available in Almaty Astana and in Semey. People who are thinking of going to study MBBS in Kazakhstan need to check the weather conditions in these states. And if you think you can bear this cold, then you can go.

People who think it is difficult to bear the cold can change their minds to study MBBS in Kazakhstan. Though the building is centrally heated it will be difficult to walk outside.

Foreign Language Study

If you want to study MBBS in Kazakhstan, you should not only give time to your MBBS study But also you need to give time to learn a new language.

If you want to interact with local people and patients during their stay and clinical years they want to communicate in the local language.

Some students find it easy but for some students, it will be a difficult task. Even it is mandatory to learn the local language and it will be available in the course curriculum, so you cannot avoid it.

Fewer Experience Universities

Students who are interested in studying MBBS in Kazakhstan need to choose the university very carefully because there are many universities that have just started teaching MBBS courses.

If the students do not do proper research on joining universities, fake agents will definitely cheat them by getting them admission to low-quality universities.

So research and get a seat in top quality universities for MBBS study. And also join the university by knowing the medium. As you need to understand the course easily.

Low FMGE Passing Percentage

Coming to the FMGE pass percentage, it will be very low in countries like Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan which is about 10%. The pass percentage is also low because of the fact that the students will not take their studies seriously.

It might be another reason too, they might take the low-budget and low-quality universities. Such a university will not have the optimum education standard.

Because of that students will have the bad result.
