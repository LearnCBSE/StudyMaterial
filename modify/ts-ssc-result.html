TS SSC Result 2019: The TS SSC Result will be declared by Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE), Telangana on its official website i.e.bsetelangana.org.  Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education (TSBIE) is going to release results for Class 10th 2019 on May 13, 2019, at 11:30 AM Onwards, on its official Website. The exam was held from March 16 to April 2, 2019. Read on to find more about the procedure to check the results online.

BSE Telangana SSC Results – How to Check the Results?

Online Mode

The results of BSE Telangana SSC Class 10 will be available online on bsetelangana.org. Follow the steps given below to check the results.

  1. Go to the official website bsetelangana.org
  2. Click on the “Results” tab
  3. Enter your roll number as well as your date of birth in the space provided.
  4. Click on “Get Result”
  5. The result page will open

Offline Mode/SMS

Candidates can check their results through SMS Mode also.

TS10ROLL NUMBER and Send it to 56263

Details Mentioned on the TS SSC Results Scorecard

The following information will be listed on the results page of the candidate

  1. Name of the Candidate
  2. Roll Number of the Candidate
  3. Name of the Institute
  4. Subject – Wise Marks Secured
  5. Subject – Wise Passed or Failed Status
  6. Subject – Wise Maximum Marks
  7. Subject – Wise Marks Breakdown
  8. Overall Marks secured
  9. Overall percentages secured
  10. Status qualified or disqualified
  11. Rank in the class

The candidates are advised to download the soft copy of the result for future uses.
