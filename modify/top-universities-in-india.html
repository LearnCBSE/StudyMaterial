Placements and Job Prospects Offered by Top Universities in India

One of the most important parameters that have been utilized to define top universities in India is graduation outcomes. Moreover, this is shattered into few parameters like placements or entrepreneurship opportunities, median salary, number of Ph.D. students and graduates allowed to top universities.

As per the data released by NIRF for engineering colleges, the average median salary of a graduating student from a four-year academic programme improved by 7.72 percent during 2017-18 and 2018-19. As opposed to this, the median salary of a student graduating from a five-year integrated Programme is increased by 20.34 percent through the same reference period.

An average median salary of an IIT graduate ranges from INR 8 LPA to INR 14.50 LPA based on whether this is a new IIT or an old IIT. For both JNU and BHU, median to max salary ranges from INR 3.25 LPA to INR 10 LPA. Whereas for Jadavpur University, this figure ranges from INR 5 LPA to INR 14 LPA. So, make your mind to get admission in the best university in India to set your career for future years.
