Maharashtra HSC Supplementary Exam 2019 | Time Table (Released), Admit Card, Results

July 5, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

Maharashtra HSC/12th Supplementary Exam 2019: The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education is a statutory and autonomous body established under the “Maharashtra Secondary Boards Act” 1965. The Board is to conduct the SSC for 10th class and HSC for 12th class examinations. Every Year the Board organizes the Class 10th, 12th Main Examination in the February/March Month and Supplementary Examination in the July Month.

How To Apply for Maharashtra HSC Supplementary Exam 2019?

It is vital that you apply before the last date and not miss this opportunity. So, apply immediately by following these easy steps:

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. Under the “Latest news” section, look for “HSC Supplementary application form 2019” link.
  3. Click on it.
  4. Register using the relevant details. Click submit.
  5. It will redirect you to a new payment page where you will have to pay the registration fee.
  6. Download your application and keep it safe for future reference.

Maharashtra HSC Supplementary Exam Admit Card 2019

It is mandatory for you to have the admit card at hand when writing the exam. You won’t be allowed inside the exam hall without it. Hence, release it as soon as it is released in the month of July form the official website. Follow the below steps to obtain your admit card:

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. Click on the “HSC” tab.
  3. You will need to log in. You can do so using the provided details.
  4. Click on the “HSC Supplementary admit card 2019” link.
  5. Download the admit card and take a print out of it. You might want to take multiple prints to be on the safe side.

Your admit card will contain your details such as name, roll number, etc. Make sure to check for any errors or discrepancies. If there are any, inform the concerned officials immediately.

Maharashtra HSC Supplementary Exam Time Table 2019

As there is not much time available to you until the supplementary exams begin, you need to make sure that not a single moment is unproductive. To make a fully efficient study plan, you need access to the official time table of the supplementary exams. These steps will help you download the same

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. Click on the “HSC” tab.
  3. You will need to log in. You can do so using the provided details.
  4. Click on the “HSC Supplementary time table 2019” link.
  5. Download the admit card and take a print out of it for convenience.

Note: that the dates may change at any time. Hence, it would be wise to keep yourself updated. Visit the official website for any new notifications regularly until the exams end.

How To Check Maharashtra HSC Supplementary Exam Results 2019?

The results will be released in the online mode, tentatively in the month of August. You can check your results from the official website. You can finally see all your hard work pay off then! It is very easy to check your results:

  1. Visit the official website (link given above).
  2. Click on the “HSC” tab.
  3. You will need to log in. You can do so using the provided details.
  4. Click on the “HSC Supplementary Results 2019” link.
  5. Download the result and take a print out of it for future reference.

Note that you won’t be allowed to attempt the supplementary exam without registering, hence register as soon as possible.
