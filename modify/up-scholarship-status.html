UP Scholarship Status 2020 | Know How To Check UP Scholarship Status from Here

March 4, 2020 by LearnCBSE Online

UP Scholarship Status 2019-2020: UP Scholarship (उत्तर प्रदेश स्कालरशिप) is one of the biggest scholarships provided by the Government of Uttar Pradesh. It provides financial assistance to meritorious students from economically weaker sections of society. The scheme is, however, open to students of all categories. Candidates can check them UP scholarship status on the official site.

How To Check UP Scholarship Status? – यूपी छात्रवृत्ति की स्थिति कैसे चेक करें?

  • Candidates must visit the official site of the scholarship at http://scholarship.up.nic.in/ .

  • Click on the “Status” tab on the website’s header navigation.
  • Enter the candidate’s registration number and the date of birth in the given fields.
  • Click “search”. The candidate will be presented the status of the application on the screen.
  • Download or take a print out for future references.
