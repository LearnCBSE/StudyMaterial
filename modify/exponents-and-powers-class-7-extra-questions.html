Question 1.
Express 343 as a power of 7.
Solution:
We have 343 = 7 × 7 × 7 = 7 3
Thus, 343 = 7 3

Question 2.
Which is greater 3 2 or 2 3 ?
Solution:
We have 3 2 = 3 × 3 = 9
2 3 = 2 × 2 × 2 = 8
Since 9 > 8
Thus, 3 2 > 2 3

Question 3.
Express the following number as a powers of prime factors:
(i) 144
(ii) 225
Solution:
(i) We have
144 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 = 2 4 × 3 2
Thus, 144 = 2 4 × 3 2

(ii) We have
225 = 3 × 3 × 5 × 5 = 3 2 × 5 2
Thus, 225 = 3 2 × 5 2

Question 4.
Find the value of:
(i) (-1) 1000
(ii) (1) 250
(iii) (-1) 121
(iv) (10000) 0
Solution:
(i) (-1) 1000 = 1 [∵ (-1) even number = 1]
(ii) (1) 250 = 1 [∵ (1) even number = 1]
(iii) (-1) 121 = -1 [∵ (-1) odd number = -1]
(iv) (10000) 0 = 1 [∵ a 0 = 1]

Question 5.
Express the following in exponential form:
(i) 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 × 5
(ii) 4 × 4 × 4 × 5 × 5 × 5
(iii) (-1) × (-1) × (-1) × (-1) × (-1)
(iv) a × a × a × b × c × c × c × d × d
Solution:
(i) 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 = (5) 5
(ii) 4 × 4 × 4 × 5 × 5 × 5 = 4 3 × 5 3
(iii) (-1) × (-1) × (-1) × (-1) × (-1) = (-1) 5
(iv) a × a × a × b × c × c × c × d × d = a 3 b 1 c 3 d 2

Question 6.
Express each of the following as product of powers of their prime factors:
(i) 405
(ii) 504
(iii) 500
Solution:
(i) We have
405 = 3 × 3 × 3 × 3 × 5 = 3 4 × 5 1
Thus, 405 = 3 4 × 5 1

(ii) We have
504 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 7 = 2 3 × 3 2 × 7 1
Thus, 504 = 2 3 × 3 2 × 7 1

(iii) We have
500 = 2 × 2 × 5 × 5 × 5 = 2 2 × 5 3
Thus, 500 = 2 2 × 5 3

Question 7.
Simplify the following and write in exponential form:
(i) (5 2 ) 3
(ii) (2 3 ) 3
(iii) (a b ) c
(iv) [(5) 2 ] 2
Solution:
(i) (5 2 ) 3 = 5 2×3 = 5 6
(ii) (2 3 ) 3 = 2 3×3 = 2 9
(iii) (a b ) c = a b×c = a bc
(iv) [(5) 2 ] 2 = 5 2×2 = 5 4

Question 11.
Express each of the following as a product of prime factors is the exponential form:
(i) 729 × 125
(ii) 384 × 147
Solution:
(i) 729 × 125 = 3 × 3 × 3 × 3 × 3 × 3 × 5 × 5 × 5 = 3 6 × 5 3
Thus, 729 × 125 = 3 6 × 5 3

(ii) 384 × 147 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 7 × 7 = 2 7 × 3 2 × 7 2
Thus, 384 × 147 = 2 7 × 3 2 × 7 2

Question 12.
Simplify the following:
(i) 10 3 × 9 0 + 3 3 × 2 + 7 0
(ii) 6 3 × 7 0 + (-3) 4 – 9 0
Solution:
(i) 10 3 × 9 0 + 3 3 × 2 + 7 0
= 1000 + 54 + 1
= 1055
(ii) 6 3 × 7 0 + (-3) 4 – 9 0
= 216 × 1 + 81 – 1
= 216 + 80
= 296

Question 13.
Write the following in expanded form:
(i) 70,824
(ii) 1,69,835
Solution:
(i) 70,824
= 7 × 10000 + 0 × 1000 + 8 × 100 + 2 × 10 + 4 × 10 0
= 7 × 10 4 + 8 × 10 2 + 2 × 10 1 + 4 × 10 0
(ii) 1,69,835
= 1 × 100000 + 6 × 10000 + 9 × 1000 + 8 × 100 + 3 × 10 + 5 × 10 0
= 1 × 10 5 + 6 × 10 4 + 9 × 10 3 + 8 × 10 2 + 3 × 10 1 + 5 × 10 0

Question 16.
Find the value of
(a) 3 0 ÷ 4 0
(b) (8 0 – 2 0 ) ÷ (8 0 + 2 0 )
(c) (2 0 + 3 0 + 4 0 ) – (4 0 – 3 0 – 2 0 )
Solution:
(a) We have 3 0 ÷ 4 0 = 1 ÷ 1 = 1 [∵ a 0 = 1]
(6) (8 0 – 2 0 ) ÷ (8 0 + 2 0 ) = (1 – 1) ÷ (1 + 1) = 0 ÷ 2 = 0
(c) (2 0 + 3 0 + 4 0 ) – (4 0 – 3 0 – 2 0 )
= (1 + 1 + 1) – (1 – 1 – 1) [∵ a 0 = 1]
= 3 – 1
= 2
