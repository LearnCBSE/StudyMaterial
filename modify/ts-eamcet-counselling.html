TS EAMCET Counselling 2019 (Started) | Registration, Document Verification, Seat Allotment, Dates

July 9, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

TS EAMCET Counselling 2019: TS EAMCET Counselling will start once the TS EAMCET Results release. TS EAMCET Results have been released on June 10th, 2019. Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) has started the counseling process for admission to engineering and agriculture programs from June 24, 2019. The TS EAMCET counseling will take place in three rounds for all the selected candidates. The candidates will have to register for the counseling round and pay the counseling fees on the official website, in order to sit through the counseling. The seat allotment will take place according to the rank secured by the candidates. Read on to find more about the TS EAMCET Counselling 2019 procedure

TS EAMCET Counselling 2019 – List of Documents Required

The following documents are needed for the TS EAMCET Counselling 2019:

  1. TS EAMCET 2019 rank card
  2. TS EAMCET 2019 hall ticket
  3. Aadhaar card
  4. SSC or its equivalent mark sheet
  5. 6th standard to inter-study certificates
  6. Transfer Certificate (T.C)
  7. Intermediate or it’s equivalent memo-cum-pass certificate
  8. Income certificate issued after Jan 1, 2018 (if applicable)
  9. Caste certificate issued by the competent authority (if applicable)
  10. Residence certificate of either of parents in Telangana for a period of 10 years in case Non-Local candidates
  11. Physically challenged (PH)/Children of Armed Personnel (CAP)/NCC/Sports/Minority certificate if applicable
  12. Residence certificate in case where the candidate has no institutionalized education

TS EAMCET 2019 Counselling Procedure

The following steps are to be kept in mind while applying for TS EAMCET Counselling 2019

  • Registration and Fee Payment on the official website @ tseamcet.nic.in
    • For OBC category Counselling fees is 1200/-
    • For SC/ST Category Counselling fees is 600/-
  • Document Verification: The candidates will have to report to the assigned helpline centers for the verification of the documents
  • Choice locking: The choice filling will take place online on https://tseamcet.nic.in
  • Seat Allotment: The seats will be allotted based on the preference and the rank secured  by the candidate
  • Payment of tuition fees online and self-reporting

TS EAMCET Counselling 2019 Eligibility Criteria

  1. Candidates should have Indian Nationality
  2. Candidate should belong to the State of Telangana /Andhra Pradesh
  3. The candidate should have completed 16 years of age as on D In respect December 31, 2019, for Undergraduate Professional Courses in Engineering (including Technology ) and Pharmacy

TS EAMCET Counselling 2019 – Cancellation of Candidature

If the candidates cancel the admission, the following will happen according to the circumstances under which the admission was canceled

  1. If the candidate cancels the provisionally allotted seat through online on or before the TS EAMCET cut off date notified for cancellation, after payment of tuition fees in the first phase, full Tuition Fee paid will be refunded
  2. 100% refund after final phase for University Colleges
  3. 50% after the final phase and before cut off date notified for cancellation.
