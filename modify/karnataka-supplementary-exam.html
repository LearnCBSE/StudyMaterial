Karnataka 2nd PUC Supplementary Exam 2019: The Karnataka PUC board conducts the Karnataka 2nd PUC supplementary Board examination for candidates who were not able to pass the main board examination (class 12th). Karnataka 2nd PUC Board examination 2019 has started from 11 June 2019 and will end on 20 June 2019. The result will be declared in the month of July tentatively. How To Download Karnataka 2nd PUC Supplementary Hall Ticket 2019?

The steps to download the hall tickets are listed below

  • Step 1 – visit the official website i.e www.pue.kar.nic.in
  • Step 2 – under the section ‘ circulars’ click on the Karnataka 2nd PUC hall ticket 2019 link.
  • Step 3 – log in using necessary personal as well as academic details.
  • Step 4 – candidates need to enter their registration number to get their hall tickets.
  • Step 5 – as soon as candidates enter their registration number, hall ticket will be displayed.
  • Step 5 – candidates can take a printout for the examination purpose.

Karnataka 2nd PUC Supplementary Result 2019

The PUC board will release the Karnataka 2nd PUC supplementary result 2019 online on the official website i.e www.karresults.nic.in. The result will be declared most probably in the month of July. Candidates can check their result by entering their roll number.

How to Download Karnataka 2nd PUC Supplementary Result 2019?

  • Step 1-visit the official website i.e www.karresults.nic.in.
  • Step 2- click on the Karnataka 2nd PUC  supplementary result link.
  • Step 3-enter the roll number and click on submit button.
  • Step 4-in new window, the result will be displayed.
  • Step 5- take a printout of the Karnataka 2nd PUC supplementary result 2019 for future use.
