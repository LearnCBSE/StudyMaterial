CBSE Class 10 Compartment Exam Admit Card 2019 – Steps to Download

  • Step 1: Visit the official website, cbse.nic.in
  • Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘ school login ‘ or ‘ private login ‘ next to admit card for compartment exam July 2019 under ‘recent announcement’
  • Step 3: A new page will open .
  • Step 4: Log-in using credentials .
  • Step 5: Admit card will appear on the screen. Download and take a print out for further reference .

CBSE Compartment Exam Class 10th Eligibility 2019

Below are the eligibility list for class 10th compartment exam conducted by CBSE

  • Only in one or two subjects, students can appear for compartment exam.
  • Students who have placed EIOP (Eligible for Improvement of Performance), appeared for the class 10th exam, is eligible for compartment exam.
  • Students are in the category of EIOP, will be given a single chance to attempt compartment exam. But if they failed in that exam, they will be given NIOP (Not Eligible for Improvement of Performance) in their results and cannot appear for a second chance.
