Google Scholarships 2023-2024 | Dates, Application Process, Rewards

October 31, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Google Scholarships 2023-2024: Google Scholarships is a lead of Google Inc. that strives at providing financial assistance to candidates who aim to pursue a career in technology. It is a general term that includes a number of scholarships and competitions that are available for candidates globally. Every year, Google grants opportunities for candidates in schools, women in technology, developers, and individuals to build their image in the technological world. A Google scholarships program not only presents the candidates with financial assistance but also grants them a chance to learn through workshops and training.

In this article, though Google grants scholarships to candidates across the world, we have only included scholarships that are available for Indian candidates. You can also see some of the famous Google competitions listed in the article that is available for school candidates. In honor of Venkat’s memory, we are offering Google Venkat Panchapakesan Memorial Scholarship in India to students who are passionate, and ever-optimistic. and want to use computer science to make the world a more sustainable place. The article presents a list of each Google scholarship for Indian Students 2023 and contests such as important dates, eligibility requirements, application procedure, and reward details.

Google Scholarships Eligibility Criteria

Every Google scholarship begins with a designated set of eligibility requirements. While each scholarship noted in the table are suitable for candidates with Indian citizenship, there are a few other requirements as well that a candidate requires to accomplish. The contests noted below are specially meant for school candidates of classes 1 to 10. To know the detailed eligibility criteria for each scholarship, read the article below.

Udacity-Google Developer Scholarship

  • The scholarship is available only for Indian residents.
  • The applicants must be at least 18 years of age.

Women Techmakers Scholars Program (formerly known as Google Anita Borg Memorial Scholarship Program)

  • The scholarship is available for female applicants only (including undergraduate or graduate applicants).
  • Candidates must be registered at a recognized university for the present academic year.
  • The applicants who are completing their graduation in 2022 and beyond can apply for the 2023-24 academic year.
  • Candidates must be persevering studies in computer science, computer engineering or any other relevant technical field.
  • Candidates must be able to prove a strong academic record and enthusiasm for increasing the association of women in computer science.

Google Venkat Panchapakesan Memorial Scholarship

  • The scholarship is available for undergraduate and graduate candidates who are enrolled in a university/college in India.
  • Candidates must be seeking a degree program in computer engineering, computer science or other related fields.

Google Conference and Travel Scholarships

  • The scholarship is available for candidates worldwide.
  • To attend a conference in India, the applicant must be enrolled in a recognized university.
  • Candidate must be in need of an organization travel fund.
  • Candidates must have a paper received with a top-tier organization on computer science.
  • Preference will be given to a conference whose research area is well related to Google’s interest.
  • The applicant is required to be the primary author of the accepted paper.

Doodle 4 Google India Contest

  • Candidates of class 1 to 10 are eligible to enter the contest.
  • Candidates need to have permission from their parents/guardians/school teachers.
  • The contest is not available for employees, contractors, interns, officials and representatives or affiliates.

Google India Code to Learn Contest

The contest is available for Indian candidates of class 5 to 10 under the following groups:

  • Class 5 and 6
  • Class 7 and 8
  • Class 9 and 10

Note: The candidates can enter the contest only through their respective computer teachers.

Generation Google Scholarship

  • Female applicants who are studying Computer Science or any other related field are eligible to apply.
  • They must be in 1st year or 2nd-year students in a bachelor’s program at an accredited university.
  • They must demonstrate a strong academic record.
  • Apply online through the official Google website

Applying for Google scholarships is simple and easy. Though each scholarship and contest require candidates to fill different applications, the process to follow for all is online. The application form for each scholarship becomes live for a limited time duration only. Thus, the candidates are required to keep track of the application timeline and apply before the deadline. Google Scholarships Rewards

Know here the benefits that a candidate will get through the Google scholarship portal. Also, get to know, how these scholarships will help the candidates and how they can use the scholarship reward. The list below gives complete information about the rewards given to the candidates under each scholarship. While some scholarships grant money to candidates to reach the educational costs, some give learning material for the candidates to explore.

Udacity Scholarship

Access to online courses of Udacity. The scholarship is given in two phases:

  • Phase 1 grants 1000 challenge scholarships to candidates for 3 months of courses.
  • Phase 2 grants 1000 full Nanodegree scholarships to candidates for 6 months of courses.

Women Techmakers Scholars Program

  • An academic scholarship
  • An opportunity for the candidates to attend the annual Women Techmakers Scholars’ Retreat.
  • The fellows can also participate in various training and workshops and connect with Google mentors as well as fellow scholars.

Google Venkat Panchapakesan Memorial Scholarship

  • The financial reward of USD 750 towards the expenses related to tuition and education.
  • An opportunity to visit the headquarters of YouTube in California, USA.

Google Conference and Travel Scholarships

  • USD 1,000 to USD 3,000 covering the cost of conference registration, accommodation, travel and other related expenses.

Doodle 4 Google India Contest

  • The national winner will receive a college scholarship of INR 5 Lakh, technology package worth INR 2 Lakh, certificate/trophy of achievement and more.
  • The group winners will receive trophy/medal of recognition, certificate of achievement and more.
  • The national finalist will also receive a certificate of achievement.

Google India Code to Learn Contest

  • Achievement certificate
  • A Chromebook laptop or equivalent gift

Generation Google Scholarship

  • USD 1000 (INR 75,000)

Does Google give scholarships?

Answer:
Google presents six big scholarships for candidates all around the world. Candidates who are studying computer science or a closely related field will qualify.

What is Udacity Scholarship?

Answer:
Udacity gives lifelong education for millions to acquire the new abilities they need for the jobs they need. Udacity scholarships make this opportunity more available for students all around the globe.

What are the Google Scholarships provided for Indian candidates?

Question 4.
What is a Google scholarship?

Answer:
The Generation Google Scholarship 2020 is education and scholarship programs aim to inspire and help students become future leaders in computing and technology by breaking down the barriers that prevent them from entering these fields.

Question 5.
How can I get Google scholarship in India?

Answer:
