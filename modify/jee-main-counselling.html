JEE Main Counselling 2019 and Seat Allotment Process

How to Apply for JEE Main Counselling 2019 and Seat Allotment Process

As we have already mentioned, the JEE Main Counseling will take place online. Therefore, the candidates have to register themselves and participate in counseling. They have to complete various stages while applying for the Counselling Round. Hence, the step by step procedure to apply for the JEE Main 2019 Counseling Round is as follows-

Registration Round

This is the First Stage of the JEE Main Counseling. Candidates have to visit the JoSAA Official Website to register. To start with the registration, the candidates have to enter their Roll Number and Password and start completing the next stages.

Choice Filling and Locking

In this section, a list of the colleges and courses will be displayed on the screen. Hence, on the basis of your preferences, select the colleges and courses. Thus, on the basis of your choices provided, the seat allotment will be done. Once you are sure with the choices, click on the lock choices to complete. Candidates must note that, once the choices are locked, you cannot modify it again.

Seat Allotment

On the basis of the availability of the seats, merit scores, and preferences, the candidates will be allotted seats in the colleges. However, before the actual seat allotment round, a mock allotment round will take place. This will give you an idea of the working of the Counselling of JEE Main 2019.

Payment of Fees and Seat Acceptance

After the allotment of the seat, the candidates have to perform any one of the following

  • Freeze- If the candidate is satisfied with the seat allotment and do not wish to participate in the further counseling rounds.
  • Float â€“ If in case, you want to keep the allotted seat and also want to upgrade the seat, then you can choose this option.
  • Slide- If you want to keep the allotted seat and want to upgrade the course in the same institute, and then you need to choose this option.
  • Withdraw- If the candidate has already accepted the seat then they can withdraw the seat by reporting the Institute allotted before the last round of the seat allotment .

Now to confirm your seat, the candidates have to make a payment of a certain amount. The payment can be either done through SBI Challan or Internet Banking. The Seat Acceptance Fee for the Unreserved Category is Rs 45, 000. Whereas, the seat acceptance fees for the SC/ST/SC-PWD, ST-PWD, GEN-PWD, OBC-PWD Category is Rs 20, 000.

Documents Required for Verification

After making the payment of the seat acceptance fees, the candidates have to visit the reporting centers and get their Document Verification done. Hence, in the document verification round, the candidates have to submit the following documents. Hence, the lists of the documents are as follows-

  • Three Passport Size Photograph (the one submitted at the time of filling the online form)
  • JEE Main 2019 Admit Card
  • Provisional JoSAA Seat Allotment Letter
  • 12th Class Performance Check
  • Undertaking by Candidate
  • Valid Proof of Identity Card (issued by govt./last attended school/12th class admit card)
  • Receipt of Seat Acceptance Fee Payment
  • Date of Birth Proof (10th Class Mark Sheet)
  • JEE Main 2019 Score Card
  • Class XII Mark Sheet & passing Certificate (if Available)
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical Certificate
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
  • OCI certificate or PIO card
  • Registration-cum-locked choices for seat allotment

Final Reporting to the Allotted Institute

Soon after the Document Verification Round, the candidates have to report the Reporting Center and pay the balance fees and confirm their admission.

JEE Main 2019 Special Round of Counselling

Soon after the JoSAA Counselling Round, the CSAB will be conducting a special round of counseling. This Counselling will be similar to that of the JoSAA.

CSAB NEUT Counseling 2019

  • This counseling will take place for filing up supernumerary seats in the North-East Region and Union Territories.
  • The Central Seat Allocation Board is responsible for the counseling round.
  • The Counselling Round will start from April 2019 onwards for the qualified North-East and Union Territory Candidates.

CSAB SFTI Counseling 2019

  • This counseling process will offer admission to Self-Financing Technician Institutes
  • The Allocation of the seats will be on the basis of the preferences submitted by the candidate.
  • All India Rank in Paper I and II will be considered for seat allocation.
  • Around 34 Institutes are participating under CSAB Counseling 2019

Thus, the JEE Main Counselling 2019, is an Online Counselling Process that will allow the qualified candidates to make their choices of Colleges and Courses. More than 20, 000 Institutes will be participating in this Counselling Round. The JEE Main 2019 Counseling will take place from 2 nd week of June 2019 onwards in seven rounds.
