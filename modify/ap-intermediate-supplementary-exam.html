AP Intermediate Supplementary Exam 2019: For candidates who scored less than 35% in the Intermediate Examination will be given a second chance to increase their scores by applying for the AP Intermediate Supplementary Examination. The Board of Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP) will conduct the examination from May 14, 2019. The details are available below. Read on to find more about the timetable, the procedure to apply and other information.

AP Intermediate Supplementary Examination 2019 Results

The Board of Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP) will declare the results of the AP Intermediate Supplementary Examination in the month of June 2019. The Board is responsible for conducting Class 10 and 12 examinations, as well as supplementary examination, and declare the results for the same. AP Intermediate Supplementary Examination 2019 Admit Card

The admit cards have been released on the official website. Follow the steps to download the admit cards for AP Intermediate Supplementary Examination 2019

  1. Go to bieap.gov.in or jnanabhumi.ap.gov.in.
  2. Click on “Download Hall Tickets”
  3. Enter the application number
  4. The admit card will be generated
  5. Take a printout of the same to be carried to the examination centre

AP Intermediate Supplementary Examination 2019 Instructions to the Candidates

  1. Do not forget to carry your admit cards
  2. Do not carry any other document or any electronic gadget
  3. Reach the examination centre well before the reporting time
