Sarla Devi Scholarship 2023-2024 | Dates, Eligibility, Application Process, Rewards

November 8, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Sarla Devi Scholarship 2023-2024: Sarla Devi Scholarship Scheme is an action taken by Dharampal Satyapal Charitable Trust which is supported by the DS Group. This is a merit-cum-means scholarship program that provides for the needs of worthy and indigent students who are incapable to proceed with education due to financial challenges and some personal crises. This scholarship scheme enables students with the education and gives them a hit to work towards a greater academic future. Under the Sarla Devi Scholarship plan, the meritorious students of Delhi NCR get the chance to avail of scholarship compensation of up to INR 75,000.

Sarla Devi Scholarship for Students in Crisis

Commenced to support exemplary students of an economically underprivileged section of society to persevere in education, the scholarship offers up to INR 75,000 to students who are studying degree programs in Science, Arts, Commerce, and Vocational streams.

Under the Sarla Devi Scholarship for Students in Crisis, those financially weak students who are encountering some kind of individual crisis are eligible to avail INR 10,000 to finish their education.

In this section, we have included the complete details about this merit-cum-means scholarship plan. Information such as Scholarship amount, key dates, eligibility conditions, the application process, necessary documents and more are represented here. Sarla Devi Scholarship Rewards

The applicants selected under the Sarla Devi Scholarship plan receive financial support so that Candidates do not meet any monetary restraint while persevering education. The details of the scholarship rewards are given in the table below.

Name of the scholarship Sarla Devi Scholarship Benefits
Sarla Devi Scholarship Each of the 14 students persevering degree courses in Science gets a scholarship amount of INR 75,000.
Each of the 50 applicants pursuing degree courses in Arts course receives INR 20,000.
Each of the 40 students studying degree courses in Commerce course gets a scholarship of INR 25,000.
65 applicants who are persevering degree courses in Vocational subjects get a scholarship amount of INR 15,000 each.

Sarla Devi Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the scholarships, the candidates must be registered in the prescribed colleges/institutes of Delhi NCR, which includes parts of three neighboring states of Haryana, Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan along with the combination of the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi. Sarla Devi Scholarship Eligibility for Commerce courses

  • Candidates must be from either Arts, Science or Commerce background
  • Students registered in any of the following colleges/institutes are eligible

Sarla Devi Scholarship Application Process

The application procedure of both the scholarships under the Sarla Devi Scholarship plan is simple and easy for candidates to understand. Follow the steps given below to apply for any of the scholarships by clicking on the Sarla Devi Scholarship 2023 application link.

  • First of all, all applicants are required to visit the official web portal of the Sarla Devi Scholarship 2023.
  • Now, applicants will have to register using Facebook, Google or Email Account.
  • A unique registration id and Password will be received by the applicants on their registered mobile number.
  • Login with the same credentials, to begin the application process.
  • Fill all necessary details required by the application form accurately.
  • Upload all the required documents of the same size as per the necessity of the application page.
  • Finally, click on the “Submit” button.
  • Take a hard copy of the application form so that it will be used in the future.

Documents Required for Sarla Devi Scholarship

While filling out the application form, the candidates must make sure that they have scanned copies of all the required documents with them. The necessary documents that scholars need to have a copy of are listed below.

  • Candidates latest passport-size photograph
  • Marksheets of previous exams
  • Income certificate
  • Candidates admission letter
  • Candidates identity proof (Aadhaar card)

FAQ’s On Sarla Devi Scholarship

Question 1.
What is the Sarla Devi Scholarship?

Answer:
Sarla Devi Scholarship is a step taken by the Dharampal Satyapal Charitable Trust to promote the education of exemplary students belonging to financially deprived families. The purpose of this merit cum means scholarship is to provide students with an opportunity for a bigger future. The scholarship program is for Science, Arts, Commerce, and Vocational studies pursuing students. Candidates who are around Delhi NCR could get a scholarship of up to INR 75,000 or tuition fees subject to satisfying the eligibility criteria for the scholarship.

Question 2.
Who is offering this Sarla Devi scholarship?

Answer:
The scholarship is being offered by Dharampal Satyapal Charitable Trust.

Question 3.
What are the benefits under the Sarla Devi Scholarship?

Answer:
Students chosen for the scholarship will get the following advantages:

  • 14 scholars from the Science programs will get a scholarship of INR 75,000
  • 50 scholars from the Arts programs will get a scholarship of INR 20,000
  • 40 scholars from the Commerce programs will get a scholarship of INR 25,000
  • 65 scholars from the Vocational programs will get a scholarship of INR 15,000

Question 4.
What are the documents needed for the Sarla Devi Scholarship 2023?

Answer:

  • Recent passport size photograph
  • Previous mark sheets
  • Income Proof (Salary Slip/Income Tax Return/Income Certificate/Affidavit)
  • Admission Proof (Admission Letter/Current year Institute Identity Card/Current Year Fee Receipt)
  • Identity Proof/Aadhar
