TBSE HS Supplementary Exam 2019 | Time Table (Released) , Admit Card, Result

July 6, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

TBSE HS Supplementary Exam 2019: TBSE Class 12 Supplementary Exam 2019 is conducted for students who have failed in a subject or exam. None of the students want to get fail or repeat the same class, but if they fail in a subject in the board exam, they are given with an opportunity by Tripura board to re-attempt the exam or give the Supplementary Exam and get passed in the particular subject, improving their scores in the 12th or HS certificate.

TBSE HS Supplementary Exam Date Sheet 2019 has been released on 6th July 2019.

State Board Supplementary Exams

TBSE HS Supplementary Exam Notification 2019 has been declared by Tripura Board of Secondary Education in the official website. Students who have to appear for the same exam can check out the entire details related to it here. Students can download the TBSE HS supplementary exam Time table or routine 2019 from the official website of the board- www.tbse.in.

Tripura Board HS exam was conducted from 1st March to 3rd April 2019 and results were declared on 21st May 2019 for Science students and 6th June 2019 for Arts and Commerce students. Therefore, if any student was not able to qualify in any subject exam can apply for the class 12th Supplementary Examinations. The Supplementary Exams for all the subjects will take place in the month of June 2019, subject wise. Students who have failed in a particular subject have to mention the subject name while applying for the Supplementary Exam. TBSE HS Supplementary Exam 2019 Overview

After the TBSE HS Supplementary Exam notifications have been declared, the Board is providing students to appear in Supplementary Exams, who have failed in a subject and passed in other. This will be an opportunity for students to pass the exam with all subjects and after this, there will be no more chance given to them. Let us get an overview of this exam;

How To Apply for Tripura Class 12th Supplementary Exam?

Students who have passed in all subject in TBSE HS Board Exam 2019 but failed in one, have the opportunity to give the supplementary exam and get passed with all subjects. The application forms are available now. Follow the below steps to apply for the supplementary exam for class 12th;

  • Go the official website of Tripura Board.
  • Click on the Download Form link, available on the home page.
  • Here you will find the link for the Application form for appearing in the supplementary examination of HS/Class 12th exam, 2019.
  • Click on the same link and you will be redirected to a new webpage.
  • A pdf format of application form will appear on the screen.
  • Download the application form and fill it under the guidance of your school authorities.
  • Students have to mention the subject name, school name, registration number, roll number and marks obtained.
  • After filling the form, they have to enclose it with a photocopy of admit card and mark sheet.
  • The application fee for the supplementary exam is Rs.120/-, which is payable at respective schools by withdrawing a challan from the bank and challan number should be mentioned in the form.

TBSE HS Supplementary Exam Time Table

To download the routine for HSE Supplementary Exam 2019 conducted by Tripura Board, follow the below steps;

  • Go to the official website for the Improvement time table- www.tbse.in.
  • Students have to click on the TBSE HS Improvement Time Table link.
  • TBSE HS Improvement Time Table 2019 will appear on the new window.
  • Download the Time Table and keep it for future use.

TBSE HS Supplementary Exam Admit Card

  • The admit card for HS Supplementary Exam 2019, conducted by Tripura Board of Secondary Education, will be available on July 2019.
  • The exams are going to be conducted in the month of July 2019.
  • The admit card will be provided by the respective schools to the students who have successfully submitted the application form.
  • The admit card will be given with authorized signature and stamp.
  • Students should check the details present in the admit card and if found any discrepancies, report to the school authority.
  • Students are required to carry the admit card in the exam hall, failing which they shall not be allowed to attend the exam.

TBSE HS Supplementary Exam Results

The results for HSE Supplementary Exam 2019, conducted by Tripura Board, will be declared in July-August 2019, after the conduct of examinations. The exams are going to be conducted on July 2019. The results for all the students will be released online on the official website, www.tripuraresults.nic.in.The level of the exam will be the State Board level.

Students who want to check the results for Tripura HS Supplementary Exam, follow the given steps;

  • Go to the Tripura Board official result website
  • Click on the Supplementary Exam HSE Result 2019 link.
  • Students are required to enter roll number mentioned in admit card and click on Submit.
  • TBSE HS Supplementary Exam 2019 Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of it for future reference.

The Board commenced its functioning from 1st January 1976.
