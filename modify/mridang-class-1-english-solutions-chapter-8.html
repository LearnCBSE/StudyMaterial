The Four Seasons Question Answer Class 1

The Four Seasons Questions and Answers

Let us speak

1. (a) What do you wear in summer?
Answer:
I wear light colour and cotton clothes in the summer season.

(b) What do you wear in winter?
Answer:
I wear warm woollen clothes in the winter season.

(c) Which season do you like most? Why?
Answer:
I like winter season because I can wear different kinds of woollen clothes.

2. Look at the pictures and tell the story in your own words.

Answer:
(1) It was raining, and a girl was playing in a puddle with an umbrella in her hand. (2)  Suddenly, a crow swooped down and snatched the umbrella from her. (3) The crow then hung the umbrella upside down on a tree branch. (4) After that the crow placed its babies inside it.

Mridang Class 1 English Chapter 8 The Four Seasons Summary

Mridang Class 1 Chapter 8 The Four Seasons Summary in English

During spring, children enjoy playing and flowers bloom everyday. During the hot summer, everyone desires to swim in the pool to stay cool.

Monsoon clouds bring rain, which offers a refreshing coolness. Winter is chilly, but on sunny days, people relish the warmth of the sun.

Mridang Class 1 Chapter 8 The Four Seasons Summary in Hindi

वसंत के समय, बच्चे खेलते हैं और फूल प्रतिदिन खिलते हैं। गर्मी के मौसम में सभी ठंडा महसूस करने के लिए पूल में तैरना चाहते हैं।

मानसून के बादल वर्षा लाते हैं, जो ठंडक प्रदान करती है। सर्दियों में ठंड होती है, लेकिन धूप वाले दिनों में, लोग सूर्य की किरणों का आनंद लेते हैं।
