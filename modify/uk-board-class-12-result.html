How to Check UK Board Class 12th Result 2024?

1st Step : Visit the UBSE official website – ubse.uk.gov.in.

2nd Step : Click on “Results/Admit Card” from the left-hand side of the page.

3rd Step : A new page opens. Now click on the link provided to check your UK Board Intermediate result 2024.

4th Step : Enter your Roll Number as mentioned on the UK Board Class 12 Admit Card.

5th Step : The UK Board Class 12 Result will appear on the screen. Download the result and take printouts for future reference

The students who are not satisfied with their marks in the UK Board result 2024 will also have an option to apply for re-evaluation and re-checking their answer sheets. The students can take benefit of this particular provision by paying a nominal fee as directed by the concerned Board and submitting an application through their respective schools/institutions.

UK Board Result 2024 – Supplementary/Improvement Examination

Students who fail to make to obtain passing marks in the UK Board result 2024 can appear for UK Board Supplementary/Improvement Exam. The supplementary UK board exam 2024 will give the candidates a choice to improve their score. Students can apply for appearing in the supplementary/improvement exam of UK board by filling in the application form that will be made available on the official website of UBSE 2024. The UK Board supplementary examination 2024 will be held in the month of June.

About Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE)

The Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE) was established in the year 2001. It was formed to regulate the secondary level education in the state. Before the formulation of the Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE), the board level examinations were conducted under Uttaranchal Shiksha Evam Pariksha Parishad which was later renamed Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE) in the year 2008. The board has made tremendous growth in the very short span of time and currently, has more than 10,000 affiliated schools and every year 3 lakh plus students participate in the UK Board 10th Examinations and UK Board 12th Examinations.
