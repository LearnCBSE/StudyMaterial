August 21, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam 2019: TBSE Madhyamik supplementary exam 2019 application forms have been released by the Tripura board. The application forms were available for everyone to download from June 11, 2019. Thus, candidates that are willing to apply for the supplementary exam can fill the application forms from the main website of Tripura board. The forms are available on the official Tripura board website which is tbse.in. After the application form, the Tripura Board has also released the TBSE Madhyamik supplementary exam routine. In this, all the dates and other information related to the TBSE Madhyamik supplementary exam has been given.

TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam 2019

Once the result for the main exam is released, the board has started the process for the supplementary exam. In order to appear in this exam, students need to fill the application form for the supplementary exam. Along with the form, there is a payment fee of Rs. 120 for the eligible students. The application form needs to be filled up as soon as possible as the school authorities need to submit this form to the board officials which is due on 18th June 2019. How To Apply for TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam 2019?

Supplementary exams give students a second chance to the candidates so that they can clear the main exam once again. As the Tripura board has released the application form, the candidates should follow the below steps to download and thereby fill up the application form

  • Step – 1: Go to the official website of Tripura board which is tbse.in.
  • Step – 2: For an application form, there is a link on the website and when you will click on it, it will redirect you to a new website.
  • Step – 3: On this new tab, you are required to fill the application form.
  • Step – 4: Once the form is filled, the candidate needs to submit this form to their respective school authorities along with the application fee.

TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam Admit Card

TBSE Madhyamik supplementary exam admits card will be available for everyone to download as soon as the board issues it. It is important to download the admit card and carry it to the supplementary exam. No candidate will be allowed to enter the exam hall without the supplementary exam admit card. There are important details like student’s name, board name, roll number of the student, examination name, timings, etc. on the admit card. It is important to go through these details and make sure there is no in these details. If there is any fault found, bring it to the notice of given authority.

Steps To Download TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam Admit Card 2019

  • Step 1: Visit the official website of TBSE board as given above in the article.
  • Step 2: On the homepage, there will be a link which states the ‘latest updates’ section.
  • Step 3: When you click on this link, the admit card will appear in a new tab. Here, candidates need to mention the required details and then click on the submit button.
  • Step 4: TBSE Madhyamik supplementary exam admit card will be visible on the screen for everyone to download it.

TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam Result 2019

The supplementary exam provides those candidates a second chance that failed to clear the main exam in the first attempt. Last year, the results for the TBSE Madhyamik supplementary exam were released on 11th August. This year also, it is expected that the same schedule will be followed by the Tripura board.

How To Check Your TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam Result 2019?

Once the supplementary exams will be over, candidates will be eagerly waiting for their results. The supplementary exam results are released by the Tripura board on their official website. Candidates that want to check their results can follow the below steps.

  • Step 1: Go to the official website of Tripura board that is mentioned above in the article.
  • Step 2: There will be a link on the official website that states the TBSE Madhyamik supplementary exam result.
  • Step 3: When you will click on this link, it will redirect you to a new tab.
  • Step 4: Once the new tab opens, you need to fill in the details like your roll number in the space provided.
  • Step 5: After submitting the details, click on ‘Show result’ button.
  • Step 6: Your result will be visible on the screen, and you can download it for any further processes later on.

What After TBSE Madhyamik Supplementary Exam Result 2019?

Once the results are out, candidates check the result on the official website. The candidates that are qualified can move on to the next level of education which is class 11. In order to appear for class 11, candidates need to choose their preferred stream. There are three streams out of which candidates can choose from, which are arts, commerce, and science.

About Tripura Board

Tripura board for secondary education was established in the year 1976 and it is popularly known as TBSE. The board was formed under the Tripura act 12. It is a board for school education in the Tripura state. Furthermore, the board is responsible for all the development and promotional activities of secondary education among the students of various schools in the state.
