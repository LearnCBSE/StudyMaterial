CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship 2023-2024 | Application, Eligibility, Rewards

November 4, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship 2023-2024: CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship offered by the Directorate of Minority Welfare (DMW), Government of Kerala. DMW published the online applications for the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship 2023-24. This Scholarship offered to Girls belonging to the Latin, Muslim or Converted Christians Community for higher education. So that they can pursue higher education without financial hassle. These students should be studying in the first year of the UG courses in any Govt/Aided Institutions.

DMW organized this scholarship to award scholarship amounts to deserving Muslim girls every year. This scheme offers scholarships as well as hostel stipends. So, girl candidates can apply for this scholarship on or before March 2023. Candidates can apply online for this scholarship on the official website of DMW, Govt of Kerala. CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship

CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship can be applied through online mode only. This scholarship offered to only the Muslim girls who are domiciled in Kerala State. This scholarship helps these community students of Kerala to complete their higher education.

This Scholarship is named in honor of the former Chief Minister C.H. Mohammed Koya of Kerala. Muslim Girls who were not able to apply in the first year can apply in the succeeding years. However, they will be considered only for the year in which they applied for the scholarship/ stipend. So, eligible girl students can apply online for the Fresh and Renewal Scholarship Applications 2023-24. The applications for this scholarship will be available only on the official website of DMW, Govt of Kerala.

CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfill the following eligibility conditions:

  • The student must belong to the Muslim, Latin or Converted Christian Community.
  • Students must be a domicile of Kerala State.
  • Students must be a Girl student studying for graduation or higher courses in Govt/Aided institutions.
  • Students who have given the Common Entrance Examinations and got admission to self-financing colleges from the merit seat are also eligible.
  • Students must have secured at least 50% marks in the qualifying examinations.
  • The student’s family income should not be more than 8 Lakhs per annum.
  • Students who apply for hostel stipend must be staying in recognized hostels.
  • Students who are eligible for the hostel stipend will not be eligible for the scholarship.
  • Students must have a bank account in any recognized bank to receive the scholarship amount.

CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship Selection Process

The selection of students for this scholarship is based on the recommendations of the Sachar Committee Report. The scholarship applications will be verified by the institutions. After this, it will be sent to the project officer of the committee to make the final decision. The project officer will verify the applications.

Then, sanction the scholarship amount for the selected candidates. The students should have a bank account in any nationalized bank to receive the scholarship amount. Then, the scholarship amount will be disbursed to the selected student’s bank account through the DBT process.

CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship Application Process

Refer to the below points to apply for the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship.

  • Go to the official website of DMW, Govt of Kerala .
  • Click on the ‘Types of Scholarship’ to appear on the homepage.

  • Then, read the instructions and click on ‘Apply Online to fill the Student Online Registration Form.
  • After this, enter your matriculation exam details, personal details, permanent address details, etc.
  • Click on ‘Submit’ to complete the Student Online Registration Form.
  • But, if you have already registered, then login with registration Id, date of birth, password and select Fresh/Renewal.
  • Fill the correct and valid information in the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship Application Form.
  • Upload the scanned copy of the supporting documents.
  • Then, submit the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship application along with the supporting documents.
  • Take a printout of the registration form.
  • Submit it along with the supporting documents to the verifying officer of your institution.

Documents Required for CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship

The scanned copy of the documents must be uploaded along with the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship application. Upload the below-listed documents to apply for the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship.

  • Photocopies of SSLC or equivalent examination certificates and mark sheets
  • Parent’s Income certificate
  • Photocopy of the first page of the bank passbook in the name of the student
  • Photocopy of student’s Aadhar card
  • Students caste certificate
  • Domicile certificate, if required

CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship Result

DMW will publish the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship result online on its official website. The result will be released in the form of a merit list in November/December. Refer to the below points to check the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship result.

  • Go to the official website of DMW, Govt of Kerala.
  • Click on the ‘Awarded Students List’  available under the ‘Home’ menu appear on the homepage.
  • After this, select scholarship type, status, institution type, state, district, college/ institution name.
  • Then, the list of recommended students for the award of scholarship 2023-24 will be displayed on your screen.
  • You can check the merit list by searching your Registration Number or Name.

CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship Application Status

DMW will publish the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship Application Statue online on its official website. Students can track their scholarship application status by searching their names. Refer to the below points to check the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship application status.

  • Go to the official website of DMW, Govt of Kerala.
  • Click on the ‘Registered Students Status’ available under the ‘Home’ menu to appear on the homepage.
  • After this, select scholarship type, status, institution type, state, district, etc. to track the status.
  • Then, the list of registered student’s scholarship application status will be displayed on your screen.
  • You can check the status by searching your name on the list.

Question 1.
What is the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship?

Answer:

Question 2.
Can I edit the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship application?

Answer:
Yes, you can edit the application if it is not verified by the Institution. Once the application will be verified by the institution, it will be disabled to restrict modification.

Question 3.
What are the starting and ending date to apply for the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship?

Answer:
The starting date to apply online for the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship is January 2024. Whereas the ending date to apply for the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship is March 2024.

Question 4.
Is the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship available for all State candidates?

Answer:
No, the CH Muhammed Koya Scholarship is available only for the Kerala State Muslim girl candidates.

Question 5.
