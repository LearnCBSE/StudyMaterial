NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List 2019 (Released) | Check TN MBBS Merit List Here

July 6, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List 2019: Directorate of Medical Education (DME) will release NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List 2019 by last week of June 2019. Meanwhile, the health and family welfare, the Tamil Nadu government have released an application app of Tamil Nadu NEET 2019, students can track them an application status for NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List by the official link given below.

The Tamil Nadu MBBS Rank List 2019 for admissions to 85% of seats are for state quota MBBS and BDS seats will be based on NEET Score. 3950 MBBS and 1000 BDS Seats are available in various medical and dental colleges of the state will prepare state rank list admissions by DME. NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List 2019 will be prepared for only those candidates who are a domicile of the state and passed NEET 2019.

Latest Update:

4th July – According to Indian Express, the selection committee of DME will release Tamil Nadu Merit List tentatively in by 6th July or 8th July.

Tamil Nadu MBBS Merit List 2019 will be released only for those students who fill the admission form within the last dates. The NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu rank list 2019 will have state candidates rank, name, category, NEET score, and category rank. Students will be called to participate in counseling for seat allocation as per based on their NEET Merit List. The allotment seats will be done strictly based on candidate’s merit position in the rank list, choice opted and considering other factors like reservation and eligibility.

NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List 2019 for 85% State Quota Seats

Tamil Nadu officials will prepare a separate merit list for state quota, management quota and NRI quota. The rank list will be applicable for filling up only 85% state quota seats while remaining 15% seats under All India Quota seats to be filled by Medical Counselling Committee (MCC).

NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List 2019 – Tie-Breaking Criteria

If two or more students have the same rank then the officials will follow the tie-breaker method to resolve the tie. Tie-breaking rules for Tamil Nadu MBBS Merit List are listed below:

  • Students who have higher marks in Biology will be given the first preference.
  • If the still exists students who have higher marks in Chemistry will be considered.
  • If a tie still exists Candidates with lesser number of NEET Exam incorrect answers will be considered.
  • In case if the tie stills exist, then students with older age will be preferred.

NEET MBBS Tamil Nadu Rank List 2019 – NEET 2019 Counselling

After the NEET Tamil Nadu MBBS List is released, students will be allowed to participate in NEET Counselling 2019 as per their merit position so that they can exercise their option secure MBBS/BDS seats selection. NEET 2019 Counselling students have to fill the choice of colleges and courses desire to study. Based on the colleges and courses chosen by students seats will be alloted. However, availability of seats and ranks of the candidates will also be considered.
