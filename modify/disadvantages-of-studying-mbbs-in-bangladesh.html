Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Bangladesh – Limitations of MBBS Study in Bangladesh

September 8, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Bangladesh: Bangladesh is a nation in South Asia that has a high amount of medical colleges and universities. Most people were interested in studying MBBS abroad like in Bangladesh. However, there are advantages and disadvantages of studying MBBS in Bangladesh for Indian students.

Here we are giving the complete information about the cons of MBBS study in Bangladesh.

Drawbacks of MBBS Study in Bangladesh

Medical education in Bangladesh is a 5-year course that is divided into 4 section examinations with a one-year internship process. The three simple stages of medical education in Bangladesh are the Pre-clinical, para-clinical, and clinical phases.

It is confirmed that studying MBBS from Bangladesh is a good choice for the students who want quality education at affordable costs but we have to accept that there are cons too. These are the disadvantages of studying MBBS in Bangladesh.

  • Language Barrier
  • Private Colleges
  • Overpopulation
  • Terrorism
  • Tuition Fees
  • Lifestyle
  • Infrastructure
  • Study Gap

Private Colleges

In Bangladesh, it is not easy for international students to get admission into public/ govt universities. Only private colleges accept admissions where the tuition fees is very high when compared to other govt colleges. Also, the private colleges offer bad infrastructure and facilities to the students.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure at Bangladesh’s private medical colleges is not so good. It doesn’t reach the expectations of international students who are curious about the campus infrastructure, hostel facilities, hygiene, and other things.

In Bangladesh, the hostel facilities are poor with poor hygiene in the toilets. The colleges take rented buildings for hostels and allot 10 persons in the same room with 1 or 2 toilets.

Lifestyle

When a student is studying in abroad, then they have so many expectations about the country and places to visit. But Bangladesh will not have facilities like parties, pubs, clubs, tourism, concerts, nightlife, and so on. It can be a disappointment to the students.

Language Barrier

Another important rule is that students have to learn the local language to complete their MBBS education smoothly. The universities mention the language as English but most of the staff and students still use the Bangla language to communicate which makes complicated for the Indian students.

Students who live out of campus have to interact with the people in Bangala only. Also, at the time of the internship, students have to use the local language to communicate with the patients.

Tuition Fees

We already know that most international students get admission from private medical colleges only. However, the tuition fee at the private universities is really high when compared with the public colleges.

Those private universities ask the students to pay at least 30% to 40% of the total tuition fees of the MBBS course as the first instalment of the MBBS course. It can be difficult for the middle-class students.

Terrorism

Student safety is the major factor that students and their parents look for. In Bangladesh, there have been many instances of terrorism detected in recent years which makes it an unsafe place to study medical education.

Many other popular crimes in Bangladesh are human trafficking, kidnapping, robbery, money laundering, corruption, political violence, black marketing, and so on.

Study Gap

Nowadays, most students have study gaps for their own reasons. But in Bangladesh, there is a specific limit of 2 years gap. That means more than 2 years of study gap is not accepted to study MBBS course.

Overcrowding

Bangladesh is one of the highest population density countries with most of the citizens being muslims. Few students may feel congested because of the overpopulation. You will find traffic on the road and crowds in the shopping markets.

FAQs on Disadvantages of Studying MBBS in Bangladesh

  1. Is doing MBBS in Bangladesh worth it?

Yes, doing an MBBS in Bangladesh is worth it if you are an Indian student. The colleges provide quality education with enough practical clinical knowledge.

  1. Which is better for MBBS Russia or Bangladesh?

Bangladesh is better for MBBS when compared with Russia. the tuition fees and cost of living in Bangladesh are less than in Russia.

  1. What are the disadvantages of studying MBBS?

The disadvantages of studying MBBS abroad are language barriers, study standards, risk of fraud, and climatic differences.

  1. Why do people choose Bangladesh for MBBS?

Bangladesh is the most popular option for pursuing an MBBS program. It is not only cost-effective but also gives students access to quality healthcare facilities that are available at low cost.
