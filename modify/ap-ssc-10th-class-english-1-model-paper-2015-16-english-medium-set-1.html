Far in the North the white bear snuffles Down in her lair the. gleaming snow She waits for all the life she’s making Outside the crashing glaciers grow.
These cubs could be the last cubs ever To freely live and roam and mate.
Our grandchild knows the white bear never
Or will the dreamer wake ?  (Or Will the Dreamer Wake)

(a)Why does the white bear snuffle ?
(b)What is the meaning of ‘glacier’ ?

12.Read the following passage.
Father had provided for my education, and I had been able to realize his dreams. My parents were truly proud of me. It was through me that they had earned a greater degree of admiration and respect from the villagers. My father would not like to see me carrying a trunk on my back and would be very* hurt if I did so. I concluded that it would be better to let him carry it. Father was used to carrying luggage anyway. He was stronger and more skilled than I in these matters. I had never got used to physical labour having stayed in hostels right from my child­hood. (The Journey)
Now answer the following questions .
(a)Who do you think provides for your education ?
(b)How had the narrator’s parents earned respect, according to the narrator ?
(c)What did the narrator conclude ? Do you think his conclusion was right ? .

ANSWERS

Part – A

1.What made Nick choose Bethany Hamilton as his teacher to learn surfing ?
Ans .Bethany Hamilton was also a disabled person. Though her left arm was bitten off by a shark at the age of 12, she did not lose hope.

2.Why did Mrs. Slater ask Victoria to change her dress ?
Ans .Because her (Victoria’s) Aunt Elizabeth and Uncle Ben are coming to their house.

3.Who carried the writer’s luggage when he came home ?
Ans. A porter carried his luggage from the bus stop, when he came home.

4.How did Roberge know the humane concern in Ray ?
Ans. After knowing his ‘Charulatha’ screenplay was missing, he did not want to hurt the reputation of the person who had stolen it.

5.Who was Bayaji ? Where did he work ?
Ans. Bayaji was (a Dalit) Mahar by caste in Maharastra. He worked in the dockyard in Bombay.

6.According to Maathai, how are the women responsible for the protection of the envi­ronment?
Ans .Women play a crucial role. They work on farms. They plant, cultivate and produce food.

7.What were the daily wages of Kalam ?
Ans. The daily wages of Kalam were an anna.

8.Why was the writer amazed with the boy’s actions ?
Ans. The white boy even at his tender age was imposing his will upon the black boy and the little black boy submitted meekly.

9.What are the three things that the father plans to do on Monday next ? What effect does it have on his daughters ?
Ans .The three things that the father plans to do on Monday next are : (a) to see his lawyer to alter his will (b) to pay his insurance premium and (c) to marry widow Shorrocks at St. Philip’s Church.
If the father does all these three things, the daughters will lose the chance of get­ting their share in their father’s assets.

10.What made ‘Maya Bazaar’ a landmark film ?
Ans .Maya Bazaar became a landmark movie because of the sterling performances of great film stars. Apart from that the director K.V. Reddy had full control over every frame of the film. It is a blend of supreme talent and technical brilliance of the unit.

11.a) Why does the white bear snuffle ?
Ans .Due to the clashing glaciers
b) What is the meaning of ‘glacier’ ?
Ans .A large mass of ice which moves slowly  down a mountain
c)Why does the white bear wait for all the life she’s making ?
Ans .For the rehabilitation of the environment
d)Why could these cubs be the last cubs ever ?
Ans .As their habitat is going to be destroyed.
e)What is the cause of destruction of white bear’s habitat ? Can you guess ?
Ans. Global warming

12.a) Who do you think provides for your education ?
Ans. Father
b)How had the narrator’s parents earned respect, according to the narrator ?
Ans. Through the narrator
c)What did the narrator conclude ? Do you think his conclusion was right ?
Ans .By allowing his father to carry his luggage. No.

13.a) Who directed ‘Maya Bazaar’ ?
Ans. K.V. Reddy
b) In how many languages, was ‘Maya Bazaar’ made ?
Ans .In two languages / In Telugu and Tamil
