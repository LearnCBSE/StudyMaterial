Siemens Scholarship Program 2023 | Dates, Eligibility, Benefits

November 8, 2023 by LearnCBSE Online

Siemens Scholarship 2023: Siemens Scholarship is an initiative of Siemens India in association with CSR. This scholarship is offered to 1st year engineering students of Govt colleges for pursuing their studies. This scholarship encourages and promotes engineering education within meritorious students of lower-income families. This scholarship aims to provide financial support to deserving students who want to become an engineer in the future. They can make a career in engineering, R&D or manufacturing.

So, engineering students can fill and apply for the Siemens Scholarship on the official website of Siemens. The application process will begin in September 2023. So, students can apply for this scholarship from September 2023. The selected students will receive the scholarship throughout their 4 years of graduation. Read the complete article to get all the information about the Siemens Scholarship for Engineering Students, Siemens Scholarship Interview.

Siemens Scholarship Program 2023

Applications for Siemens Scholarship 2023 will be released in online mode only. All eligible engineering students can avail of this scholarship. Siemens has organized this scholarship to support engineering students from underprivileged families for pursuing their education. This scholarship helps students having devotion and perseverance in learning. After graduation, students can get a job at Siemens or leading companies in many sectors. Sectors like banking, IT, engineering, finance, automotive, consumer goods, etc.

Siemens India is a private limited company focused on electrification, digitalization, and automation. It has launched the Siemens Scholarship program based on the German model of dual education to promote youth to work in industries. This scholarship program supports 435 students from 49 Govt engineering colleges across 22 states in India. Siemens will provide financial support to talented engineering students of economically backward sections. Selected students will also get an opportunity for internships and training at Siemens. This scholarship provides 50% of reservation for girl engineering students.

Siemens Scholarship Eligibility Criteria

Students must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to apply for the Siemens Scholarship.

  • Students must be a 1st-year student of Government Engineering colleges from any stream. The following streams are applicable
    • Mechanical
    • Production
    • Electrical
    • Electronics/Instrumentation
    • Electronics & Telecommunication
    • Computers or Information Technology​
  • Student’s age should not be more than 20 years.
  • Students must have secured at least 60% of marks in SSC (PCM).
  • Students must have secured at least 60% of marks in HSC.
  • The student’s family income should not be more than 2 lakhs per annum.

Siemens Scholarship Benefits

The selected students will get the following financial benefits.

  • Tuition fees reimbursement. But, if the student is availing any other scholarship then the balance tuition fee will get reimbursed.
  • Book allowances, stationery, hostel fees, and extra classes will be provided.
  • A holistic development program by training and mentoring at Siemens.

Siemens Scholarship Selection Process

The students will get selected on the basis of merit and academic performance. The applications received from students will be scrutinized by the authorities of Siemens. The final decision will be taken by Siemens to shortlist the students. Then, the selected students will be informed through their registered email. After this, the scholarship will be disbursed to students who passed in all subjects and scored first class.

Siemens Scholarship Apply Online

Refer to the following points to apply online for the Siemens Scholarship.

  • Go to the official website of Siemens Scholarship.

  • Fill the correct information such as personal, educational, and engineering details.
  • Select whether you are availing any other scholarship in present or not.
  • Select whether you are availing hostel services or not.
  • Accept the terms and conditions in the application form.
  • Then, submit the Siemens Scholarship application form.

Siemens Scholarship Renewal

This scholarship is awarded to the selected students throughout their 4 years of study. The scholarship will be renewed only if the student qualifies all subjects. Also, scores 1st class in one attempt.
