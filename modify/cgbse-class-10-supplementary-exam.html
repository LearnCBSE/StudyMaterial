CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam 2019 | Check Chhattisgarh Board 10th Supplementary Result 2019 Here

August 12, 2019 by LearnCBSE Online

CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam 2019: Chhattisgarh board of secondary education which is also known as CGBSE has released the notification for class 10 supplementary exam. Thus, all the students who are looking for appearing into CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam can now check the schedule. CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam Admit Card has been released on 27th June 2019.

Along with the time table, we are also going to help you with the admit card, application form, and result. All the CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam related information is available on the official website which is cgbse.nic.in. However, it is important to fill the application form first before appearing for the supplementary exam. Below is the whole content related to the fee structure, examination date, and other information for the CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam.

CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam Application Form

Before appearing for the class 10 supplementary exam, it is important to fill the application form. To fill the form, students need to login first by selecting the class first as well as providing their information like roll number in the space given. So, all the students that are willing to apply for the application form need to fill it online. The last date to fill up the form without any late is on June 10, 2019. While the application form with the late fee can be filled from June 11 to June 18.

How To Download CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam Application Form?

  • Step 1: Go to the official webpage of Chattisgarh board which is cgbse.nic.in.
  • Step 2: On the homepage, there will be a link which states CGBSE 10th supplementary registration form, click on this link.
  • Step 3: Once the link opens, candidates are required to provide their exam roll number to choose the subjects that are to be taken for the supplementary exam.
  • Step 4: Download the CGBSE class 10 application form and fill it under the guidelines of school authorities.
  • Step 5: Once the form is filled, the payment needs to do, because only after this the form will be submitted.

CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam Admit Card

The tentative dates for the release of admit card are expected to be in June 2019. CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam admit card will be provided to the students who have finished their registration process successfully. Without the supplementary exam admit card, no student will be allowed for the exam. Also, the official admit cards can be downloaded by the students from the main website.

How To Download CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam Admit Card?

  • Step 1: Go to the official webpage of Chattisgarh board which is cgbse.nic.in.
  • Step 2: On the homepage, there will be a link which states CGBSE 10th supplementary admit card, click on this link.
  • Step 3: Once the link opens, candidates are required to provide their exam roll number to choose the subjects that are to be taken for the supplementary exam.
  • Step 4: Download the CGBSE class 10 admit card that is displayed on the screen.

CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam Result

Chattisgarh board as decided that the results for the supplementary exam will be released around August 2019. The results for everyone will be released on the Chattisgarh board’s official website.

How To Check CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam Result?

  • Step 1: Open the official website of Chhattisgarh board from your desktop.
  • Step 2: Under the student’s corner, there will be a link for exam results.
  • Step 3: On the homepage, select the High school examination result 2019 link.
  • Step 4: Under the high school exam menu, click on the supplementary examination option
  • Step 5: Here, candidates are required to enter their roll number and captcha in the space provided.
  • Step 6: After filling all the details completely, click on the submit button.
  • Step 7: CGBSE Class 10 Supplementary Exam results will be visible on the screen and students can download it in the form of PDF for any future purpose.

Important Instructions

  1. The exam timings for every student are from 9 am to 12:30 pm.
  2. It is advisable for students to go through the examination time table carefully.
  3. The answer sheets to every student will be provided by 9:05 am and the question paper will be distributed by 9:25 am.
  4. Under no circumstances, there will be changes made in the examination dates.
  5. Students are advised to go through the time table very carefully.
  6. The practical examination for the candidate will be taken at the respective examination center.
